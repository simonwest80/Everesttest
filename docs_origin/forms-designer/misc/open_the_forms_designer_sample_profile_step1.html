Open the Forms Designer

To open the Forms Designer from the Item profile, do the following:

  • Open the Items browser.
  • Double-click an item to select it and open the Item profile.
  • From the Options menu, select Design.
  • The FormsDesigner appears with the target form in Design view. The word “Design” is located next to Item in the title bar. The Field Chooser, Component Palette, and Object Inspector are displayed near the target form. The target form displays the standard profile template.

You decide to close and reopen the Field Chooser, Object Inspector, and Component Palette.
