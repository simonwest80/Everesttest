Purchasing Reports

The Purchasing cycle involves decisions and processes required for procurement of products and services for operating a business. Some of the important purchasing reports are as follows:

  • Accounts Payable Aging
  • Item Purchase History
  • Purchase Doc Tracking (Group Report)
  • Purchase Doc Tracking (Selection)
  • Purchase Order
  • Purchase Invoice
  • Vendor Statements
  • Vendor Tracking (Group)
  • Vendor Tracking (Selection)
  • Vendor Transactions
