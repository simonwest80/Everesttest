Debit Memos

The Debit Memo is the third and final step in the purchase return process. It is created when inventoried items are actually returned to the vendor or an allowance is given by the vendor for unsatisfactory services.

The debit memo in Everest thus serves a dual purpose:

  • Records return of items - The debit memo acts as a Goods Dispatched Note as items are taken out of inventory only after their return is recorded on a debit memo.
  • Accounts for purchase returns - The debit memo is the base document to account for the amount of purchase returns.

The debit memo is a final document. It is on the basis of the debit memo that inventory values, vendor balances, stocks and accounts payable are updated. Accounting entries are automatically generated when a debit memo is created.
