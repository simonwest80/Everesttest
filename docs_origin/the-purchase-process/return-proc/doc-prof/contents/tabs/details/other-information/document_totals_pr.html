Document Totals

The following totals are summed, automatically updated and displayed in all purchase return documents:

Field Description
Taxable Displays the total value of items that are subject to tax. This amount excludes discounts allowed, if any.


The line items that are subject to tax have a Tax Code associated in the Item Details Grid.


Tax Code


  Tax inclusive items are included in this total if they have a purchase tax associated in the item details grid but the tax is stripped out.


Manual Debit Memos


This field displays the tax-exclusive total amount of all the “Taxable Purchase” entry types.
Tax The Tax field displays the total purchase tax on items (the sum of all the values in the Tax Amount column of the item details grid). This is a display field and cannot be edited.


Manual Debit Memo


This field displays the total purchase tax of all recoverable and non-recoverable entries automatically generated by the system. The Tax Breakdown is not available on manual documents.
Exempt The Exempt field displays the total amount (after reducing the discount, if any) for the line items that have a non-taxable tax code or that do not have a tax code associated with them. This is a display field and cannot be edited.


Manual Debit Memo


This field displays the total amount of all the “Exempt Purchase” and “None” entry types.
Payment discount This field is disabled for purchase return documents.
Total The Total field displays the sum total of the Taxable, Tax and Exempt amounts. It is a display field and cannot be edited.


Manual Debit Memo


This total matches the amount specified in the Accounts Payable entry.
Paid Displays the total amount paid on the document. Click on the button beside this field to view the Payment History for payments made against the document.
Balance This field displays the balance amount (total amount - paid amount) to be paid on the document by the vendor for refunds.
