Accounting Entries - Edit a Purchase Invoice

The accounting processes that occur when you edit a purchase invoice depend on the purchase invoice’s FOB Type. In addition, the process differs for different scenarios.

  • When the system debits or credits the Accrued Purchases account with the balance of the Accrued Purchase account for a multicurrency transaction, if the variance in base or multicurrency is zero the system debits/credits the variance to the gain or loss accounts defined in the accounting defaults.
  • Changes to the item cost occur when you edit the basic item cost, the discount applied, the taxes, or any add-on costs.

Inventoried Items

  • Entries for Increasing Item Cost of Inventoried Items
  • Entries for Decreasing Item Cost of Inventoried Items

Non-inventoried Items

  • Entries for Changing Item Cost of Invoiced Non-inventoried Items
  • Entries for Changing Item Cost of Received Non-inventoried Items
