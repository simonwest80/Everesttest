Fill Sales Order

The Fill SalesOrder option (path: PurchaseInvoice profile > Options > Fill SalesOrder) gives you information on the ‘Open’ sales orders that can be fulfilled by the current invoice.

This option is enabled only if the purchase invoice is saved.

To view the quantity that can be fulfilled by the current purchase invoice, do the following:

  • From the Purchase Invoice profile, select Options > Fill SalesOrder. You will see the Parts Fulfillment dialog box.
  • Select the required options and click OK. You will see the Fill Sales Order Browser.
