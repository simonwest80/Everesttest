Status Bar Information - Purchase Documents

The Status Bar is present at the bottom of all document profiles in Everest. The options available in the status bar of purchase document profiles are:

  • Preview On/Off
  • Show/Hide
  • Zoom Out/In
  • Batched Payments Pending
  • Document Policy
  • Unposted/Posted
  • Item Notes

The Stock Area field displays in the status bar when you need to select a stock area. Select a stock area and press the Enter key or ESC key to exit this field and continue.
