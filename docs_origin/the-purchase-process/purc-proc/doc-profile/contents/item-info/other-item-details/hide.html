Hide

The Hide column (check box) allows you to hide lines in the Item Details Grid.

You cannot hide a line item that has a cost.

To hide a line in the Item Details Grid, do the following:

  • From the purchase document profile, highlight the item.
  • Scroll to the Hide column.
  • Check the box.
  • Click from the purchase document status bar. This is a toggle button. The selected line is not displayed in the Item Details Grid.
