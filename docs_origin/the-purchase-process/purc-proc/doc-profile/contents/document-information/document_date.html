Document Date

The date displayed in a purchase document is the date of creation of the document. The document date:

  • Is by default the login date. You cannot change the date once the document is saved. To create documents with a different date, you must log in with a different date. You can, however, edit the Document Date field before saving when creating a new Purchase Invoice.
  • Is used to compute the due date for the document if the payment terms are of the On Account.

Processed Documents

If a quote is converted to an order, or an order is converted to a receipt or invoice, it displays the login date of the conversion.

Document Date - Purchase Invoice/Debit Memo

The document date on a purchase invoice/debit memo determines the accounting period in which the accounting entries for purchases/returns must be created.

Document Date – Purchase Invoice

With the Purchase Invoice Date: Modify security right, you can edit the new Purchase Invoice date before saving. The Document Date cannot be less than any Purchase Orders or Purchase Receipts linked to the Purchase Invoice and it must fall into a valid (Open) accounting period.
