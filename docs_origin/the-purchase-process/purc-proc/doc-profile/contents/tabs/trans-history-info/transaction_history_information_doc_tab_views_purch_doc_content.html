Transaction History Information

The History tab in a purchase document records the entire history of a transaction from its inception (purchase quote) to the payment on the final document.

This tab is displayed only when you save the document.

The History tab displays the following information:

  • Details of purchase quote creation such as date and time of creation, amount of document and the user who created the quote.
  • Details of purchase order creation.
  • Details of purchase receipt creation.
  • Details of purchase invoice creation.
  • Details of payments or deposits made and refunded on the document such as the payment type and reference number. Details of payments that are applied to the document are also displayed. Credit card numbers are masked using the format XXXXXXXXXXXX when the **Enable Extended Mode** check box is cleared (path: **File > Setup > Preferences > Workstation Settings > Company Settings** tab).
  • Details of modifications made to documents such as changing the status of the purchase order, changes in the vendor contact and shipping addresses, change in the document alias, pay terms, delivery method, location/sub-location and jurisdiction.
  • Details of the modification made to the purchase receipt or invoice columns such as Item Cost, Discount Value, Discount %, Tax Code, Job Code, and Add-on Cost Value.
  • Displays ‘Purchase receipt/invoice cost modified’ event with Cost Adjustment Document’s number and General Journal # in the Reference column.

To maintain transaction history for documents, you must select the option Maintain Transaction Historyfor Documents (path: File > Setup > Preferences > FlowControl > Documents > Miscellaneous > Others > Others1 tab).

You can also set an option to Copy the transaction history attached to a document to its corresponding intermediary or final document check box (path: File > Setup > Preferences >Flow Control > Documents > Miscellaneous > Others > Others 1 tab). If you select this option, the transaction history of a quote, order, or receipt  is copied to the corresponding documents when it is converted to a order, receipt, or invoice.

Documents Dialog Box

To open a document displayed in the browser, select the OpenDocument option from the right-click menu. The Transaction History browser has standard options common to all browsers.
