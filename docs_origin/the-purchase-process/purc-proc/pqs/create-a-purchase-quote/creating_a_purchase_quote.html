Create a Purchase Quote

To create a purchase quote from the purchase quotes browser, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Purchasing > Purchase Quote. You will see the Purchase Quotes browser.
  • From the Purchase Quotes browser, select Options > New > In New Window. You will see the Purchase Quote profile.

You can also create a purchase quote from an existing Purchase Quote profile by selecting Options > New.
