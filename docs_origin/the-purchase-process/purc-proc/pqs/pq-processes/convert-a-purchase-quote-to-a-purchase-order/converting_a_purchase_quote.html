Convert a Purchase Quote to a Purchase Order

To convert a purchase quote into a purchase order, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Purchasing > Purchase Quote. You will see the Purchase Quotes browser.
  • Highlight the document to be converted and select Options > Edit > In New Window. You will see the Purchase Quote profile.
  • From the Purchase Quote profile, select Options > Process > Reserve. The purchase quote is converted to a purchase order.

All details of the quote are displayed in the new Purchase Order profile. By default, the Ordered column in the purchase order displays the quantity quoted. You can make changes to any information (including vendor information) in the purchase order and save it.
