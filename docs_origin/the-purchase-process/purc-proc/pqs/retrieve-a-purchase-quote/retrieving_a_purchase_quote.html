Retrieve a Purchase Quote

Once a purchase quote has been converted to a purchase order, it can no longer be viewed in the PurchaseQuotes browser.

You can however view it by retrieving it. To retrieve a purchase quote, you must cancel the purchase order. The purchase order then reverts to the purchase quote and can be viewed from the PurchaseQuotes browser.

To retrieve a purchase quote, do the following:

  • From the Purchase Order profile; select Options > Process > Cancel Order.
