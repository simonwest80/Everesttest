Tax Breakdown

The Tax Breakdown option (path: Purchase Document profile > Options > Payments > TaxBreakdown) allows you to view:

  • Item-wise breakup of taxes.
  • Tax-wise breakup of taxes.

To view the tax breakdown window, do the following:

  • From the Purchase document profile select, Options > Payments > TaxBreakdown. You will see the Tax Breakdown dialog box.
