Cancel a Purchase Order

If you do not wish to continue the purchase process with your vendor, then you can cancel the purchase order (path: PurchaseOrder profile > Options > Process > Cancel).

On doing so, the system converts it to a purchase quote which is available as a normal system document.

The following purchase orders cannot be canceled:

  • A purchase order that is a ‘Back Order’.
  • A purchase order on which a deposit has been made.
