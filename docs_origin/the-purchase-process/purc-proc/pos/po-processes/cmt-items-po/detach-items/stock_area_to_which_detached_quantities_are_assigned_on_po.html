Stock Area to Which Detached Quantities are Assigned

The stock area to which detached quantities for purchase orders are assigned is determined by the following rules:

  • The items are assigned to the stock area defined for detached quantities in the Location profile (path: File > Setup > Accounting > Location/Sub-Location> Options > Edit > In New Window > StockTransfer > Purchase Document tab > Select stock area to transfer inventoried items into when un-assigning section > Purchase Order) of the logged in location/ sub-location.
  • If the stock area is not defined in the login location, the items remain in the stock area from which the items were attached/assigned.

The Stock Area field displays in the status bar when you need to select a stock area. Select a stock area and press the Enter key or ESC key to exit this field and continue.
