View Serial Numbers

To view serial numbers committed to a serialized/auto-serialized item, do the following:

  • From the Purchase Order profile, select the line item.
  • Select Options > Item > Show Serial #. You will see the Serial # browser.

To view serial numbers committed to all items in the document, do the following:

  • From the Purchase Order profile, select Options > Show Serial #. You will see the Serial # for All Items browser.
