Back End Processes - Mark a Purchase Order Complete

  • When you mark an order as complete, its status changes to ‘Closed’. Closed orders are displayed in the Received Orders browser (path: Purchase Order profile > Options > Received PurchaseOrders toggle option).
  • The ordered quantity is reduced to either the received or invoiced quantity, whichever is less.
  • The committed quantity, if any, is detached from the order.
  • The “On Order” and “On Order Committed” quantities in the Item Stock Status dialog box correspondingly reduce.
