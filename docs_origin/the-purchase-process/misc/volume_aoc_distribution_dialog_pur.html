Volume

The Volume column displays the total volume of the items. The total volume is computed using the quantity of the line items entered in the purchase/purchase return document and the volume per unit of measure defined in the Item profile (path:Inventory > Items > Options > Edit > In New Window >  Purchases tab - Freight column). It is converted to the volume measure for the logged in country and displayed. Purchase orders and debit orders consider the quantity in the document’s Ordered column; purchase receipts and debit memos consider the quantity in the document’s Received column. You cannot edit this column.

On a purchase invoice, the volume is shown under both the Received on Invoice and Invoiced headings.
