Payment Amount Details

Document Amount
The document amount is the total value of the document.
Already paid
This is the amount of the document that has been received. The amount paid includes both posted payments and unposted payments.
Amount
This is the amount of refund you are receiving from the vendor. You cannot get a refund that is greater than the value of the debit memo.
Advance applied
The advance applied displays the amount of the debit memo that has been applied to purchase orders and purchase invoices of the vendor.
Discount available, Discounts applied
This field is not enabled for collection on a debit memo.
Total paid
The total amount paid on the document is the sum of the amount already paid and the amount currently paid. This amount cannot be greater than the document value. You cannot edit this field.
Balance
The balance remaining to be received on the debit memo is displayed.
