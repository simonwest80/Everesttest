Calculate Due Date from Purchase Invoice Date

The vendor sends a single invoice (for all purchase orders placed) after the first shipment. If the order is fulfilled in parts, purchase receipts and/or invoices are created as and when the items are received.

If the payment terms are from the Date of the vendor’s invoice, payments on all shipments become due on the same date regardles of the date when the shipment was received.

The option Terms from purchase invoice date has been provided to compute due dates for each purchase invoice from the date of the invoice, if the vendor’s payment terms take effect from the date of receipt of the items.

This option is checked by default if the option Due date based on purchase invoice date has been selected in the Vendor profile (path: Purchasing > Vendors > Options > Edit > In New Window > Accounting tab).
