Payment Dialog Box - Prior Payments

This tab is displayed when you refund the payment received from a foreign currency vendor on a debit order . If you receive advance payments from the vendor (over a period of time) at different exchange rates, this tab allows you to indicate the refund that must be applied as a payment.

This tab comprises two sections:

  • Advance Document Available - Displays all available deposits (i.e., deposits not applied on a debit memo) on the current debit order.
  • Advance Document Applied - All advances that you apply to the current document are displayed in this column. Use the > and » buttons to move the available deposits from the Advance Documents Available column to this column. You can change the amount being applied by editing the Amount column.

The amount applied cannot be greater than the amount available. The total amount applied is displayed in the Amount field (path: Payment dialog box > General tab).

When you refund a payment, a transaction is automatically created in a payment journal (for the amount applied).
