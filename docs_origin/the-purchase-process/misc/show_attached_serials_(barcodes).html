Show Attached Serials (Barcodes)

Select this option to view details of the serial numbers attached to serialized items on a sales or purchase return document. These details are displayed in the Serial Numbers browser. You can view the item code, the manufacturer’s serial number, the vendor and document number in which the serial number was generated, the cost, the lot number, the stock area to which the item is transferred, and the stock area type.

You can print serial numbers as bar codes from this browser by selecting **Label > Label Print > Serial #
