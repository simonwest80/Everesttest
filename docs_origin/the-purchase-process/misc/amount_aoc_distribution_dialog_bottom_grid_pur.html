Amount

This column displays the monetary value of the items (excluding line item discounts) calculated by multiplying the quantity with the Cost after Discount. The value here is the same as the value displayed in the Amount column in the purchase document’s items grid before you enter the add-on costs.

Purchase orders and debit orders consider the quantity in the document’s Ordered column; purchase receipts and debit memos consider the quantity in the document’s Received column.

On a purchase invoice, the amount is shown under both the Received on Invoice and Invoiced headings.

You cannot edit this column.
