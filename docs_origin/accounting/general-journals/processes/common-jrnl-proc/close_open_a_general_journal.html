Lock/Open a General Journal

To lock or open a general journal, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Accounting > General Journal. You will see the Unposted General Journals browser.
  • From the Unposted General Journals browser, highlight the required journal.
  • Select Options > Lock Journal(s) to lock a journal and Options > Open journal(s) to change the status of a journal to open.

The status of a journal is displayed in the Status column of the Unposted General Journals browser.
