Lock/Open a Payment Journal

To lock or open a payment journal, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Accounting > Account Payable > Payment Journal. You will see the Unposted Payment Journals browser.
  • From the Unposted Payment Journals browser, highlight the required journal.
  • Select Options > Lock Journal(s), ** to lock a journal and **Options > Open journal(s) to change the status of a journal to open.

The status of a journal is displayed in the Status column of the Unposted Payment Journals browser.
