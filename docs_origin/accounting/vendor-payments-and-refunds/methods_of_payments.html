Methods of Payment

You can use any of these methods to make vendor payments and accept refunds:

  1. Payments/refunds from documents – You can make a payment or accept a refund against a document from within the purchase order, purchase invoice or debit memo.
    • Payments/refunds in a payment journal – You can record payments and refunds in a payment journal.
    • Multiple payments wizard – The multiple payments wizard allows you to make multiple payments and refunds without having to make individual payments in the payment journal or from within documents.
    • Payments for payroll transactions from the US Payroll – You can make payments to your employees using the Everest US Payroll module.
