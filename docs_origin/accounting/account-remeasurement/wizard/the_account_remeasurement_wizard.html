The Account Remeasurement Wizard

The Account Remeasurement Wizard comprises the following sections:

  • Welcome
  • Account Type
  • Exchange Rates
  • Finish

 You can navigate within the wizard by clicking the Next or Back buttons or by selecting the sections in the left panel.

 It is recommended that you follow the steps in the wizard sequentially. This ensures that all the required data for remeasuring accounts have been specified.
