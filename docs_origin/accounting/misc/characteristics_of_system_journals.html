Characteristics of System Journals

  • Entries cannot be added manually to the journal.
  • Entries cannot be modified from the journal; you should modify the base document to change the entry.
  • System journals cannot be purged.

Everest also creates automatic entries in Payment and Receipt journals when recording payments against a purchase order, purchase invoice, debit memo, sales order, sales invoice and credit memo. But such payment entries are not made in system journals. These entries can be modified from within the journal.
