The Posted General Journals Browser

When the Posted Journals option is selected from the Unposted General Journals browser, the Posted General Journals browser is displayed. This browser displays all the general journals that are posted to date.

The Transactions option in the Posted General Journals browser (path: Posted General Journals browser > Options > Transactions) allow you to view details of the accounts debited or credited in the journal.

The options common to all browsers in Everest are also available in this browser.
