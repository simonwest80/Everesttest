The Payment Journal Summary Browser

The Payment Journal Summary browser is displayed when the General Ledger (Summary) (path: Unposted Payment Journals browser > Options > Transactions > General Ledger (Summary)) option is selected. This browser lists all the accounts debited or credited in the journal, and the net amount of debit and credit for the account.

The options common to all browsers in Everest are also available in this browser.
