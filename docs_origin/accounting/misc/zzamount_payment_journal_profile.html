This section displays the amount (in figures) that is being paid to or refunded by the vendor. When the document is selected in the Document column of the Transaction Entries section, this field is automatically updated. When the entry type is ‘refund on a purchase receipt’, ‘refund deposit’ or ‘apply/refund debit memo’, this field displays a negative amount.
