General Ledger (Summary)

Select this option (path: Unposted Payment Journals browser > Options > Transactions > General Ledger (Summary)) to view the Payment Journal Summary browser. This browser lists all the accounts debited or credited in the journal and the net amount of debit and credit for the account.

The options common to all browsers in Everest are also available in this browser.
