You can filter the report to see details of records, which are overdue in payments. Enter the starting and ending number to specify the range for Days Late Over and Days Late Under.

You can generate the report for the customers whose balances are due to you for a period of 1 to 30 days.

By default, Days Late over and Days Late under is 9999. The report will consider balances that are due from ‘0’ day to the ‘9999’ day by the customer.
