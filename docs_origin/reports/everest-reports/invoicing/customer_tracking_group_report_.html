Customer Tracking (Group)

You can track your customers by the tracking group they are assigned to, at the time of creation. The Customer Tracking (Group) report provides information on how customers are categorized on the basis of tracking groups.

Customers

A customer might have heard of your company through a television commercial. Referring to your customer data, you can gauge the effectiveness of your television advertising. You can also analyze how much revenue has been generated via this sales promotion media.

‘Found us via’, is a tracking group and ‘television’ advertisement is a tracking selection.

To generate the Customer Tracking Group report, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Reports > Invoicing > Customer Tracking (Group). You will see the Customer Tracking (Group) dialog box.

  • Specify the report parameters and click:

  • Preview, to preview the report in the Report Preview dialog box
  • Print, to print the report
  • Export, to save the report in text, Excel, HTML or Crystal Report file formats
