Activate a Discontinued Activity

To activate a discontinued activity, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select CRM > Business Activity Monitoring > Processes/Activities. You will see the Process/Activities browser.
  • From the Process/Activities browser, select Options > View Discontinued > Activity. You will see the Discontinued Activity Browser displaying the discontinued records.
  • From the browser, highlight the process you want to activate.
  • Select Options > Activate. You will be prompted for confirmation.
  • Click OK. The activated activity will be displayed in the Process/Activities browser.
