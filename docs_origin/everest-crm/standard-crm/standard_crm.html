Standard CRM

Everest CRMprovides you with a standard set of features to manage sales opportunities, create and schedule tasks and appointments, and manage business activities. These may include sending e-mails when events are triggered in Everest, setting templates, defining processes and creating activities to execute the process, scheduling and monitoring activities.

Standard features of CRM can be categorized as below:

  • Opportunity Management
  • Contact Manager
  • Business Activity Monitoring
