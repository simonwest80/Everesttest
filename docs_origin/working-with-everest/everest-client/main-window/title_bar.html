Title Bar

This section is displayed at the top of the Everest main window. It displays the name of the company that you are currently logged into. The title bar contains a few menu options that may be accessed by clicking on the Everest icon on the Title Bar or by right clicking anywhere on the Title Bar.

 Title Bar Menu Options

These title bar menu options allow you to save the modified settings of the main window. You can also view a list of browsers, profiles, documents and journals that are currently open at the workstation.
