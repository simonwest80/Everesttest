Logging In

There are three ways to log in to Everest:

  • Double-click on the Everest icon on your desktop.
  • From the Windows Start menu select Programs > Everest Advanced Edition > Everest.
  • Double-click on the Everest.exe file. This file will be present in the directory specified at the time of installing the Everest client.

When you click on the Everest.exe you will see the Everest login dialog box.

Logging in to your company is a two-step procedure:

  • You must log in to the company database and specify the user details.
  • You then specify the jurisdiction and location/sub-location. The jurisdiction logged into determines the amount of sales tax to be charged. Revenue and expenses will be accounted for in the logged-in location/sub-location. All sales and purchase documents are created for the logged-in location/sub-location. The location/sub-location specified also determines which logo will be printed on documents.
