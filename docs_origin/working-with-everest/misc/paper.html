Paper

You can select the paper size in this field. Some of the sizes available are listed below:

  • Custom
  • Letter 8½ by 11 in
  • A4 210 X 297 mm
  • A5 148 X 210 mm
  • Mini 4¼ X 5 in
  • Legal 8½ X 14 in
  • Executive 7¼ X 10½ in

When you choose a unit of measure in the Dimension in field, the width and height of the paper gets automatically populated depending upon the type of paper size you selected. A check box for rolled paper is displayed when you select Custom option from the Paper drop down list.
