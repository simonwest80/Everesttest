General Notes

You can enter notes that help users to record any relevant details and make it available for other users as well. A timer is automatically invoked to record the number of minutes and seconds for the duration of the call.

If a technician is assigned the task of assessing the extent of work required on a repair job, the technician can record the details of his findings as notes - the technicians who are actually going to do the repair can, refer to these notes.

All general notes can be viewed or discontinued from the Contact Manager profile, My Tasks or All Tasks browser. General notes are added to the above browsers/profile and this icon is displayed besides them. Users can group by task to see the list of tasks, calls and notes.
