Discontinue/Reactivate a Tracking Category for Items

Everest allows you to temporarily discontinue records of tracking categories. When a record is discontinued, it is not deleted from the database; it is merely deactivated. A discontinued record can be made available for selection by activating it again.

 When you discontinue/reactivate a tracking category, the tracking groups and the related tracking choices under it are also discontinued/reactivated.
