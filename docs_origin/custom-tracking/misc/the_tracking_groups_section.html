The Tracking Groups Section

This section displays a list of tracking groups created for the selected tracking category. The tracking groups in the selected tracking category are listed in order of rank.

The Tracking Group Rank

The tracking group rank is the order in which tracking groups are listed when you access the Custom Tracking Setup browser for an item. It also denotes the order in which tracking groups are displayed on relevant pages on your Everest E-Commerce store.

 You can reorganize the rank by moving the selected group using and arrows provided beside the Tracking Groups section.
