The Custom Tracking Setup - Vendor Browser

The Custom Tracking Setup - Vendor browser (path: Purchasing > Custom Tracking > Vendors) displays the tracking categories, tracking groups and tracking choices created for vendors. The groups and choices are ranked according to the order of their creation. You can modify the rank.

The options (path: Custom Tracking Setup - Vendor browser > Options) available in this browser are:

  • Custom Fields for Groups
  • Show Details
  • Selection Break-up
  • Tree View
  • Switch Pane
  • Standard Browser Options
