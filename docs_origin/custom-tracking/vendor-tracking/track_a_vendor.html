Track a Vendor

You will have to assign a vendor to a particular tracking choice in order to track it.

To assign a vendor to a tracking choice, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Purchasing > Vendors. You will see the Vendors browser.
  • Highlight the vendor you want to track, and select Options > Vendor Tracking. You will see the Custom Tracking - Vendor browser listing all the custom tracking category, groups and choices created by you.
  • Highlight the desired tracking group, and tag the choices you want. Choices are displayed in the Selection section of the browser.

 You can append additional information as ‘Notes’ to the selected choice. To do this, point the cursor to the tagged tracking selection and select Notes from the right-click menu. The right-click menu also allows you to select and deselect a choice and select Notes Preview.
