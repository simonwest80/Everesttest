List of System Documents in Everest

The System Documents in **Everest are listed below:**

Purchase Quote Records quotes received from vendors during purchase negotiations.
Purchase Orders Records orders placed with vendors.
Purchase Receipts Records the receipt of items into inventory.
Purchase Invoice Records payment due to the vendor for items and services.
Debit Quote Records proposed vendor returns.
Debit Order Records the items identified to be returned to the vendor.
Debit Memo Records actual returns to vendors.
Sales Quote Records quotes issued to customers during sales negotiations.
Sales Orders Records orders placed by customers.
Sales Invoice Records actual sales of items and services.
Credit Quote Records proposed returns to customers.
Credit Order Records items identified to be returned by the customer.
Credit Memo Records actual returns from customers.
POS Invoice Invoice issued in a Point-of-Sale transaction.
POS Credit Records customer returns in a Point-of-Sale transaction.
Stock Transfer Records inventories moved from one stock area to another.
Work Order Records work orders issued for production.
Inventory Adjustment Documents Records adjustments of quantities and value of items in stock.
