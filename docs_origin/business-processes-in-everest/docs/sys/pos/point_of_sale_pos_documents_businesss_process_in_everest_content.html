Point of Sale (POS) Documents

The Point of Sale (POS) system is synonymous with across the counter sales, where the items are handed over to the customer on immediate payment. You need not create an account for every POS customer. It is relevant only if the items are to be shipped later, or are sold on credit. Everest handles POS using two documents - the POS Invoice and the POS Credit Memo.
