Specify the default (reason closed) group to which the reason closed selection must belong. Sales opportunities are automatically closed when:

  • The sales quote for the sales opportunity is converted to a sales order
  • A sales order or invoice is directly created

When a sales opportunity is automatically closed, the reason closed group specified in this field is automatically displayed in the Reason Closed Group field of the associated Sales Opportunity profile (path: Sales Order profile > Options > Edit Opportunity > Details tab).
