Gift Card Balance Inquiry

You can check gift card balances from the cash register.

To find out the balance on gift cards from the cash register, do the following:

  • Open the POS browser (path: Invoicing > Point of Sale or Ctrl+Shift+F4).
  • Select a Cash Register and press the Enter key. The POS profile displays.
  • Select Options > Gift Cards Balance Inquiry. The Gift Card Number dialog box displays.
  • Swipe, scan or type the gift card number in the Gift Card Number field.
  • Click the Balance button. The system displays the balance on the gift card.

The Gift Card Balance Inquiry menu option is enabled only when your company purchases and activates a license for gift cards.
