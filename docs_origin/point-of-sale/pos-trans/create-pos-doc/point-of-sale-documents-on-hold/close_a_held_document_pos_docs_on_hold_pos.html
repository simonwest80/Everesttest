Close a Held Document

A Point of Sale Document that has been put on hold can be closed from the Held Document browser (path: Options > Document > Open Held Document from the Point of Sale profile).

To close a held document, do the following:

  • Select the document in the Held Document browser and select Options > Close Held Document. The closed document is saved as a closed sales order in the Sales Orders browser (path: Invoicing > Sales Orders > Shipped sales orders button).
