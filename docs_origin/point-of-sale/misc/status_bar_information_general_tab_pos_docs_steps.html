Status Bar Information

The Status Bar is displayed at the bottom of all document profiles in Everest. The options available in the status bar of ** the **Point of Sale (sales invoice) profile:

  • Document Policy
  • Zoom Out/In
  • Preview Off/On
  • Tax
Document Policy
Click the button to view the document policy. This policy can be edited.
Document Policy
Zoom In/Out
Click the Zoom out button to view only the Customer Information, Item Detail Grid and Document Totals. Click the Zoom in button to view all the document details.
Preview On/Off
Choose Preview On to view the inline notes in the new line and choose Preview Off to view only the first line of the inline notes.
Preview On/Off
Tax
Click the icon to recalculate taxes for the Point of Sale (sales invoice) document.
Tax
