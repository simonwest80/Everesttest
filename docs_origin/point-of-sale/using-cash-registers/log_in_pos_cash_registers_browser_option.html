Log In

Point of Sale profiles can be created only if you log in to an available cash register.

To log in to an available cash register, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Invoicing > Point of Sale. You will see the POS Cash Registers browser.
  • From the POS Cash Registers browser, select the available cash register and click Options > Log In. You will see the Point of Sale profile.
