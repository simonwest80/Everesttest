Enter the actual amount of local tax payable by the employer. This is either the percentage or rate or the flat amount depending on the calculation type selected. If you specify the calculation type as Flat Amount or Based on the Hours Worked, the Percentage field changes to the Amount field. For rest of the calculation type, the Percentage field is displayed.
