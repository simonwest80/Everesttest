Additional Earnings and Deductions

All payroll items set up for the additional earnings, deductions and company contributions are displayed in this grid. The records are picked up from the Payroll Transactions profile.

The columns in this grid will be blank, if no payroll items of type additions, deductions and company contributions are set up for the employee in the Employee profile (path: Payroll > Employees >Employee Data > from the Employees browser, double-click the required record).
