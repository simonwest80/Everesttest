Resetting Leave Balances

You can reset the vacation and sick leave balances of the employees to zero at the beginning of every calendar year, if you do not want to carry over them.

Only the leave balances of the employees for whom you have selected the options Reset sick every year and Reset vacation every year in the General tab of the Employee profile (path: Payroll > Employees > Employee Data > from the Employees browser, double-click the required record) are set to zero.

If the option Reset sick every year is not selected for an employee in the Employee profile, the sick leave balance of that employee will not be set to zero, when you reset the leave balance.

To reset sick and vacation leave balances, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Payroll > Reset Leave Balance. You will see the Confirmation window.
  • Click Yes to reset the sick and vacation leave balances to zero.
