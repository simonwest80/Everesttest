View a Payroll Journal

The newly created payroll journals can be viewed from the Unposted Purchase Journals browser.

To view a payroll journal, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Accounting > Account Payable > Purchase Journal. You will see the Unposted Purchase Journals browser listing all the purchase journals which are not posted.
  • Double-click the required record to view its details.

Posted payroll journals can also be viewed from the Unposted Purchase Journals browser by selecting Options > Posted Journals.
