Form W3

Form W-3 summarizes the total wages, Social Security wages, Federal Income Tax withheld, and FICA tax withheld from employees during the year and lists the number of W-2s being transmitted.

Form W3 is a summary of all the Form W2s that you have issued to your employees. Anyone required to file Form W2 must file Form W3 to transmit Copy A of Forms W2. Make a copy of Form W3; keep this and Copy D (For Employer) of Forms W2 with your records for 4 years. Be sure to use Form W3 for the correct year.
