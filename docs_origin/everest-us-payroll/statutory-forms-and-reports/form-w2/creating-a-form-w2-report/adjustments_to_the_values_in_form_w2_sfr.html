Adjustments to the Values in Form W2

You can make adjustments to the values specified in Form W2 and also keep track of them.

You can make adjustments to all the values in the boxes 1 to 14 except for the local taxes and state taxes.

To make adjustments, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Accounting > US Payroll > Statutory Forms > W2. You will see the Form W2 - Employee List dialog box, listing all the employees on your payroll.
  • Select the employee for whom you want to create a Form W2 report and then click W2. You will see the Employee Form W2 created for the selected employee.
  • Click on the box for which you want to make adjustments. You will see the Form W2 Details dialog box.

Clicking the Social Security Wages field takes you to Form W2 Details - Box3.
