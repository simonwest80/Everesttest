Managing Physical Stocks

Effective storage and retrieval of inventoried items require stock areas and locations to be clearly defined; items must be correctly labeled and location of items recorded accurately. You can manage physical stocks in Everest in the following ways:

  • Stock groups and areas - Creating multiple levels in stock groups and stock areas helps you manage your warehouse locations.
  • Item labeling - You can print, design and customize address, item, document and other labels from relevant browsers.
  • Stock transfer - Everest records item movements through stock transfer notes, sales and purchase documents.
