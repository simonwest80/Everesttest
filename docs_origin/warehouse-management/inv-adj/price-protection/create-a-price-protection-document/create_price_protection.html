Create Price Protection

To create a price protection document, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Inventory > Inventory Adjustment > Price Protection. You will see the Price Protection browser.
  • From the Price Protection browser, select Options > New > Price Protection. You will see the Price Protection profile **

You can also create inventory adjustments for price protection from an existing ** Price Protection profile by selecting **Options > New.
