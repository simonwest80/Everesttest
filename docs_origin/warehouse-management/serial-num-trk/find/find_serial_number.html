Find Serial Number

Serial number filters are used to search for serialized/auto-serialized items and items with manufacturers’ serial numbers attached to them. The serial number filter is an extension to the Find Items filter and facilitates search for serial numbers of items.

The Find Serial Number option is useful in the following ways:

  • Finding serial numbers of items, which have an expiry date to ensure that the expired items are not used in sales documents. This is particularly useful in the pharmaceutical industry and grocery stores.
  • Printing bar codes for serialized items, by using the serial number of the item in the bar code.
  • When a lot of serialized items is damaged, you can use the lot number to find serial numbers of the damaged items and discontinue those items from use in document.
  • Locating the stock areas where the serialized items have been stored, to enable speedy inspection and delivery to customers.

The Find Serial Number option yields the data in the same format as the Serial Numbers browser does.
