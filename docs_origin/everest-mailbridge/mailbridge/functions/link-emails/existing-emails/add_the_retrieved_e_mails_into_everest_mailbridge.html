Add the Retrieved E-mails into Everest

To add the retrieved e-mails, do the following:

  • From the right panel of the Process E-mail window, choose the mail you want to link, and select Options > Add to Everest. The mail is retrieved as a ‘task’ or ‘e-mail’ based on the settings you have made.
