Process E-mail Window

The Process E-mailwindow allows you to retrieve e-mails and link them to Everest as ‘tasks/e-mails’.

To access the Process E-mail window, do the following:

  • Once you have logged in to Everest MailBridge, right-click on the icon on the status bar of your desktop and select Process E-mail. You will see the Process E-mail window.
