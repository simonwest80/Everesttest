Link New E-mails

Everest MailBridge allows you to link e-mails you receive/send in the inbox of your local mail client, to Everest.

Based on the option you have selected in the Link E-mail to Everest as field of Settings dialog box, you can link e-mails that you receive from customers as ‘tasks’ or ‘e-mails’.
