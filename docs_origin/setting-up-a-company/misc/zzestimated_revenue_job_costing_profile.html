Enter the revenue that you have projected to receive from the project being defined. You can enter a maximum of 15 integers and 4 decimals in this field.

The number of decimals displayed in the Estimated Revenue field depends on the number of decimal digits specified in your settings:

  • Currency profile - File > Setup > Accounting > Multicurrency > Currency.
  • Regional Settings Properties dialog box – Operating Systems > Regional Settings Options > Currency tab.
