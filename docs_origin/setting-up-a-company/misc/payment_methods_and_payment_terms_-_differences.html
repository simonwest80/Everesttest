Payment Methods and Payment Terms - Differences

Payment methods define how the payment must be made, while payment terms define when the payment must be made.

Use payment terms to compute the due date for payment. Discounts applicable for early payments are also specified in the PaymentTerms profile.

Payment methods can be assigned to payment terms so that for a particular payment term, payments can be made or received only through the specified payment methods.

Make sure that you distinguish between payment terms and payment methods. For instance, cash and check are payment methods, whereas Net 30 days and Cash on delivery are payment terms.
