Use the Move Up option to move the segment up to a higher position in the segment hierarchy.

Use the Move Down option to move the segment down to a lower position in the segment hierarchy.

Use the Delete option to remove a segment from your segment format.

Menu Options

Before accounts are created, options are available to move and remove segments in your segment format. The following options are available from the Options menu or by right clicking a segment in the list at the bottom of the screen:
Move Up
Move Down
Delete

Once an account gets created for a segment, these options are no longer available.
