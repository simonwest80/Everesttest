View and Activate a Discontinued Chart of Account

To view and activate a discontinued chart of account, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Accounting > Chart of Accounts. You will see the Chart of Accounts browser.
  • From the Chart of Accounts browser, select Options > View Discontinued. You will see the discontinued record in the Discontinued Chart of Accounts browser.
  • From the Discontinued Chart of Accounts browser, highlight the record.
  • Select

    Options > Activate. You will be prompted for confirmation.

  • Click

    OK. The activated record will be displayed in the Chart of Accounts browser.
