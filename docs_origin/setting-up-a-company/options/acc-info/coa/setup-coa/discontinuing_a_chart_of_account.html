Discontinue a Chart of Account

Everest allows you to temporarily discontinue a chart of account. When you discontinue a chart of account, the record is not deleted from the database; it is deactivated. A discontinued record can be made available for selection by activating it again.

You cannot discontinue a chart of account that:

  • has a non-zero balance
  • has a detail account beneath it
  • is assigned to a posting group
  • is assigned to a payment method
  • is included in an unposted journal
  • is used in Everest US Payroll

To discontinue a chart of account, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Accounting > Chart of Accounts. You will see the Chart of Accounts browser.
  • From the Chart of Accounts browser, highlight the record.
  • Select Options > Discontinue. You will be prompted for confirmation.
  • Click OK. The record that was selected is no longer visible in the Chart of Accounts browser.
