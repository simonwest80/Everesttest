View and Activate a Discontinued Delivery Method

To view and activate a discontinued delivery method, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select File > Setup > Freight > Delivery Methods. You will see the Delivery Methods browser.
  • From the Delivery Methods browser, select Options > View Discontinued. You will see the discontinued record in the Discontinued Delivery Methods browser.
  • From the Discontinued Delivery Methods browser, highlight the record.
  • Select

    Options > Activate. You will be prompted for confirmation.

  • Click

    OK. The activated record will be displayed in the Delivery Methods browser.
