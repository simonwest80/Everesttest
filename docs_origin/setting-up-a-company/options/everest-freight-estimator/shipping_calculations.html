Everest Freight Estimator

Freight is the shipment of goods from your company/warehouse to the shipping address or vice versa and freight rate is the charge applicable to ship the ordered items. You can estimate the freight payable on items shipped to customers or shipped from/to vendors using the Everest Freight Estimator (EFE). EFE is a multi-carrier freight charges calculator. It calculates the freight between any two ZIP codes in the U.S. For international customers, you have to specify the zones (for the origin and destination countries) for computing the freight charges on items.

Freight charges also include related costs like COD, Residential charges, and Insurance.

On the online store, the shopper can view the shipping calculator only at the time of checking out. By selecting the delivery methods from the drop-down list, the shopper can view the different freight charges for the items.

You can calculate freight charges by creating the following components in Everest. These are interlinked to compute freight charges for items purchased on the online store.

  • Carrier
  • Delivery Methods
  • Zones
  • Zone Details
  • Rates
