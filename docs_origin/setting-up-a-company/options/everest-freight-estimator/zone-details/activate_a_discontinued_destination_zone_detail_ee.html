Activate a Discontinued Zone Detail

You can make a discontinued zone detail available for selection by activating it again.

To view and activate a discontinued zone detail, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select File > Setup > Freight > Zones Details. You will see the Zone Details browser.
  • From the Zone Details browser, select Options > View Discontinued. You will see the Discontinued Zone Details browser listing all the deactivated zone details.
  • Highlight the zone you want to activate.
  • From the Discontinued Zone Details browser, select Options > Activate. You will be prompted for confirmation.
  • Click OK. The activated record will be displayed in the Zone Details browser.
