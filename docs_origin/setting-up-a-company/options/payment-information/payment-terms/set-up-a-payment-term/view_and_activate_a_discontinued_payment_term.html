View and Activate a Discontinued Payment Term

To view and activate a discontinued payment term, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select File > Setup > Accounting > Payment Terms. You will see the Payment Terms browser.
  • From the Payment Terms browser, select Options > View Discontinued. You will see the discontinued record in the Discontinued Payment Terms browser.
  • From the Discontinued Payment Terms browser, highlight the record.
  • Select

    Options > Activate. You will be prompted for confirmation.

  • Click

    OK. The activated record will be displayed in the Payment Terms browser.
