Job Explorer - Left Panel

This panel displays the job codes in a tree-view. The tree-view makes it easier to see the relationship between parent and child job codes. You can create jobs or modify the existing jobs from the explorer.

To create a job from the explorer, right-click in the left panel and select New > In New Window from the menu.

To modify an existing job, select that job in the left panel, right-click and choose Edit > In New Window. You will see the Job Costing profile where you can change the information.
