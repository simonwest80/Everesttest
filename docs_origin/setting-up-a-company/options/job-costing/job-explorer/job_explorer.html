Job Explorer

The Job Explorer (path: File > Setup > Miscellaneous > Job Explorer) displays details of existing jobs. To limit the amount of data displayed, you can select the date range of the jobs to view. By default, the Job Explorer shows job type details created from the beginning of the current month to the current date. You can change the date range of the jobs to view and save your selection so that your browser always opens to your selected date range. An option is also available to filter the type of documents viewed, such as only final, sales, or purchase documents. The Job Explorer contains three sections: the Date Filter header and the Left and Right panels.
