View and Activate a Discontinued Special Tax Rule

To view and activate a discontinued special tax rule, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select File > Setup > Accounting > Taxes > Special Tax Rule. You will see the Special Tax Rules browser.
  • From the Special Tax Rules browser, select Options > View Discontinued. You will see the discontinued record in the Discontinued Special Tax Rules browser.
  • From the Discontinued Special Tax Rules ** browser, highlight the record.
  • Select

    Options > Activate. You will be prompted for confirmation.

  • Click

    OK. The activated record will be displayed in the Special Tax Rules browser.
