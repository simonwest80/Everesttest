Product Registration

You can either continue with your evaluation or register Everest and the various integrated options from the Company Setup profile > Activation tab.

  • After the specified evaluation period, when you log in to Everest, the registration details are displayed.

To access the registration details, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select File > Setup > Company. You will see the Company Setup profile.
  • From the Company Setup profile, choose the Activation tab.
