Accounting - Chart of Accounts - Template

Use Chart of Accounts set up in template company
Select this radio button to use the predefined Chart of Accounts settings from the template company. Click the link Click here to view the Chart of Accounts to view the COA of the template company.
  • Click Next. You will see the Accounting - Posting Group screen.
Select a COA from a list of pre-defined COAs
Select this radio button to use the pre-defined Chart of Accounts available in Everest.
  • Click Next. You will see the Accounting - Chart of Accounts  screen.

: The COA selected from a list of pre-defined COAs will replace the COA of the template company and the following COA information in the template company will be lost:

  • : Tax Components
  • : Posting Groups
  • : Payment Methods
  • : Accounting Defaults
  • : Other Accounts
  • : Payroll Accounts
