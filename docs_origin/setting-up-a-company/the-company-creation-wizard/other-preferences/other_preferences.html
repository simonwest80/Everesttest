Other Preferences

You can customize Everest for your business needs through the Flow Control and Default preferences. Use this section to define these settings.

You can direct the system to automatically print documents. You can also restrict access to documents.

It is very important that you go through each of these options as they can make a significant difference to the efficiency and practical usefulness of Everest.

The applicable preferences to each section in Everest are detailed under the respective section. For example, Item preferences and Defaults are dealt with under the chapter Managing Items.

Everest’s preferences are divided into two categories:

  • Flow control
  • Defaults

After specifying the preferences, click Next. You will see the Conclusion section of the wizard.
