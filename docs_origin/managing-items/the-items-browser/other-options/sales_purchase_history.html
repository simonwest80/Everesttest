Sales/Purchase History

Use the Sales/Purchase History option (Path: Items Browser > Options > Sales/Purchase History) to view the history of a selected item for the specified document type. This is an extremely useful feature of Everest which enables the Sales and Purchasing departments to negotiate better purchase or sales prices.

When a document is selected, the appropriate (Item Purchase History/ Item Sales History) browser is displayed. UM, Quantity, Price, Discount, and Cost are shown in the measure entered in the relevant document.
