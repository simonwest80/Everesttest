Item Reports

Inventory reports display inventory assigned to brands, they are grouped under categories, stock areas/bins, items and items that are tagged to tracking groups or selections.

The inventory reports that are available in Everest are:

  • Detail by Brand
  • Detail by Category - Entry Date (Serialized)
  • Detail by Category - Stock Bin
  • Detail by Item - Entry Date (Serialized)
  • Item Tracking (Group)
  • Item Tracking (Selection)
  • Inventory Summary
  • Summary by Category - Multiple Stock Bin
  • Summary by Category - Single Stock Bin
  • Summary by Item Code - Stock Bin
  • Inventory Control Sheet
