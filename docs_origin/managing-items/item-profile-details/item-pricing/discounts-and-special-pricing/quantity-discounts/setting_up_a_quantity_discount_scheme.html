Define Quantity Discounts

To define quantity discounts, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Inventory > Discounts > Quantity Discounts. You will see the Quantity Discounts browser.
  • From the Quantity Discounts browser, select Options > New > In New Window. You will see the Quantity Discount dialog box.

You can also define quantity discounts from existing Quantity Discount dialog box (Select Options > New) and from the Item profile – Sales tab (Click the Special Pricing button and on the Quantity Discount tab, select Options > New > In New Window to create a pricing scheme in the Quantity Discount dialog box).
