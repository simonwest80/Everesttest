Activate a Discontinued Purpose of Visit

You can make a discontinued purpose of visit available for selection by activating it again.

To activate a discontinued purpose of visit, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Time Clock > Purpose of Visits. You will see the Purpose of Visits browser.
  • From the Purpose of Visits browser, select Options > ViewDiscontinued. You will see the Discontinued Purpose of Visits browser.
  • From the Discontinued Purpose of Visits browser, highlight the record to be activated.
  • Select Options > Activate. You will see the Confirm window.
  • Click OK.
  • The record is activated and displayed in the browser.
