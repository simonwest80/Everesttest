Leave Carry Forward Browser

The Leave Carry Forward browser displays the leave types, leaves availed, leaves credited, and leaves which are carried forward to an employee’s account, from the previous year.

You can carried forward leaves only if the Is Carry Forward option is checked in the Leave Types profile.

Sick leaves cannot be carried forward. Unavailed sick leaves lapse. Paid leaves/privileged leaves can be carried forward, from the previous year.

 

To view the leave carry forward browser, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Time Clock > Leave Carry Forward. You will see the Leave Carry Forward browser.

Leave Carry Forward Browser Columns

The options available in the Leave Carry Forward browser are:

  • Carry Forward Process
  • Carry Forward Log
