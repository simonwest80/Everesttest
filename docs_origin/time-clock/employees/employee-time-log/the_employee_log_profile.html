The Employee Log Profile

The Employee Log profile allows you to enter the employee code, shift and the employee clock in/clock out time. Once the Clocked In Time and Clocked Out Time is entered and saved, it does not change when viewed with other time zone or daylight saving time settings.

Code
Select the unique code that identifies the employee. This is a mandatory field.
Shift
Specify the employee’s work shift. This is a mandatory field.
Clocked In Time
Specify the date and time that the employee clocked in. By default, 12:00 AM gets displayed beside the date when you select the date from the drop-down calendar. This is a mandatory field.
Once this time is entered and saved, it does not change when viewed with other time zone or daylight saving time (DST) settings.
DST settings that change after an employee clocks in but before clocking out impact only the clock out time and not the clock in time.

On November 3, 2007, an employee clocks in at 9:00pm. Daylight saving time changes at 2:00am on November 4, 2007. The employee clocks out at 6:00am on November 4, 2007. The clock out time of 6:00am is from the revised (changed) time.

Clocked Out Time
Specify the date and time that the employee clocked out. By default, 12:00 AM gets displayed beside the date when you select the date from the drop-down calendar. This is a mandatory field.
Once this time is entered and saved, it does not change when viewed with other time zone or daylight saving time (DST) settings.
DST settings that change after an employee clocks in but before clocking out impact only the clock out time and not the clock in time.

On November 3, 2007, an employee clocks in at 9:00pm. Daylight saving time changes at 2:00am on November 4, 2007. The employee clocks out at 6:00am on November 4, 2007. The clock out time of 6:00am is from the revised (changed) time.

Working Day
Check this box if the employee works on a holiday that is counted as a working day.
On Duty
Check this box if the employee is out on office work and the Time Out field was validated for that day in the employee’s absence.
Notes
Enter any relevant notes, information, or comments.
