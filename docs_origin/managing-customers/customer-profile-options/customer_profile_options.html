The Customer Profile - Options

The options available from the Customer profile (path: Invoicing > Customers > Options > New > In New Window/In Current Window > Options) are grouped under the following headings:

Information Available from the Customer profile

  • History
  • Quick Aging
  • Sales Opportunity
  • Documents

Other Options

  • View Formatted Address
  • Modify Address
  • Credit Cards
  • Customer Fax
  • Customer Tracking
  • Print Label
  • Events History
  • Send Email
  • Goto Website
  • Contact Manager

The Customer profile contains standard menu options common to all profiles in Everest.
