The Customer Profile – Logo

This tab allows you to attach a bitmap (.bmp) image of the customer’s business logo. This logo is used when printing packing slips specifically for a blind shipment of goods on behalf of the customer.

 The **Blind Ship option allows you to drop ship items to your customer’s customer. Details of the actual vendor are not printed on the packing slips (in the Blind Ship format), sent with the goods.**

 A customer (A) who buys goods from you could be a retail vendor, with his own customers (B, C and D). If customer (A) does not have sufficient stocks to fulfil the sales orders of customers (B, C and D), he can request you to deliver the goods, as a blind shipment. You will ship the goods to customers (B, C and D), with either the address or logo of customer (A) printed on the packing slips. When customers (B, C and D) receive the goods, they are unaware of the origin of the shipment. You can raise a normal sales invoice on customer (A) and the acknowledged copy of the packing slip must be attached as proof of delivery.

 The recommended size for the bitmap is 5.0 x 1.4 inches. Only file formats of the type .bmp are supported. The recommended file size of the logo is less than 1 MB. If the file size exceeds 1 MB, the performance of report printing job may be affected because the logo must be copied to the local workstation for each print job. However sub-locations cannot have separate logos.

To attach the customer’s business logo, do the following:

  • Click the look up button beside Image. You will see the Open dialog box.
  • Select the required logo bitmap (.bmp) file and click Open. The logo appears in the display area.
