Comments

Any comments relevant to the customer may be entered in this field.

Notes and comments are automatically displayed prior to creating/modifying a document associated to the customer if either:

  • The Pop up comments on document option (path: Customer profile > Miscellaneous tab) is selected OR
  • The Pop up and first show notes attached to the respective customer or vendor associated with the document prior to opening it check box in the Document dialog box (path: File > Setup > Preferences > FlowControl > Documents > Popups tab) is selected.
