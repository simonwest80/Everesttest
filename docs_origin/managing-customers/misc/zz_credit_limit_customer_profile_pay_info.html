Enter the credit limit applicable to the customer using a maximum of 15 integers and 3 decimals.

The Credit Limit field cannot have a negative value and the last decimal is rounded off. The number of decimals displayed in this field depends on the number of decimal specified in your settings:

  • Currency profile - path: File > Setup > Accounting > Multicurrency > Currency > Options > Edit > In New Window > Decimals to display field.
  • Regional Settings Properties dialog box - From the Currency tab in the regional settings. You can modify the regional settings from the Control Panel of your operating system.
