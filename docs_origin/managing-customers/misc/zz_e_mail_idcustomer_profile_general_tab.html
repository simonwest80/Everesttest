Enter a valid e-mail address of the customer. The customer can use this e-mail ID to login to your Everest eStorefront online store.

You cannot select multiple e-mail addresses in this field.

You can send an e-mail to a customer directly from this field. Double-click the e-mail ID of the customer to open a New Message window of Everest E-mail. The “To” field of the e-mail window is populated with the name of the customer.
