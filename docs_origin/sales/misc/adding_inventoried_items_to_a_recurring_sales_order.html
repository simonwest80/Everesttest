Adding Inventoried Items to a Recurring Sales Order

If you add an inventoried item to a recurring sales order and save it, the recurrence link is lost.

‘Recurrence link’ is the connection that ties up all sales orders forming the recurring series. When the recurrence link is broken for a sales order, it is no longer displayed in the RecurringSales Orders browser.

You create recurring sales orders for a printer maintenace contract. If you replace the printer’s toner as part of the maintenance, you can add the item to the recurring sales order.

When you add an inventoried item to a recurring sales order Everest **warns you that the document being modified will be delinked from the series.**

  • Click Yes to proceed.

The Recurring Document ID for the document being modified is deleted and the order is not visible in the Recurring Sales Orders browser (path: Sales Orders browser ** > **Options > Recurring Documents); however, orders that have been delinked are visible in the regular view of the Sales Orders browser.

  • Click Cancel to abort the process.
