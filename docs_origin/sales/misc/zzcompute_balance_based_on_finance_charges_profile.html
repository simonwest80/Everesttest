Select the documents that are to be included while arriving at the outstanding balance due from customers. The All Documents option is displayed by default in this field. The options are:

  • All Documents - When this option is selected the balance due is arrived at by summing the customer’s invoices and deducting the deposits received on sales orders.
  • Invoices Only - When this option is selected the balance due is computed as the sum of the customer’s invoices only.
  • Invoices and Credit Memos Only - When this option is selected, the outstanding balance is computed by summing the customer’s invoices and deducting the balances of the customer’s credit memo(s).
