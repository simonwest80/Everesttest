Attach a Note to a Sales Document

To attach a note to the sales document, do the following:

  • From the Sales Document profile, click the Note icon displayed beside the Document #. You will see the Document Notes window.
  • Enter any information about the document.
  • Select Options > Spell Check or press the F7 key to spell check your notes.
  • Click the Save ** button and exit from the window.

OR

  • From the Sales Document profile, select Options > Document > Document Notes.
  • Enter any information about the document.
  • Click the Save button and exit the window.
