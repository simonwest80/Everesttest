Received/Open Credit Orders

When you select Invoicing > Credit Orders, ** by default the **Credit Orders browser that is displayed contains the open credit orders. You can also view the received (closed) credit orders.

Document Status

Select Options > Received Credit Orders to view the credit orders that have been shipped in the Shipped Credit Orders browser.

From the Received Credit Orders browser, select Options > Open Credit Orders to view the open credit orders.
