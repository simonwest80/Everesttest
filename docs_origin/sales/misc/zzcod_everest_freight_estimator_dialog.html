COD (Cash on Delivery) fee is payable if the carrier is responsible for collecting the invoice value from the customer. This fee is included in the total freight charges. This box is automatically selected if COD is selected in the Payment Term Type field of the Payment Term profile (of the term selected in the Terms field of the document profile). This field cannot be edited.
