Purge a Sales Quote

You cannot cancel a sales quote; however, you can purge it. Purging permanently deletes the sales quote from the system.

A sales quote can be purged when it expires.

There are two ways to purge sales quotes:

  • Using the Purge option in the Sales Quotes Browser.
  • Using the Purge option in the Utility menu.

To purge a sales quote from the sales quote browser, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Invoicing > Sales Quotes. You will see the Sales Quotes browser.
  • Highlight the required sales quote(s) and select Options > Purge.

To purge sales quotes from the utility menu, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Utility > Purge > Sales Quotes. You will see the Purge (Sales Quotes) browser.
  • Highlight the required quote(s) and select Options > Purge. You will see a confirmation window asking you to confirm the purge command.
  • Select OK to purge the quote.
