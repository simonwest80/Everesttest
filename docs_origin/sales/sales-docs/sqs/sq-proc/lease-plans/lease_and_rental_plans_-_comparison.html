Lease and Rental Plans - Comparison

Lease and rental plans are compared in this section.

  • Everest does not distinguish between lease and rental plans. Both are created from the same Lease Plan profile.
  • Rental plans are used when you want to rent out your product to a customer but do not want to transfer the ownership. Lease plans are used when you want to lease out the product to the customer and also transfer the ownership.
  • The main difference between the lease plan and rental plan is that in case of a lease, the leasing company would buy your product and lease it to the customer. In case of rent, you rent the product to the customer.
