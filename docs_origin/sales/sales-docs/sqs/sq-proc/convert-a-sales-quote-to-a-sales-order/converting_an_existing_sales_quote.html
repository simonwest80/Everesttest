Convert an Existing Sales Quote to a Sales Order

You can convert a sales quote to an order by selecting **Options > Process > Reserve in the sales quote. All the details of the quote are displayed in the new Sales Order profile. The Ordered column displays the quantity quoted by default. You may make any changes required to the order quantity and to any other information (including customer information) in the order and save the order.**

You can no longer access the sales quote from the **Sales Quotes browser. However, it appears as a reference number in the Document Status section of the Sales Order profile.**
