Customer Balance

This option brings up the Customer Aging Information browser that displays the sales or sales return documents that have outstanding payments or deposits.

Status Displayed Icon Description
No Balance


(color: blue text)		 The customer has no outstanding payments to be made to the company.
Balance overdue


(color: red text)		 The customer has a balance with at least one document past due. The customer balance is encrypted in the icon in the format ‘Bnnyyyyyynn’ where ‘yyyyyy’ is the balance. The first alphabet, the first two numbers and the last two numbers do not represent any information and are random. In the icon shown in the column on the left, the customer’s outstanding balance is 11,408.44.
Balance within terms specified


(color: yellow text)		 The customer has a balance that is not past the due date specified in the sales document. The customer balance is encrypted in the icon in the format ‘Bnnyyyyyynn’ where ‘yyyyyy’ is the balance. The first alphabet, the first two numbers and the last two numbers do not represent any information and are random. In the icon shown in the column on the left, the customer’s outstanding balance is 63.95.
Credit Balance


(balance owed to customer is prefixed with a negative sign)		 Your company owes the customer money. The balance owed to the customer is prefixed with a negative sign. The balance is encrypted in the icon in the format ‘Bnn-yyyyyynn’ where ‘-yyyyyy’ is the balance. The first alphabet, the first two numbers and the last two numbers do not represent any information and are random. In the icon shown in the column on the left, the balance due to the customer is 5,149.05.

You will see the Customer Balance indicator in the status bar of a sales document only if you have set a flow control preference by selecting the Show customer balance in encoded fashion check box in the Documents dialog box (path: File > Setup > Preferences > Flow Control > Documents > Miscellaneous > Others > Others 1 tab).
