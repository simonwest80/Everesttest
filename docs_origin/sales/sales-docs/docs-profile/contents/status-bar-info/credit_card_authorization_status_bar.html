Credit Card Authorization

Authorizations obtained for credit card payments are indicated in the Credit card authorization section of the status bar on sales document (sales order and sales invoice) profiles. This section is visible only when you receive payment through a credit card and have authorized it.

Authorization for a credit card payment is obtained by selecting the Pre-Authorization option in the Card Verification dialog box.

The Card Verification Dialog Box

Click in this section to view the Batch Payment Process browser. The Batch Payment Process browser allows you to process the payment so that the funds are transferred to your account from the customer’s account by the financial institution with whom you have an agreement.
