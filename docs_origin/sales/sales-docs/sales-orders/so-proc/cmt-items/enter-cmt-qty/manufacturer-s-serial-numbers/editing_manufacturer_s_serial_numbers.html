Editing Manufacturer’s Serial Numbers

You may edit a manufacturer’s serial number from any of the following locations:

  • The Serial Numbers browser (path: Inventory > Serial Numbers)

Modifying the serial number profile

  • The Serial # browser (from the Sales Order profile select Options >All Items > Show All Serial #). From this browser multiple manufacturer’s serial numbers can also be re-assigned.
