Credit Card Processing for Refunds

Refunds may have to be made against a credit card payment that is authorized and captured. A credit card payment is automatically processed when the payment method for the credit card has the UsePayment Processor check box selected in the Credit Card Processing tab (path: File > Setup > Accounting > Payment Methods > Options > Edit > In New Window >Credit Card Processing tab).

When a refund is made, the charge to the credit card is reversed and amounts are transferred from your merchant account back to the credit card holder’s issuing bank.

To refund a payment to a customer, do the following:

  • From the Sales Order/Invoice profile, select Options > Payment > Refund. You will see the Refund dialog box.
  • Select the appropriate credit card payment method in the Payment Method field.
  • Enter the amount to be refunded in the Amount field.
  • Click OK. You will see the Card Verification dialog box.
