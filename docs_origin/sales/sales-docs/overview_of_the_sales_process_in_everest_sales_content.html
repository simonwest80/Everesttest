Overview of the Sales Process in Everest

The sales process in Everest is managed through the following sales documents:

  • Sales Quote
  • Sales Order
  • Sales Invoice

The Flow Chart of the Sales Process gives you an overview of the Sales Process in Everest. It depicts each step in the process, the documents created and the system journals in which accounting entries are made.

Everest also allows you to track pre-sales efforts through Sales Opportunities and Sales Analysis Reports.
