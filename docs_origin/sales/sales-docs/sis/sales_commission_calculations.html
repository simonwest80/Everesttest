Sales Commission Calculations

Commissionable/Non-commissionable Revenues

Revenues can be classified into commissionable and non-commissionable. This helps you in computing the commissions to be given to the sales staff at the end of a period. The Include in commission report calculation check box and the Commission Rate % in the Item profile (path: Inventory > Items > Options > Edit > In New Window > Sales tab) facilitate in doing this.

The Commission Report calculates the commission that must be given to the sales representatives for the sales made by them.
