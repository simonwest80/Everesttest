Record Colors in the Pick Ticket Browser

The records in the Pick Ticket browser are sometimes colored blue, green and brick red. Everest automatically displays these colors to indicate different statuses. These are detailed below:

This color indicates that back orders are not allowed for the selected customer. For example, you CANNOT partly ship the items to the customer.


Allow Back Order Creation


By default, such sales orders are not selected in the Pick
Ticket Browser.


You can however, enter the picked quantity in the Quantity
to be Picked column and commit them from the Pull
Sheet browser.
Indicates that the items marked for creating drop-ship purchase orders are unchecked in the browser.


You can identify that these items have been marked for generating drop-ship purchase order since the check box in the Dropship column (line items grid) in the respective sales order is selected.
Indicates that the items marked for creating purchase order with links are unchecked in the browser.


You can identify that these items have been marked for generating purchase order with links since the PO
# column (line items grid) in the respective sales order displays the purchase order number for the line item.
This color indicates that the items cannot be fully picked. For example, some items may be on back order.
This color indicates that critical and dependent items on the sales order cannot be partially shipped.


Critical Items


You can, however, enter the picked quantity for both critical and non-critical items in the Quantity to be Picked column and commit them from the Pull Sheet window.
This color indicates manually committed line items. These items are selected or deselected depending upon the selected option in the Line Items section of the Pull Sheet Wizard.
