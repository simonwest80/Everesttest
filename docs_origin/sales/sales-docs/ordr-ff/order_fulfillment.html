Order Fulfillment - Introduction

The order fulfillment process comprises all the activities involved in shipping the items ordered or in delivering the services ordered. In case of inventoried items, it normally involves identifying the orders,  identification of item quantities, the stock bins from which the items to be shipped must be drawn (taken), packing the items, printing packing slips, and printing shipping labels.

Everest helps you execute the following functions:

  • Printing Packing Slips
  • Tracking Shipment Information of Items Shipped
  • Printing Address Labels
  • Pick List Creation
