View Recurring Sales Orders

Recurring sales orders are displayed in the Sales Orders browser (path: Invoicing > Sales Orders)

You can view them by selecting Options > Recurring Documents from the browser. All recurring sales orders that have not been processed into sales invoices are displayed.

Group the Sales Orders browser by the Recurring doc. ID column to view the sales orders pertaining to each series.
