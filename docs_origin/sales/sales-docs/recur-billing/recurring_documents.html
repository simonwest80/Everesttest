Recurring Documents

Recurring documents may be defined as documents that you create for sales and purchases on a recurring or repeated basis.

Characteristics of recurring documents

Recurring documents are characterized by the following attributes:

  • A schedule for billing

Rent is paid on the 5th of every month.

  • A time period of contract

Lease of building for one year during which rent has to be paid.

  • Identical items, quantity and price

If recurring documents are created for rent, the item code for rent, the price and the amount would be identical for all the 12 documents created during the lease period.

  • Identical billing information

The billing information such as customer or vendor information, taxes, delivery method, payment terms, etc are the same for all documents in the series.
