Create a Sales Opportunity

To create a sales opportunity, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Invoicing > Sales Opportunities. You will see the Sales Opportunities browser.
  • From the Sales Opportunities browser, select Options > New. You will see the Sales Opportunity profile.

Sales opportunities ** can also be created:

  • From an existing Sales Opportunity profile by selecting Options > New.
  • If you create a sales quote directly without first creating a sales opportunity, the Sales Opportunity profile is populated with details from the sales quote. You can view it  from the Sales Quote profile by selecting Options > Edit Opportunity.
