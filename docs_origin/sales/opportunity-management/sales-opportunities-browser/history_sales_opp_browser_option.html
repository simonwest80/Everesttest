History

Select this option to view the Sales Opportunities History browser. This browser displays changes made to the Sales Opportunity profile. It provides details such as the date and time modifications were made to the sales opportunity. The time is displayed with reference to the database server, and not the workstation.

You can view the history for the selected opportunity (path: Options > History > This Opportunity) or for all opportunities (path: Options > History > All Opportunities).
