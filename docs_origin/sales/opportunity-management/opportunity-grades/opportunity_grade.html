Opportunity Grades

The grade option allows you to rate whether the opportunity is good, excellent, etc. Grading enables you to focus on opportunities with good ratings.

An opportunity from a reputed company who is likely to give you repeat orders is graded high. Whereas one from a company that does not have a good credit rating would be graded lower.

To create a grade, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select CRM > Opportunity Management > Setup > Grade. You will see the Opportunity Grade browser.
  • From the Opportunity Grade browser, select Options > New > In New Window. You will see the Opportunity Grade profile.

You can also create an opportunity grade from:

  • Any profile that contains the Grade field.
  • An existing Opportunity Grade profile by clicking Options > New.
