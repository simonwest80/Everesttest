The Opportunity Grade Browser

The Opportunity Grade browser (path: CRM > Opportunity Management > Setup > Grade) displays all the grades that are defined.

The browser displays the Rank column. The rank identifies the hierarchy of the grades.

If Excellent is the best grade that can be given, you would defined its rank as 1.

Use the Up and Down arrows to change the rank of an opportunity grade.

Options common to all browsers are available in this browser.
