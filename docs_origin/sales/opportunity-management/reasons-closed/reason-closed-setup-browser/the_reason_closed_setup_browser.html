The Reason Closed Setup Browser

The Reason Closed Setup browser (path: CRM > Opportunity Management > Setup > Reason Closed) displays all the reason closed groups and the corresponding reason closed choices that are defined under each group.

The browser displays the Rank column. The rank identifies the hierarchy of the reasons for closure.

If “Customer not Interested” is the reason why opportunities are closed most frequently, you would define its rank as 1.

Use the Up and Down arrows to change the rank of a reason for closure.

Options common to all browsers are also available in this browser.
