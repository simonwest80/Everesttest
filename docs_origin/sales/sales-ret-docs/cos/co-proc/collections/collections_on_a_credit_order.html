Collections on a Credit Order

You may initially refund a customer for items that are returned. Later, your customer may decide to take another item in exchange for the returned item. In this case, you would receive either a full or partial collection on a credit order/memo from the customer.

Collections from customers on a credit order/memo can be made by cash, check or credit card. A credit card payment is processed when you select the Use Payment Processor option in the Credit Card Processing tab (path: File > Setup > Accounting > Payment Methods > Options > Edit > In New Window > Credit Card Processing tab).

To receive collection using a credit card, do the following:

  • From the Credit Order/Credit Memo profile, select Options > Payment > Collect. You will see the Collection dialog box.
  • Select the appropriate credit card payment method in the Payment Method field.
  • Enter the amount to be collected in the Amount field.
  • Click OK. You will see the Card Verification dialog box.

The Collect option is enabled only when you have made the refund on the credit order/memo.
