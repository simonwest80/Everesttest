Refunds to Customers on a Credit Order

Refunds to customers from a credit order can be made by cash, check, or a credit card. A credit card payment is processed only when you select the Use Payment Processor option in the Credit Card Processing tab (path: File > Setup > Accounting > Payment Methods > Options > New > In New Window > Credit Card Processing tab).

To make refunds on a credit order, do the following:

  • From the Credit Order profile, select Options > Payment > Refund. You will see the Refund dialog box.
  • Select the appropriate credit card payment method in the Payment Method field.
  • Enter the amount to be refunded in the Amount field.
  • Click OK. You will see the Card Verification dialog box. **

The Refund option is enabled only when the total amount on the credit order is NOT refunded to the customer. On a new credit order, the option is available when the credit order is saved.
