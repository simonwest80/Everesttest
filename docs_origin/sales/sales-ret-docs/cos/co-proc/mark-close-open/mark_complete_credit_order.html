Mark Close - Credit Order

A credit order is closed either when all items on the order are received completely or when you mark it closed. You can mark an order closed even if the order contains line items with committed quantities.

Closed credit orders are displayed only in the Received Credit Orders browser. The status of a credit order is displayed in the lower section of the Credit Order profile.

To mark a credit order close, do the following:

  • From the Credit Order profile, select Options > Process > Mark Close/Open. The credit order will be marked as ‘Closed’.

When you close an ‘open’ credit order, the following changes take place:

  • The requested quantity is reduced to zero.
  • The committed quantity, if any, is detached.

When you close a ‘backorder’, the following changes take place:

  • The requested quantity is reduced to the quantity credited (on partially credited quantities).
  • The committed quantity, if any, is detached.
