The Credit Orders Browser

The Credit Orders browser (path: Invoicing > Credit Orders) displays all the credit orders that are created, but not converted to credit memos.

By default the Credit Orders browser displays documents that are created in the default period specified for sales document browsers.

Identify the Credit Order Status

The Status column in the Credit Order browser shows the status of the credit order. There are three statuses:

  • Open – None of the items are converted into a credit memo.
  • Back Order – Some of the items are pending in the credit order.
  • Closed – All of the items in the credit order are converted into a credit memo.

In addition, the browser has the following options:

  • Received/Open Credit Order
  • Show Attached Serials (barcodes)
  • Serial Number History
