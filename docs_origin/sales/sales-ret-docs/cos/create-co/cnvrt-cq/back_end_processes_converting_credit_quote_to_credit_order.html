Back End Processes - Converting a Credit Quote to a Credit Order

When a credit quote is converted to a credit order, the following back-end processes occur:

  • The credit quote gets converted to an open credit order. The credit quote is no longer visible in the Credit Quotes browser.
  • The sequence number of the credit order is generated by the system.

: Document Information - Document Number

  • The quantity ‘on credit’ for each line item increases by the quantity requested in the credit order.
