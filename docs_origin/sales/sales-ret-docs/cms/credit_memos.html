Credit Memos

A credit memo (path: Invoicing > Credit Memos> Options > Edit > In New Window) is created to account for the items or services returned. The credit memo in Everest, thus, serves a dual purpose:

  • Recording return of items - The credit memo acts a ‘Goods Received Note’ as items are taken into inventory only after their return is recorded on the credit memo.
  • Accounting for Sales returns - The credit memo is the base document for accounting the amount of sales returns.

A credit memo is required to do the following:

  • Enter returns against a credit order.
  • Record the returned items and quantities for inventory purpose.
  • Automatically account for the loss or profit on return.
