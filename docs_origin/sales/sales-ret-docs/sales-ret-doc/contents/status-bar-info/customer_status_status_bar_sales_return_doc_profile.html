Customer Status

The status of the customer is displayed in this section. Place your cursor over the icon in the status bar of the sales return document to view the status of the customer. The customer status indicators are as follows:

Status Displayed Icon Description
Customer On Hold The customer has been put on credit hold.
Over Credit Limit (Has prior Purchases)
The balance due from the customer exceeds the credit limit specified in the Credit Limit field in the Payment Information tab of the Customer profile.
Over Credit Limit (No prior Purchases) The balance due from the customer exceeds the credit limit specified in the Credit Limit field in the Payment Information tab of the Customer profile.


A sales invoice has NOT been created for the customer previously.
Normal with prior Purchase(s) The balance due from the customer does not exceed the credit limit specified in the Credit
Limit field in the Payment Information tab of the Customer profile.


A sales invoice HAS been created for the customer.
Normal without prior Purchase The balance due from the customer does not exceed the credit limit specified in the Credit Limit field in the Payment Information tab of the Customer profile.


A sales invoice has NOT been created for the customer.
Past Due The customer has not paid the balance due by him within the due date specified in the sales document. There are amounts pending on sales invoices that are due on a date before the database server date.
