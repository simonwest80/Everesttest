Create a Credit Quote

To create a credit quote, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Invoicing > Credit Quotes. You will see the Credit Quotes browser.
  • From the Credit Quotes browser, select Options > New > In New Window. You will see the Credit Quote profile.

You can also create Credit Quote profiles from the following:

  • The Credit Quotes browser by selecting Options > New.
  • An existing Credit Quote profile by selecting Options > New.
