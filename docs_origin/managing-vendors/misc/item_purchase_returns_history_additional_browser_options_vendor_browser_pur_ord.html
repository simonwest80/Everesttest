Item Purchase/Returns - Purchase Orders

Use the Item Purchase/Returns option (path: Vendors browser > Options > History > Item Purchase/Returns > Purchase Orders) to view the purchase orders created for the selected vendor in the Item Purchase History browser. The items’ purchase history is useful to obtain the total quantity of items ordered.

By default, this browser is grouped by the items contained in the document. The Item Purchase History browser lists details such as the document number, description, the item quantity, the price, document date, amount of discount, tax code, asset and expense account.
