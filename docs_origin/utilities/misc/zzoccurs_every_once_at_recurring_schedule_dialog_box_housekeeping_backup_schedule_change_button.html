Select the Occurs every option if the data must be backed-up once every minute/hour on the selected day. You can also specify the start and end times during which the data must be backed-up.
If you want to backup data every two hours, from 11.00 a.m. to 10 p.m. on the selected day, you must enter 2 in the Occurs every < > and select minutes or hours, and specify 11.00 a.m. in the Starting at field and 10 p.m. in the Ending at field.
