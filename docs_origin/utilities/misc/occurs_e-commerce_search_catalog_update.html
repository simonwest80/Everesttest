Occurs

This section allows you to specify if the activity has to be executed on a daily, weekly or monthly basis.

Daily
This option enables the Every <number of> Day(s) field. Specify the frequency of the schedule in this field.
To execute the activity once in every 5 days, enter 5 in this field.

You can specify a maximum of 100 days.

Weekly
This option enables the Every <number of> Week(s) on field and the weekday check boxes. Select the required fields based on your schedule.
 To schedule an activity every alternate Friday, enter 2 in the Every <number of> Week(s) on field and select the Friday check box.
Monthly
Select this option to set a monthly schedule for executing the activity.
  • Based on the day of the month.

:  To schedule an activity on the 30th day of every third month specify ‘30’ in Day <number of> Days and ‘3’ in Every <number of> Month(s) fields.

You can specify a maximum of 100 months.

  • Based on the weekday.

:  To schedule an activity on the last Friday of every third month specify ‘Last Friday’ and 3 in Every <number of> Month(s) field.

You can specify a maximum of 100 weeks.

Never
Select this option if you do not want to schedule the activity.
