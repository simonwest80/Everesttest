The Update Price Differentials for Kits Browser

The Update Price Differentials for Kits browser (path: Utility > Update Price Differentials > Kits) displays a list of all kit updates created in Everest. The options available in the Update Price Differentials for Kits browser are detailed below.

Process
Click the Process button to update the kit price. The prices are updated as specified in the Related Items profile (path: Inventory > Items > Options > Related Items).

Options common to all browsers are also available in this browser.
