Customize Everest E-mail

You can customize menus and toolbars in Everest E-mail. You can add and remove buttons and menus on toolbars, create your own custom toolbars, hide or display toolbars, and move toolbars. You can customize the menu bar the same way you customize any built-in toolbar.

You can create a toolbar for storing unused buttons and menus, move the button or menu to this storage toolbar, and then hide the storage toolbar.
