# Current Stock Area


This parameter is used to track serialized/auto-serialized items on  the basis of the stock areas where they are currently stored.


![example.gif]({{site.wm_baseurl}}/img/example.gif) If  the items stored in a warehouse are destroyed by fire, you can track the  serial numbers of those items and mark them for write-off in your book  of accounts.


The drop-down button provided along with the **Current 
 Stock Area** field is used to view history of search parameters specified  earlier.


You can use browse button to look for stock areas.


You can either enter or look for the stock area using the browse button.  Click the **Browse** button. You will  see the **Selection** dialog box where  all the stock areas (whether **In**  type or **Out** type) are listed.  From the **Selection** dialog box,  you can choose the individual or multiple stock areas of your choice.


If you select multiple stock areas, the word ‘Multiple’ is displayed  in the field in the form of a URL link by default.
