# Discount Percent


The **Discount** **Percent**  column allows you to enter the discount available on an item as a percentage  of the price per unit of purchase measure. You can modify the discount  percentage even after saving the purchase document.


![]({{site.pp_baseurl}}/img/lens.gif) [Measure]({{site.mi_chm}}/item-profile-details/other-items-information/purchase/measure_pur.html)


When you enter the discount percentage, the discount value per unit  of purchase measure is computed automatically and displayed in the **Discount Value** column, and vice-versa.
