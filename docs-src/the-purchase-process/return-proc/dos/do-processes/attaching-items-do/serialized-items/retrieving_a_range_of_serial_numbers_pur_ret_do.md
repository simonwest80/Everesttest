# Retrieve a Range of Serial Numbers


You can attach serialized inventory only from ‘stock’ type of stock  areas to a debit order/memo.


![]({{site.pp_baseurl}}/img/steps.gif)**To retrieve a range of serial numbers into a debit order/memo, 
 do the following:**

- From the **Debit** **Order**/**Debit** **Memo**  profile, select **Options** > **Attach**/**Retrieve****Serial** **#** > **Serial** **# 
 Range**. You will see the **Retrieve 
 Serial Numbers** dialog box.
- Specify the  starting and ending serial numbers to be attached to the order/memo in  the **Serial # From** and **Serial 
 # To** fields and click **Apply**.
- The serial  numbers are attached to the line item.



You can scan the serial numbers into the  system using a bar code scanner.


Select the ![]({{site.pp_baseurl}}/img/pur_history_button.gif) button to view details of the serial numbers  attached to the item.


![]({{site.pp_baseurl}}/img/lens.gif) [History]({{site.pp_baseurl}}/return-proc/dos/do-processes/attaching-items-do/serialized-items/retrieving_individual_serial_numbers_pur_ret_do.html#retrieve_serial_no_db_history)


When you attach a serial number that does not exist on the document,  **Everest** asks you to confirm that  you want to attach the item. Click **Yes**  or **No** as per your requirement.


{:.note}
The prompt will be displayed only if the **Prompt when trying to retrieve and serialize 
 an item that is not present on a document** option (path: **File**  > **Setup** > **Preferences**  > **Flow** **Control**  > **Documents** > **Others 
 - Others 2** tab) is selected.
