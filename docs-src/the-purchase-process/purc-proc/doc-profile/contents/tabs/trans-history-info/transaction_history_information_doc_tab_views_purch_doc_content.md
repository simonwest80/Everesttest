# Transaction History Information


The **History** tab in a purchase  document records the entire history of a transaction from its inception  (purchase quote) to the payment on the final document.


{:.note}
This tab is displayed only when you save the  document.


The **History** tab displays the  following information:

- Details of purchase  quote creation such as date and time of creation, amount of document and  the user who created the quote.
- Details of purchase  order creation.
- Details of purchase  receipt creation.
- Details of purchase  invoice creation.
- Details of payments  or deposits made and refunded on the document such as the payment type  and reference number. Details of payments that are applied to the document  are also displayed. Credit card numbers are masked using the format XXXXXXXXXXXX<last  four digits of the card> when the **Enable 
 Extended Mode** check box is cleared (path: **File 
 &gt; Setup &gt; Preferences &gt; Workstation Settings &gt; Company Settings**  tab).
- Details of modifications  made to documents such as changing the status of the purchase order, changes  in the vendor contact and shipping addresses, change in the document alias,  pay terms, delivery method, location/sub-location and jurisdiction.
- Details of the  modification made to the purchase receipt or invoice columns such as **Item Cost**, **Discount 
 Value**, **Discount %**, **Tax Code**, **Job 
 Code**, and **Add-on Cost** Value.
- Displays ‘Purchase  receipt/invoice cost modified’ event with Cost Adjustment Document’s number  and General Journal # in the **Reference**  column.



{:.note}
To maintain transaction history for documents, you must  select the option **Maintain** **Transaction** **History****for** **Documents**  (path: **File** > **Setup**  > **Preferences** > **Flow****Control** > **Documents**  > **Miscellaneous** > **Others** > **Others1**  tab).


You can also set an option to **Copy the transaction history attached to a document to its corresponding 
 intermediary or final document** check box (path: **File 
 &gt; Setup &gt; Preferences &gt;Flow Control &gt; Documents &gt; Miscellaneous 
 &gt; Others &gt; Others 1** tab). If you select this option, the  transaction history of a quote, order, or receipt  is  copied to the corresponding documents when it is converted to a order,  receipt, or invoice.
