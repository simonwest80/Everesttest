# SUTA Payable Account and Vendor


**SUTA Payable Account and Location/Sub-Location**
: This is the liability account to which the SUTA  taxes withheld from employees are credited.


**SUTA Payable Vendor**
: This is the vendor to whom the SUTA taxes withheld  are paid. You have to identify the vendor code for the State Government  or its Revenue Department.
