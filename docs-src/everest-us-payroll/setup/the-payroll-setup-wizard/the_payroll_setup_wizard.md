# The Payroll Setup Wizard


The **Payroll Setup Wizard** facilitates  the setting up of your company for the US payroll. The wizard takes you  through all the steps required to be set up before you start processing  payroll for your employees. Since each of the options accessed through  the wizard is accessible from the menu options for US Payroll, the **Payroll Setup** **Wizard**  is not explained in detail.


The wizard is divided into the left and right panels. The left panel  lists the various sections/steps in the wizard. The steps related to a  particular function are grouped under that function.


{:.example}
Steps like FUTA rates, Federal accounts, Local  taxes are grouped under the section **Taxes**  on the left panel of the wizard.


The right panel displays the details of a step selected from the left  panel. When you finish specifying the details for a section, a red check  mark appears beside the section name on the left panel.


You can navigate through the wizard by clicking the **Next**  button or double-clicking a section on the left panel.
