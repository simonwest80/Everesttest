---
title: Entry date
---


This field allows you to specify the entry date to search for account  activity based on the journal entry date. The journal entries could be  system or manual journals or both.


![]({{site.acc_baseurl}}/img/example.gif) Â If  you want to search for accounts receivable activity of sales made on 30  January, select the date in this field and the account name "accounts  receivable" in the **Account Name**  field of the **Quick Find** tab. The  resulting account activity browser will display all the transactions in  accounts receivable on 30 January.
