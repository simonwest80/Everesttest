# The Posted Payment Journals Browser


When the **Posted Journals** option is selected, the **Posted Payment Journals** browser is displayed. The **Transactions** menu options in the **Posted Payment Journals** browser (path: **Posted** **Payment Journals** browser **&gt; Options &gt; Transactions**) allow you to view details of transactions in the journal or a summary of the accounts debited or credited in the journal.


The **Transactions** menu has the following sub menu options:

- [General Ledger (Detail)]({{site.acc_baseurl}}/misc/general_ledger_detail_posted_payment_journals_browser_options.html)
- [General Ledger (Summary)]({{site.acc_baseurl}}/misc/general_ledger_summary_posted_payment_journal_browser_options.html)
- [Payment Method (Summary)]({{site.acc_baseurl}}/misc/payment_method_summary_posted_payment_journals_browser_option.html)



The options common to all browsers in **Everest** are also available in this browser.
