---
title: Select the method of payment from the list of payment methods associated with the chosen bank account
---


Specify the method of receiving payment from the customer such as check  or cash. The drop-down list displays all payment methods that are used  for collections.


If you choose to enter the receipts in an existing receipt journal (in  the previous section of the wizard), only payment methods associated to  the bank account of the selected journal are available for selection.
