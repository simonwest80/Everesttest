# The Posted Payment Journal Summary Browser


The **Posted** **Payment Journal Summary** browser is displayed when **Options** > **Transactions** > **General Ledger** (**Summary**) **** is selected in the **Posted Payment Journals** browser.


This browser lists all the accounts debited or credited in the journal, and the net amount of debit and credit for the account.


The options common to all browsers in **Everest** are also available in this browser.
