Specify whether you want to print either the **Last 
 Activity** report or **Without Activity**  report.

- **Last 
 Activity** - Lists those customers for whom documents, tracking details  or tasks are created/modified during the given period. The report also  displays the customerâ€™s code, name, address, customer created date, sales  representative, last activity date and type of activity under the **Last Activity** column.
- **Without
