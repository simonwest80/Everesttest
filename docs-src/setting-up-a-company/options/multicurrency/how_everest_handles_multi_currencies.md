# How Everest Handles Multicurrencies


**Everest** multicurrency supports  multicurrency transactions in sales, purchase and general ledger. The  multicurrency function also complies with statement 52 of the Federal  Accounting Standards Board. This statement lays down the guidelines to  be followed when accounting for foreign currency transactions, gains and  losses, and re-measurement of assets and liabilities. **Everest**  multicurrency is also Euro compliant.
