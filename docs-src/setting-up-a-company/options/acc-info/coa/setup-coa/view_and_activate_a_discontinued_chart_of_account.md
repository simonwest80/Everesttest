# View and Activate a Discontinued Chart of Account


{:.steps}
To view and activate a discontinued chart of account, do  the following:

- From the main  menu bar, select **Accounting &gt; Chart 
 of Accounts**. You will see the **Chart 
 of Accounts** browser.
- From the **Chart of Accounts** browser, select **Options** > **View Discontinued**. You will see the discontinued record in the  **Discontinued Chart of Accounts** browser.
- F<font color="#000000" class="hcp2">rom the </font>**Discontinued**<font color="#000000" class="hcp2"> </font>**Chart of Accounts** <font color="#000000" class="hcp2">browser, 
 highlight the record.</font>
- <font color="#000000" class="hcp2">Select </font>**Options &gt; Activate**<font color="#000000" class="hcp2">. 
 You will be prompted for confirmation.</font>
- <font color="#000000" class="hcp2">Click </font>**OK**<font color="#000000" class="hcp2">. The activated record 
 will be displayed in the </font>**Chart 
 of Accounts** <font color="#000000" class="hcp2">browser. </font>
