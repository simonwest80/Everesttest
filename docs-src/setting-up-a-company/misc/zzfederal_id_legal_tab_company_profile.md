---
title: Federal ID
---


Enter the Federal ID of the company. The Federal ID Number is the identification  number assigned to companies by the United States Government.


{:.example}
Suppose your company is headquartered in Virginia, and has branches  at two other locations - Texas and California.


You need to enter the Federal ID in the **Legal**  tab of the **Company** profile to  report tax Â for  Virginia. Further, the Federal IDs given by the respective governments  for carrying out business in the California and Texas branches need to  be entered in the **Location** profile.
