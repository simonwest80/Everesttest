# Reports


**Everest** provides you the utility  to generate reports based on the specified parameters. The time displayed  in the Time Clock reports is the time recorded in the original transaction.  This time does not change when viewed with other time zone or daylight  saving time settings.


Here is a list of the reports generated for [Time  Clock]({{site.rpt_chm}}/everest-reports/time-clock/time_clock.html):

- Attendance
- Employee Log
- Employee Log Details
- Leave
- Leave Request Details
- Leave Summary
- Visitors' Log
- Monthly Attendance
