---
title: Allow Freight Distribution
---


Select this check box to distribute the freight charge among the items you purchase according to the distribution methods specified in the **Freight Distribution** dialog box. If the option **Allow Freight Distribution** is unchecked the item will not be available for the distribution of freight charges.
