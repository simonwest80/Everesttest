# View Committed Serial Numbers - Sales Orders


The serial numbers of items committed on a sales order can be viewed  individually or for all the items in the document.


{:.steps}
To view the serial numbers committed on an  item in a sales order, do the following:

- From the **Sales Order** profile, click on the line  item.
- Select **Options &gt; Item &gt; Show Serial #**.  You will see the [**Serial 
 Numbers** browser]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/sales-docs/docs-profile/options/items/serial_browser.html) for the selected item.



{:.steps}
To view the serial numbers of all items that  have been committed on a sales order, do the following:

- From the **Sales Order** profile, select **Options 
 &gt; All Items &gt; Show All Serial #**. You will see the [**Serial # for All Items** browser]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/misc/serial_for_all_items_browser.html).
