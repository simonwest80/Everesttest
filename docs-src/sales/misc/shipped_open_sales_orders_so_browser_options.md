# Shipped/Open Sales Orders


By default, the **Sales Orders** browser  displays open sales orders. You can also view the shipped (closed) sales  orders by selecting **Options &gt; Shipped 
 Sales Orders**.


![]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/img/lens.gif) [Document  Status]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/sales-docs/docs-profile/contents/tab-details/details/other/document_status_document_information_sales_content.html)


From the **Shipped Sales Orders** browser,  select **Options &gt; Open Sales Orders** to view the **Sales Orders** browser  containing open sales orders.
