# Enter Sales Quote Information


Information on a sales quote can be entered in two ways:

- Entering item  details for the new quote.

- Retrieving  item information from another sales quote, sales order, sales invoice  or credit memo and making the required changes.



**![]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/img/lens.gif) [Retrieve  Items from a Sales Document]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/misc/retrieve_items_from_a_sales_document.html)**
