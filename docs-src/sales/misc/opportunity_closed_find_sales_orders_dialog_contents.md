# Opportunity Closed


This field allows you to search for sales orders using the opportunity  status as defined in the sales opportunity associated with the order.


![]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/img/lens.gif) [Sales  Opportunity]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/opportunity-management/sales_opportunities_an_introduction.html)


The following options are available:

- **All**  - All sales orders are searched (irrespective of whether the opportunity  is closed or not).
- **Yes**  - Sales orders for which the sales opportunity has been closed are searched.
- **No**  - Sales orders for which sales opportunity has not been closed are searched.
