---
title: Processed
---


This option allows you to search for invoicing documents based on the  **Processed** check box in the documents.  The options available are:

- **All**  - All documents are searched, irrespective of whether the document is  marked as processed or not.
- **Yes**  - Only documents marked as processed are searched.
- **No**  - Documents that are not marked as processed are searched.
