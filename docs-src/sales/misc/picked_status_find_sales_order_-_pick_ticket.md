# Picked Status 


This option allows you to filter sales orders based on whether they  have been picked or not.

- **Not 
 Picked** - The search yields sales orders on which items have not  been picked.
- **Partially 
 Picked** - The search yields sales orders on which items have been  partially picked.
- **Fully 
 Picked** - The search yields sales orders where all the items have  been picked.
