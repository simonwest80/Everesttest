---
title: Already Paid
---


The amount already paid/received is displayed. In case of orders, the  amount already paid is the deposit received against the order. Payments/Receipts  whose journal have or have not been posted are included. This is a display  field.


Click the **Already Paid** label  to view the **Payment History**.
