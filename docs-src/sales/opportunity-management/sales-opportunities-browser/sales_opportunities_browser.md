# The Sales Opportunities Browser


The **Sales Opportunities** browser  (path: **Invoicing &gt; Sales Opportunities**)  lists all opportunities that are not converted to sales quotes.


The options (path: **Sales Opportunities** browser > **Options**) available  in the **Sales Opportunities** browser  are:

- [Convert  to Quote]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/opportunity-management/sales-opportunities-browser/convert_to_quote.html)
- [Sales  Funnel]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/opportunity-management/sales-opportunities-browser/sales_funnel_sales_opp_browser_options.html)
- [History]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/opportunity-management/sales-opportunities-browser/history_sales_opp_browser_option.html)
- [Purge]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/misc/purge_sales_opp_brsr_options.html)



Options common to all browsers are also available in this browser.
