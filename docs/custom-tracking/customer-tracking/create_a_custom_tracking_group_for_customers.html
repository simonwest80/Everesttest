Create a Custom Tracking Group for Customers

To create a custom tracking group for customers, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Invoicing > Custom Tracking > Customer. You will see the Custom TrackingSetup - Customer browser.
  • From the Custom TrackingSetup - Customer browser, select Options > New > Group > In New Window. You will see the Custom Tracking Group under category profile. **

 You can also create a custom tracking group for customers from an existing Custom Tracking Group - Customers profile - Select Options > New.
