Create a Custom Tracking Group for Sales Documents

To create a custom tracking group for sales documents, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Invoicing > Custom Tracking > Documents. You will see the Custom Tracking Setup - Documents browser.
  • Highlight the tracking category, and from the Custom Tracking Setup - Documents browser, select Options > New > Group > In New Window. You will see the Document - Custom Tracking Group under category profile.

 You can also create a custom-tracking group for sales documents from an existing Document - Custom Tracking Group profile - Select Options > New.

The Custom Tracking Group profile for sales documents consists of the following tabs:

  • General
  • Available
  • View Only
