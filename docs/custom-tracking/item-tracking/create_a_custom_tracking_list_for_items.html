Create a Custom Tracking List for Items

The Item Selection option (path: Inventory > Custom Tracking > Item Selection) generates a list of all items tagged to the tracking selection you have selected in the Custom Tracking List dialog box.

To create a custom tracking list for items, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Inventory > Custom Tracking > Item Selection. You will see the Custom Tracking List dialog box.
  • After specifying the selections, click OK. You will see the Selection criteria for item list dialog box.
  • Specify the details and click OK. You will see the Tracking Item Selections browser.
