Availability Details

The Available tab of the Custom Tracking Group profile allows you to define the purchase documents on which the custom tracking group being defined is available.

Purchase/Debit
These options allow you to specify the documents to which the tracking group is available.

 The tracking group ‘Purchase Quote Approval’ may be applicable only to purchase quotes whereas the tracking group ‘Shipment Status’ will be applicable to sales orders and purchase orders.

 The Sales, Credit and Assembly sections do not pertain to Purchase Documents.
