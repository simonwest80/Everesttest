Selection Break-up

The Selection break-up option in the Custom Tracking Setup browser provides a percentage breakup of tracking selections made for a tracking group in the specified period.

 If the customer tracking group ‘How did you hear about us?’ has been tagged with the selection ‘Web’ 10 times, the selection ‘Newspaper’ 15 times and the selection ‘TV’ 5 times, the percentage breakup of each choice is calculated by dividing the number for each selection by the total number for all selections. The percentage of ‘Web’ would be 10/ 30, of ‘Newspaper’ 15/30 and of ‘TV’ 5/30.

 The selection breakup is calculated based on the total number of tracking choices made and not the total number of records tracked. If a tracking group allows multiple selections, the total number of selections will be all selections made for each record.

 In the above example, if the tracking group ‘How did you hear about us?’ allows multiple selections if a customer has been tagged to all choices, the total selections would include 3 tags for that customer.
