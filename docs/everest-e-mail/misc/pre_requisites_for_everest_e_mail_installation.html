Pre-requisites for Everest E-Mail Installation

Everest E-Mail requires at least 50MB of free space on the drive where Everest is installed. You also need to install MAPI compliant mail servers for sending and receiving mails, if you are using MAPI account.

To activate

Everest E-Mail, you need to do the following:

Register your company by providing a valid Serial Number, Registration Code and Validation Code in the Company Setup profile. Enter a valid

Activation key for the option E-mail in the Activation window for the registered company.

After you have installed Everest E-Mail, set up POP3 and/or MAPI accounts for sending and retrieving mails. Everest E-Mail will scan your MAPI and POP3 accounts at a frequency you define. When a new message is found, it is marked read on your server and loaded on to Everest E-Mail as new e-mail.
