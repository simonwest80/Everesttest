New Account Wizard - Exclude Address

If you specify the text in the “Exclude addresses” ** fields, **Everest checks for the same in the To, Cc or Bcc fields (for outbound mails) and the From field (for inbound mails). All the addresses containing the specified text are not linked to the My E-mail and All E-mail browsers.

In other words, if you exclude e-mail addresses or names in the exclude section, mails will still be sent or received but classification of the mails based on customer, vendor or user will not be available. The Type column in the Everest E-Mail window will be blank and the mails will not be listed in the My E-mail and All E-mail browsers.

However, if you do not add any text in the exclude list, the e-mails sent or received will be listed in the respective browsers (Customers/Vendors/ Users) under Options > E-mail > My E-mail/All E-mail.

You have specified a customer with an e-mail address andy@accel.com in the exclude list for outbound e-mail. When you send an e-mail to andy@accel.com, the e-mail is delivered but is not displayed in the My E-mail/ All E-mail browser for that customer (path: Customers browser > Options > E-mail > My E-mail/All E-mail). The Type column in the Everest E-mail window is blank.
