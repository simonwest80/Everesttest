Discontinue/Reactivate an Item

Everest allows you to temporarily discontinue records of items. When a record is discontinued, it is not deleted from the database; it is deactivated. A discontinued record can be made available for selection by activating it again.

You can discontinue all item types (regular, item kit and assembly) created in **Everest. You can also discontinue an item that has stock and view its stock from the Item Stock Status browser.**
