Auto-serialized

Auto-serialized items are those for which serial numbers are automatically generated and assigned by Everest at the time of purchase. Since you do not want to manually track each item, you would not affix serial number labels to it. However, you can scan and assign the manufacturer’s serial number to the item.

At the time of sale, Everest automatically selects the quantity of the item from the default stock area/bin specified. To automate this selection, select None in the Prompt method for selecting a stock bin when auto-serializing sales document field of the Flow Control Setup dialog box (path: File > Setup > Preferences > FlowControl > Documents > Prompts > SalesDocument > Sales Document 1 tab).

Flow Control - Documents - Prompts for Sales Documents

Set up an item as auto-serialized if:

  • You want to track the purchased items purchased by their manufacturer’s serial numbers instead of the serial numbers assigned to them. You can attach the manufacturer’s serial number to the item and let Everest automatically generate a serial number.

Dealers in computer hardware may not want to individually assign serial numbers to the pointing device (mouse), but may still want to track their manufacturer’s serial numbers.

  • You want to track the manufacturer’s serial numbers of defective items that are shipped to customers, because only customers can detect such items

A dealer in computers may want to track the manufacturer’s serial numbers of the keyboards shipped to customers as this will help him return the defectives if any, to the right vendor.
