Inventory Type

An item’s inventory type determines:

  • Whether the stock of the item is maintained and tracked.
  • How the item movements are tracked - by lots or serial numbers.
  • Costing of the item.
  • Accounting for expenses on the item.

Items are broadly classified into Inventoried Items and Non-inventoried Items. Inventoried items are further classified into the following:

  • Serialized Items
  • Auto-serialized Items
  • Non-serialized Items
