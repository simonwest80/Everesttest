Define a Promotional Pricing Scheme

To define a promotional pricing scheme, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Inventory > Discounts > Promotional Pricing. You will see the Promotional Pricing browser.
  • From the Promotional Pricing browser, select Options > New > In New Window. You will see the Promotional Pricing dialog box.

: OR

  • From the Item profile Sales tab, click the Special Pricing button. You will see the Special Pricing dialog box Promotional Pricing tab. Select New > In New Window. You will see the Promotional Pricing dialog box.

You can also define a promotional pricing scheme from:

  • An existing Promotional Pricing dialog box - Select Options > New.
