Include in Freight

Use this option to indicate whether the weight of this item is to be included when estimating freight on a sales document in which the item is included.

You can enter the weight for computation of freight on sales in the Weight field of the Item Freight dialog box. Double-click the Freight field in the Cost Information section grid. Freight is computed on sales only for items that have this option selected.
