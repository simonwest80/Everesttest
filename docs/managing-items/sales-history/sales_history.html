Sales History

Everest allows you to view the sales details of all the items for which final documents have been created. This data enables the sales department in negotiating a better sales price. You can view the sales history from the Sales History browser.

To access the Item Sales History browser, do the following:

  • From the main menu, select Inventory > Sales History. You will see the Sales History dialog box.
  • After specifying the details, click OK. The Item Sales History browser is displayed.

This browser displays all the final sales documents that meet the criteria specified in the Sales History dialog box.
