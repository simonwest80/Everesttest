Stock Chart

Use this option (Path: Item Browser > Options > Stock Chart) to view a graphical representation of items’ stock status. This chart details total stock, available stock, reserved stock, stock on debit, stock on order and stock on credit.

All the standard chart toolbar options are available in the Stock Chart. All quantities are shown in the stock measure.
