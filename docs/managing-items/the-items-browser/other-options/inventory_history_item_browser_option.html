Inventory History

Use the Inventory History option to view the stock history of an inventoried item. Use this option to:

  • View a chronological record of all transactions that affect the item’s inventory quantity or value.
  • View the impact of each transaction on the item’s stock and value.

Transactions for the item that are carried out through Purchase Receipts and Invoices, Debit Memos, Credit Memos, Sales Invoices and Inventory Adjustment Documents are recorded in the item’s history. Stock movements and transactions that do not affect the item’s inventory are not found in the inventory history.

Stock transfers, quantity committed on purchase orders and sales orders are not recorded in the item’s history.

This option is disabled for non-inventoried items.

When you access the Inventory History option, you will see the Inventory History browser for the selected item.
