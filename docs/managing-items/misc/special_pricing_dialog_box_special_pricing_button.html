Special Pricing Dialog Box

This dialog box is displayed when you select the Special Pricing button in the Sales tab of a saved Item profile. You can set up the following pricing schemes for the item from this dialog box:

  • Promotional Pricing - Select Options > New > In New Window to create a pricing scheme in the Promotional Pricing dialog box.
  • Special Pricing - Select Options > New > In New Window to create a pricing scheme in the Special Pricing dialog box.
  • Quantity Pricing - Enter the Quantity Pricing schemes in this tab.
  • Quantity Discount - Select Options > New > In New Window to create a pricing scheme in the Quantity Discount dialog box.

Special pricing will not apply any rules or discount rates to items that have their selling price defined as 0 in the Item profile, even after a price is entered in the Point of Sale document.  Such items will come into documents with price 0and any discount rates will have to be defined manually.
