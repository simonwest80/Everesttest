Stock Status Details Section

The lower section of the Item Stock Status dialog box displays a detailed break up of the stock status based on the stock areas. The columns in this section are detailed below:

Column This Column Displays
Stock Area The name of the stock area in which the item is located.
Area Description The description of the stock area.
Area Type The type of stock area (Stock or Out).


Type of Stock Area
Inventory Type The type of stock (Normal, In Process,
Exchange, Scrap).


Types of Stock
Stock The quantity of the item in that
stock area, if the stock area is of type 'Stock'.
Out The quantity of the item that has
been shipped to customers and returned to vendors if the stock area is
of type 'Out'. When sales invoices or debit memos are created in Everest,
items shipped and returned are moved to an 'Out' type stock area.
Free Stock The quantity of the item in that
stock area that has not been committed for shipment against sales orders
or debit orders. Free Stock = Quantity in Stock column - Quantity on Reserve
committed for the stock area - Quantity in
On Debit committed for that stock area.
Free Out The quantity of the item that has
not been committed to a purchase order or a credit order. Free Out Stock
= Quantity in Out column - Quantity on Order - Quantity on Credit.
On Order


(Committed)		 The quantity in the stock area that
has been committed against a purchase order.
On Reserve


(Committed)		 The quantity in the stock area that
has been committed for shipment against a sales order.
On Debit


(Committed)		 The quantity in the stock area that
has been committed for shipment against a debit order.
On Credit


(Committed)		 The quantity in the stock area that
has been committed to credit orders.
Lot Number Columns If the item is tracked for lots, a break up of the quantities in that stock area is displayed for each Lot Number. All columns in the stock details section are once again displayed for each lot number.
Underpicking The quantity for which a pick ticket has been created but the quantity has not yet been picked. This column is displayed only if a quantity is marked for picking. The stock under picking does not affect the display of any other quantity in the Item
Stock Status dialog box.
