Activate a Discontinued Customer/Vendor Category

To activate a discontinued customer/vendor category, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select CRM > Business Activity Monitoring > Setup > Customer/Vendor Category. You will see the Customer/Vendor Category browser.
  • From the Customer/Vendor Category browser, select Options > View Discontinued. You will see the Discontinued Customer/Vendor Category browser displaying the discontinued records.
  • From the browser, highlight the category you want to activate.
  • Select Options > Activate. You will be prompted for confirmation.
  • Click OK. The activated category will be displayed in the Customer/Vendor Category browser.
