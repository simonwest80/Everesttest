E-mail Accounts Browser

The E-mail Accounts browser (path: CRM > Business Activity Monitoring > Setup > E-mail Accounts) lists all the e-mail accounts that have been created. The browser displays details such as the e-mail address, account name, the mail server for logging in and sending mails and the port number.

Options common to all browsers in Everest are also available in this browser.
