Modify Form W2

You can access Form W2 for modification from the following locations:

  • The Form W2 - Employee List dialog box - Select the year and the employee for whom you want to edit the Form W2, and click the W2 button.
  • The Employees browser - Highlight the employee and select Options > Form W2.

You can make adjustments to the values in boxes 1 to 15.
