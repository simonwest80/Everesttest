: If you were required to pay state unemployment tax in more than one state, check the box in Line 1b. Then fill out Part 1 of Schedule A (Form 940) by clicking the Schedule A button and attach it to your Form 940.

  • : The Schedule A tab will be enabled only if you have selected this option.
  • : If you have selected 1b and do not select at least one state in Schedule A, you will see the error message.
  • : If you were checked No option from Line A of old Form 940, then the Line 1b will be automatically checked in the new Form 940.
