View a Payment Journal

The payment journals can be viewed from the Unposted Payment Journals browser.

To view a payment journal, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Accounting > Account Payable > Payment Journal. You will see the Unposted Payment Journals browser listing all the payment journals which are not posted.
  • Double-click the required record to view its details.

Posted payment journals can also be viewed from the Unposted Payment Journals browser by selecting Options > Posted Journals.
