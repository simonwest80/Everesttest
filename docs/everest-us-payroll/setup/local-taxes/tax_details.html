Tax Details

The local tax rates and calculation method have to be specified for each calendar year. Tax details need not be specified for the 7 default taxes. US Payroll calculates these taxes from its tax tables. Different tax rates and methods can be specified for employee withholdings and for employer payments.

Details of this tab have been explained in the following sections;
