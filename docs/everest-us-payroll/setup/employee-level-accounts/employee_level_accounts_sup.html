Employee Level Accounts

Accounts for payroll items, federal, state and local taxes for the employee are specified in the Employee Account Setup dialog box. They override the company level accounts specified in the Federal Account Setup, State Account and Local Tax dialog boxes.

If accounts are specified for an employee in the Employee Account Setup dialog box, ** US Payroll uses them when accounting for the employee’s payroll transaction in the payroll journal.
