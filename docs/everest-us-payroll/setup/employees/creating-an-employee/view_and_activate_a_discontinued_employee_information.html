View and Activate a Discontinued Employee

You can make a discontinued record ** available for selection by activating it again.

To view and activate a discontinued employee, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Payroll > Employees > Employee Data. You will see the Employees browser.
  • From the Employees browser, select Options > View Discontinued. You will see the Discontinued Employees browser listing all the discontinued records.
  • Highlight the record you want to activate.
  • From the Discontinued Employees browser, select Options > Activate. You will see the Confirm window.
  • Click OK to activate. The activated record will be displayed in the Employees browser.
