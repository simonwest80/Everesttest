Modify W2 Tracking

You can modify W2 Tracking by assigning payroll items to the following:

  • Social security tips
  • Allocated tips
  • Dependent care benefits
  • Nonqualified plans
  • Box 12

You can access the Employee Form W2 Tracking by selecting Payroll > Setup > W2 Tracking.
