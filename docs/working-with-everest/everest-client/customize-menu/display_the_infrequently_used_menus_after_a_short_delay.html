Display Infrequently Used Menus After a Short Delay

Additionally, you can customize the display of submenus, such that the infrequently used commands are seen a short time after the regularly used options are displayed. They can also be displayed by clicking on the down arrow of the drop-down list.

To display infrequently used menus after a short delay, do the following:

  • Ensure that the Enhanced or Flat option has been selected as the Bar Style in the Workstation Settings dialog box (path: File > Setup > Preferences > Workstation Settings > General tab).
  • Right click on any toolbar and select Customize. You will see the Customize dialog box.
  • From the Options tab, select the Show full menus after a short delay check box. This option is visible only if enable or flat option is selected.
