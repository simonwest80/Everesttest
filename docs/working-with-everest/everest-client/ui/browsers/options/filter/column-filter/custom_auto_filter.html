Custom Auto Filter

To filter the list by two values in the same column or to apply comparison operators such as equals, greater than, or lesser than, click the arrow in the column header and then click Custom. You will see the Custom AutoFilter dialog box. Select the operators in the boxes on the left, and specify the values in the boxes on the right to select comparison operators and values.

When you apply a filter to a column, the records that meet the filter condition are displayed in the browser. These records can be further filtered based on any other column.

You can apply up to two conditions to a column.

Displaying records that meet more than one criterion:

  • From the Custom AutoFilterdialog box:
  • To match one criterion, click the comparison operator you want to use in the first box under Show rows where and then enter the value you want to match in the box immediately to the right of the comparison operator.
  • To display rows that meet two conditions, enter the comparison operator and value you want, and then click the And button. In the second comparison operator and value boxes, enter the operator and value you want.
  • To display rows that meet either one condition or another, enter the comparison operator and value you want, and then click the Or button. In the second comparison operator and value boxes, enter the operator and value you want.

While evaluating data, Everest does not distinguish between uppercase and lowercase characters.
