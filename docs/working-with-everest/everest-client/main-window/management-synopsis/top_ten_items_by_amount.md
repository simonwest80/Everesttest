# Top Ten Items by Amount


This section displays the top ten inventoried and non-inventoried items  sold in terms of amount for the current month, previous month, and the  year-to-date.


The top ten items by amount section displays two columns:


| **Items** | List of items |
| **Amount** | Total Value of Items = Total value of sales - Total value of credit  memos |



![]({{site.wwe_baseurl}}/img/lens.gif) [Login Date]({{site.wwe_baseurl}}/misc/login_date_management_synopsis.html)


**Everest** identifies the top ten  items based on the highest value of items sold and lists them in the descending  order even if the net sales amount is negative.
