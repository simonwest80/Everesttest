# Sales & Purchase


The **Sales** & **Purchase**  option displays all the sales and purchase related details, such as the  credit orders, credit memos, open orders, purchases, and debit memos.


{:.steps}
To view the sales & purchase information,  do the following:

- Click on the  **Sales** **&amp;** **Purchase** option from the **Everest**  desktop. You will see the **Sales** **&amp;** **Purchase** window.

: The **Sales &amp;****Purchase** window gives you a detailed  break-up of the following:

- [Invoicing]({{site.wwe_baseurl}}/misc/invoicing_sales___purchase_top_ten.html)
- [Purchase]({{site.wwe_baseurl}}/misc/purchases_sales___purchase_top_ten.html)
- [New]({{site.wwe_baseurl}}/misc/new_sales_purchase_top_ten.html)
