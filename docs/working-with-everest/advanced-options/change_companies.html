Change Companies

Everest allows you to easily change the company that you have logged into by using the Change Company option. This feature is useful when you have created more than one company in Everest.

To change the company that you have logged into, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select File > Change Company. You will see the Everest Logindialog box.
  • Log into the required company by selecting the company code.
