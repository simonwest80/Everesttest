# The Drop-down Calendar


The calendar enables quick selection of dates, months and years, and  when relevant, selection of time is also enabled. Date and time are displayed  in the format specified in the **Regional 
 and Language Options** of your workstation (path: **Control 
 Panel &gt; Regional and Language Options**).


The sections of the drop-down calendar are depicted in the picture below.  Click on a section, or the name of a section **** to view further details.
