Date Filter

Use the date field to effectively filter records based on a specific date or date range.

All date options (such as Today and This Week) are with respect to the login date. Everest provides a standard set of date options, which you can access by clicking the drop-down button.

The date options available are detailed below.

Options Displays a list of:
All All records (irrespective of their date of creation).
Today Records created on the login date.
This Week Records created during the current week.
This Week to Date Records created from the beginning of the week to the login.
This Month Records created during the current month.
This Month to Date Records created from the beginning of the month to the login date.
This Year Records created during the current year.
This Year to Date Records created from the beginning of the year to the login date.
Yesterday Records created on the day prior to the login date.
Last Week Records created during the previous week.
Last week to date Records created from the beginning of the previous week to the login date.
Last Month Records created during the previous month.
Last Month to Date Records created from the beginning of the previous month to the login date.
This Fiscal Quarter Records created during the current fiscal quarter (three-month period).


If your fiscal year is January to December, and your login date is 30th May 2007, the records created between 1st April 2007 and 30th June 2007 are displayed.
This Fiscal Quarter to Date Records created from the beginning of the current fiscal quarter to the login date.


If your fiscal year is January to December and your login date is 30th May 2007, the records created between 1st April 2007 and 30th May 2007 are displayed.
This Fiscal Year Records created during the current fiscal year.
This Fiscal Year to Date Records created from the beginning of the current fiscal year to the login date.
Last Fiscal Quarter Records created during the quarter prior to the one in which you have logged in.
Last Fiscal Quarter to date Records created from the beginning of the previous fiscal quarter to the login date.
Last Fiscal Year Records created during the fiscal year preceding the one in which you have logged in.
Last Fiscal Year to date Records created from the beginning of the previous fiscal year to the login date.
Next Fiscal Quarter Records created for the next fiscal quarter.


You can select this option if your login date is 21st February 2007 and you need a list of records for the quarter April to June 2007.
Next Fiscal Year Records created for the next fiscal year.


You can select this option if your login date is 1st December 2006 and you need a list of records for the fiscal year January to December 2007.
Custom Records created on the specified date or within the selected date range.
Ask Select this option to ensure that data is entered in this field every time the saved filter is run.


If you select Ask in the date parameter, this field becomes mandatory for every subsequent use of this filter.
