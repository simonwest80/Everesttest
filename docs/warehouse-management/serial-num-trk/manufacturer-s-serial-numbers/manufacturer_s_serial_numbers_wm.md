# Manufacturer's Serial Numbers


The Manufacturer’s serial number is the number assigned by the manufacturer  to the product. You may want to assign manufacturer’s serial numbers to  auto-serialized items to track them while shipping.


![Lens.gif]({{site.wm_baseurl}}/img/lens.gif) <font color="#008000" class="hcp2"><a href="{{site.mi_chm}}/item-profile-details/inventory-type/inventoried-item/auto_serialized.html">Auto-serialized</a></font>


Manufacturer's serial numbers can be assigned to a serial number from  within a purchase or sales document. Manufacturer's serial numbers can  also be assigned from the **Serial Number**  profile.
