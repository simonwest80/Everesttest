# Serial Number Tracking


Serial numbers are distinct numbers assigned to individual units of  the same item. Serial numbers help distinguish between two identical units  of the same item.


![example.gif]({{site.wm_baseurl}}/img/example.gif) Serial  numbers assigned to microchip processors distinguish one piece from the  other.
