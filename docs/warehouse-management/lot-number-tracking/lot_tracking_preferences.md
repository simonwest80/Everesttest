# Lot Tracking Preferences


Items that are mass-produced are manufactured in lots or batches. You  can assign unique lot numbers or batch numbers and affix them on all received  quantities of mass manufactured items.


![Lens.gif]({{site.wm_baseurl}}/img/lens.gif) [Lot  Tracking - Items]({{site.mi_chm}}/item-profile-details/other-items-information/purchase/lot_tracking_item_profile_content.html)


You will find lot tracking preferences for the following:

- [Items]({{site.wm_baseurl}}/misc/lot_tracking_for_items.html)
- [Auto  generated lot numbers]({{site.wm_baseurl}}/misc/auto_generated_lot_numbers.html)
