# Update Physical Stock


After retrieving items into the Stock Count document, select the **Block for Counting** or **Take 
 Snapshot to Count** option to trigger the process of counting. The  status assumed by the document is **Counting 
 in Process**. It is at this stage that you can enter, scan or import  the physical stock.
