# Stock Transfer Details


The **Stock Transfer** profile (path:  **Inventory &gt; Inventory Adjustments 
 &gt; Stock Transfer** **&gt; Options 
 &gt; New**) allows you to transfer items from one stock area to another.


{:.note}
Stock Transfer can be done from one area of type ‘STOCK’  to another area of type ‘STOCK’, or from one area of type ‘OUT’ to another  area of type ‘OUT. Cross transfers like from ‘STOCK’ area to ‘OUT’ area  or ‘OUT’ area to ‘STOCK’ area are not allowed in the document.


The items may be serialized, auto-serialized or non-serialized. Information  on this profile has been grouped in to the following sections:

- [Document  Information]({{site.wm_baseurl}}/misc/document_information_stck_trsr.html)
- [Item  Information]({{site.wm_baseurl}}/misc/item_information_stck_trsfr.html)
- [Document  tab view Details]({{site.wm_baseurl}}/misc/document_tab_view_information_stck_trsr.html)
- [Status  bar information]({{site.wm_baseurl}}/misc/status_bar_information_stck_trsr_profile.html)
