# Purchase Receipt Number, Purchase Invoice Number and Other Information


**Purchase Receipt #**
: When serialized items are received on a purchase  receipt, the serial numbers are automatically assigned to the items. The  **Items** browser shows the purchase  receipt where the serial number has been generated. You can search for  serial numbers using the purchase receipt number.
: The drop-down button beside the **Purchase 
 Receipt #** field is used to view the history of search parameters  specified earlier.
: You can search for the purchase receipt number by  clicking the browse button. The **Purchase 
 Receipts** browser is displayed, from where you can select an individual  or multiple purchase receipts for your search. When you select the purchase  receipt of your choice, the document number and name of the vendor will  appear in the **Selection** dialog  box. If you select multiple purchase receipts, the word ‘Multiple’ is  displayed in the field in the form of a URL link by default.


**Purchase Invoice #**
: When serialized items are received on a purchase  invoice, the serial numbers are automatically assigned to the items. The  **Items** browser shows the purchase  invoice where the serial number has been generated. You can search for  serial numbers using the purchase invoice number.
: The drop-down button beside the **Purchase 
 Invoice #** field is used to view the history of search parameters  specified earlier.
: You can search for the purchase invoice number by  clicking the browse button. The **Purchase 
 Invoices** browser is displayed, from where you can select an individual  or multiple purchase invoices for your search. When you select the purchase  invoice of your choice, the document number and name of the vendor will  appear in the **Selection** dialog  box. If you select multiple purchase invoices, the word ‘Multiple’ is  displayed in the field in the form of a URL link by default.


**Other Information**
: The **Serial Number**  profile contains a memo column where you can attach notes for your reference.  You can search for a serial number, using this note as a parameter. You  can enter the entire note or just the first word or letter with which  the note begins. The search will yield all serial numbers to which the  note has been attached.
