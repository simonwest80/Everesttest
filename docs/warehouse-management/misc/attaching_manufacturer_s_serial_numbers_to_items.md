# Attach a Manufacturer's Serial Number to Items


You may attach the Manufacturer's serial numbers to committed quantities  of items. You can do it in two ways:

- Entering or  scanning<font style="color: #ff0000;" color="#FF0000"> </font><font style="color: #000000;" color="#000000">the manufacturer's 
 serial number in the <span class="hcp1">Serial # for All 
 Items</span> browser.</font>
- Automatically  generate the numbers to be assigned.



{:.steps}
To attach a manufacturer’s serial number  to items from the stock transfer profile, do the following:

- From the **Stock Transfer** profile, select **Options &gt; Show All Serial #**. You  will see the **Serial # for All Items**  browser.
- From the **Serial # for All Items** browser, select  **Assign Manuf**. **Serial No** from the right-click menu.  You will see the **Assign Manuf**.  **S****erial 
 No**. dialog box.
- Enter the details.



{:.note}
If the quantity specified is more than one, the manufacturer’s  serial numbers are automatically generated and assigned to the quantity  specified.
