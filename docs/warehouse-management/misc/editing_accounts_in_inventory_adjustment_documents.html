You can edit the From Invt Acnt and To Invt Acnt columns.

It is not recommended to select a Posting Group which has an Inventory control account defined for the inventory control account column. Since this account would override the account specified in the location of the document’s department a warning message appears when you try to edit the inventory control account. This message informs you that  discrepancies may arise between the financial and inventory reports. You may choose to continue, or select another Posting Group in this field.
