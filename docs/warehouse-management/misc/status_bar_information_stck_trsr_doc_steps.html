Status Bar Information

Zoom In/Out

click the Zoom In button to view all the document details. click the Zoom Out  button to hide the lower half of the stock transfer document. Zooming out enables you to view all the items contained in the document.

Stock Status

Click the stock status icon in the status bar of the stock transfer document to view the stock status of the items in the document.

The stock status indicator denotes that the available stock for the item is greater than zero. click this icon to view the Item Stock Status dialog box that displays detailed information about the stock status of the items in the stock transfer document.
