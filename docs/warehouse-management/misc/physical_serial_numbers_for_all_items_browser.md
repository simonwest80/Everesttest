# Physical Serial Numbers for all Serial Browsers


The **Physical Serial Numbers for All 
 Items** browser displays details of the physical quantities of serialized  items. You can view the serial numbers of the items available physically,  the item code, the stock area type and the bin where the quantity is physically  available. This browser comes up when you double click in the **Physical 
 Quantity** column of the **Stock Count**  profile.
