Status Bar Information

Zoom In/Out

click the Zoom In  button to view all the document details. click the Zoom Out  button to hide the lower half of the cost adjustment document. Zooming out enables you to view all the items contained in the document.

Stock Status

Click the stock status icon in the status bar of the cost adjustment document to view the stock status of the items in the document.

The stock status indicator denotes that the available stock for the item is greater than zero. click this icon to view the Item Stock Status dialog box that displays detailed information about the stock status of the items in the inventory adjustment documents

Posted/Unposted

This section displays whether the journal pertaining to the cost adjustment has been posted. When you click in this section, the Journal Transactions browser is displayed which displays the accounting entries pertaining to the cost adjustment
