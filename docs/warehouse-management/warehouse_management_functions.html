Warehouse Management Functions

Warehouse management includes the following tasks:

  • Physical storage - It includes storing materials in appropriate places, recording their location and periodical checking of stocks.

Demarcating areas for sales shipments, vendor returns, items received on approval and storing items in appropriate places.

  • Segregating sales and purchase order quantities - Quantities attached to a sales order, and those received against a purchase order must be appropriately labeled to identify the document number to which they are attached.
  • Stock transfers - Stocks are transferred from one stock area to another or from one warehouse to the other.

: Stocks may be moved to a Packing Area prior to shipping.

  • Tracking items by serial number or lot number - The location of an item can be tracked by its serial number or lot number.

A serialized item returned by a customer must be traced to its receipt date and its vendor for returns.

  • Recording inventory adjustments - If the physical quantity of stocks differs from the stocks as per the books, inventory adjustments may be required to resolve the excess or shortfall.
