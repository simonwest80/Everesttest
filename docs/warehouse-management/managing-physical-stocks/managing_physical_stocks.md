# Managing Physical Stocks


Effective storage and retrieval of inventoried items require stock areas  and locations to be clearly defined; items must be correctly labeled and  location of items recorded accurately. You can manage physical stocks  in **Everest** in the following ways:

- [**Stock groups and areas**]({{site.wm_baseurl}}/managing-physical-stocks/stock_groups_and_stock_areas.html) - Creating  multiple levels in stock groups and stock areas helps you manage your  warehouse locations.
- [**Item labeling**]({{site.wm_baseurl}}/managing-physical-stocks/item_labeling.html) - You can print, design  and customize address, item, document and other labels from relevant browsers.
- [**Stock transfer**]({{site.wm_baseurl}}/managing-physical-stocks/stock_transfer.html) - **Everest**  records item movements through stock transfer notes, sales and purchase  documents.
