Select this check box if you want Everest to automatically compute the discount amount based on the Payment Term while making payments to vendors on a purchase invoice. The discount percentage has to be specified in the Discount (%) field in the Payment Terms profile (path: File > Setup > Accounting > Payment Terms > Edit > In New Window). The amount that is available for discount is automatically displayed in the Discount available field in the Payment dialog box. The discount amount that can be applied defaults to the discount which is available and is displayed in the Discount Applied field in the Payment dialog box if the above option is selected. You can edit this field if required.
