Select this check box to automatically apply item discounts applicable to a customer when retrieving the item in the document. This option automatically applies item discounts based on special pricing rules or quantity discounts for selected customer levels, when items are retrieved into a sales document. The discounts related to items are defined in the Sales tab of the Item profile (path: Inventory > Items > Options > Edit > In New Window).

Item discounts are set for special items like computer consumables for customers who place bulk orders.  Everest allows you to apply the special pricing allowed for the customer when such an item is pulled into a document.
