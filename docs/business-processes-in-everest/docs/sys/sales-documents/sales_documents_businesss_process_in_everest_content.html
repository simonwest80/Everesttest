Sales Documents

The sales documents record details of sales made to the customers.

The steps in the sales process in Everest are as follows:

  • Create a sales quote.
  • Create a sales order.
  • Create a sales invoice.
  • Receive customer payments.

 You can create a sales order directly without creating a sales quote, or create a sales invoice without creating a sales order.
