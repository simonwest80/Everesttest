# Items with Promotional Pricing 


Where the item selected has a promotional pricing scheme additional  quantities of the item that are on offer are added as a new line item  with the special prices.


![]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/img/example.gif) An  item can be sold with the following offer: buy X quantity for $Y  and get Z quantity  free or buy X quantity at $Y  and get Z quantity  more at a certain % off.
