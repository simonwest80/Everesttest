Ordered Quantity

Enter the ordered quantity for each combination. The item codes of combinations with quantity greater than zero are added as new line items in the document.

In case of sales quotes, the ordered quantity is displayed in the Quantity column in the Item Details Grid.

In case of sales orders, the ordered quantity is displayed in the Ordered Quantity column in the Item Details Grid.

In case of sales invoices, the ordered quantity is displayed in the Ordered column in the Item Details Grid.
