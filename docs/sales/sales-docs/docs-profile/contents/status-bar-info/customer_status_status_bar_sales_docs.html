Customer Status

Place your cursor over the icon in the status bar of the sales document to view the status of the customer. The customer status indicators are as follows:

Status Displayed Icon Description
Customer On Hold The customer has been put on credit hold.
Over Credit Limit (Has prior Purchases)
The balance due from the customer exceeds the credit limit specified in the Credit
Limit field in the Payment
Information tab of the Customer profile.


The balance specified for calculating available credit would be based on the preference made in the Customers dialog box (path: File > Setup >
Preferences > Flow Control > Customers).
Over Credit Limit (No prior Purchases) The balance due from the customer exceeds the credit limit specified in the Credit
Limit field in the Payment
Information tab of the Customer profile.


The balance specified for calculating available credit would be based on the preference made in the Customers dialog box (path: File > Setup >
Preferences > Flow Control > Customers).


A sales invoice has NOT been created for the customer previously.
Normal with prior Purchase(s) The balance due from the customer does not exceed the credit limit specified in the Credit
Limit  field in the Payment Information tab of the Customer profile.


A sales invoice HAS been created for the customer.
Normal without prior Purchase The balance due from the customer does not exceed the credit limit specified in the Credit
Limit field in the Payment
Information tab of the ** Customer profile.


A sales invoice has NOT been created for the customer.
Past Due The customer has not paid the balance by the due date specified in the sales document. There are amounts pending on sales invoices that are due on a date before the database server date.
Document is paid in Full The customer has paid the balance by the due date specified in the sales document (Or) The customer has not exceeded the credit limit i.e. not on credit hold.
