# Customer Balance


This option brings up the **Customer 
 Aging Information** browser that displays the sales or sales return  documents that have outstanding payments or deposits.


| Status Displayed | Icon | Description |
| No Balance | ![]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/img/sales_cust_no_balance_icon_status_bar.gif)<br/><br/><br/>(color: blue text) | The customer has no outstanding payments to be made  to the company. |
| Balance overdue | ![]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/img/sales_balance_over_due_icon_status_bar.gif)<br/><br/><br/>(color: red text) | The customer has a balance with at least one document  past due. The customer balance is encrypted in the icon in the format  'Bnnyyyyyynn'  where 'yyyyyy'  is the balance. The first alphabet, the first two numbers and the last  two numbers do not represent any information and are random. In the icon  shown in the column on the left, the customer's outstanding balance is  11,408.44. |
| Balance within terms specified | ![]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/img/sales_cust_balance_within_terms_specified_icon.gif)<br/><br/><br/>(color: yellow text) | The customer has a balance that is not past the due  date specified in the sales document. The customer balance is encrypted  in the icon in the format 'Bnnyyyyyynn'  where 'yyyyyy'  is the balance. The first alphabet, the first two numbers and the last  two numbers do not represent any information and are random. In the icon  shown in the column on the left, the customer's outstanding balance is  63.95. |
| Credit Balance | ![]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/img/sales_customer_credit_balance_icon_status_bar.gif)<br/><br/><br/>(balance owed to customer is prefixed with a negative  sign) | Your company owes the customer money. The balance owed  to the customer is prefixed with a negative sign. The balance is encrypted  in the icon in the format 'Bnn-yyyyyynn'  where '-yyyyyy'  is the balance. The first alphabet, the first two numbers and the last  two numbers do not represent any information and are random. In the icon  shown in the column on the left, the balance due to the customer is 5,149.05. |



{:.note}
You will see the Customer Balance indicator in the status  bar of a sales document only if you have set a flow control preference  by selecting the **Show customer balance 
 in encoded fashion** check box in the **Documents**  dialog box (path: **File &gt; Setup &gt; 
 Preferences &gt; Flow Control &gt; Documents &gt; Miscellaneous &gt; Others 
 &gt; Others 1** tab).
