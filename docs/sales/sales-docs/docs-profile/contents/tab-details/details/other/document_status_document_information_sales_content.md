# Document Status


The document status information is displayed in all sales documents.


![]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/img/sales_invoice_status_sal.jpg)


The document status information is listed below:

- The document  number with its prefix. The prefixes used are:
- SQ  - for a Sales Quote
- SO  - for a Sales Order
- SI  - for a Sales Invoice
- The status  of the documents of the type sales quotes, sales orders and sales invoices.



| **Document** | **Status** |
| Sales Quotes | A sales quote does not have any status. The sales quote number is displayed  on sales orders and sales invoices for reference. |
| Sales Orders | **Open** - Items have not been delivered  to the customer.<br/><br/><br/>**Backorder** - Items have been  partially delivered.<br/><br/><br/>**Closed** - Delivery of items is  complete or the order has been marked complete.<br/><br/><br/>![]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/img/lens.gif) [Mark  Close/Open]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/sales-docs/sales-orders/so-proc/mark-close-open/mark_a_sales_order_as_complete_open.html) |
| Sales Invoices | **Open** - Payments are due from  the customer.<br/><br/><br/>**Paid** - Payments have been fully  received. |


1. Document numbers  of the original quote or order.



| **Process** | **Number Displayed** |
| A sales quote is converted to an order | The sales quote number is displayed on the order. |
| A sales order is converted to an invoice | The sales order number is displayed on the invoice. |


- The posting  status of the sales journal, in which the accounting entry for the sales  invoice is made, is displayed on the status bar. The status displayed  is **Posted** or **Not 
 posted**.



{:.note}
Accounting entries are generated only for sales invoices.
