Generate Drop Ship Purchase Orders

Drop shipments refer to the direct shipment of items from your vendors to your customers. This method is useful for firms that do not stock inventories of the products they sell. A purchase order is placed with vendors for the products ordered by customers; the vendor directly supplies the items to the customer.

Everest provides you with a utility to create drop ship orders from sales orders (path: Sales Order profile > Options > Generate Purchase Orders > Generate Drop Ship PO).
