Find Sales Orders - Amounts

Amount
Specify the amount based on which the sales orders have to be filtered.
The amount specified represents the document amount irrespective of whether it is paid or not.
Paid Amount
Specify the amount of payment based on which the search has to be performed. This amount is specified in the Paid field of the document.
Balance
Specify the balance amount based on which the search has to be performed.
Balance is the amount that is NOT yet paid on the order. It is displayed in the Balance field of the Sales Order profile.
Taxable Amount
Specify the taxable amount based on which the search has to be performed.
Taxable amount is the total value of items on the document that are subject to tax and appears in the Taxable field of the Sales Order profile.
Tax Amount
Specify the tax amount based on which the search has to be performed.
Tax amount is the total tax chargeable on all items contained in the document and is displayed in the Tax field of the Sales Order profile.
Exempt Amount
Specify the exempt amount based on which the search has to be performed.
Exempt amount is the total value of items that are exempt from tax. It appears in the Exempt field of the Sales Order profile.
Trade Discount Amount
Specify the trade discount amount based on which the search has to be performed.
Trade discount amount is the amount of payment discount that was applied to the document and is displayed in the Trade Discount field of the Sales Invoice profile.

This search yields only sales invoices as the Trade Discount field appears only in sales invoices.

Weight
Specify the weight based on which the search has to be performed.
This refers to the total weight of the items contained in the order.
