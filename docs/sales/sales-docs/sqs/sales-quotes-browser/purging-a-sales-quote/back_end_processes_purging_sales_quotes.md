# Back End Processes - Purging a Sales Quote


The following back-end processes occur  when sales quotes are purged:

- The sales quote  ceases to be a system document, and cannot be retrieved, viewed or used  to process any other document.
- The sequence  number of the purged sales quote cannot be used again.
