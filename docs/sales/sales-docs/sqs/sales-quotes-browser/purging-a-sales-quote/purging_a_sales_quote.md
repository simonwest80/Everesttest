# Purge a Sales Quote


**You cannot cancel a sales quote; however,  you can purge it. Purging permanently deletes the sales quote from the  system.**


{:.example}
A sales quote can be purged when it expires.


**There are two ways to purge sales quotes:**

- Using the **Purge** option in the **Sales 
 Quotes** Browser.
- Using the **Purge** option in the **Utility**  menu.



{:.steps}
To purge a sales quote from the sales quote  browser, do the following:

- From the main  menu bar, select **Invoicing &gt; Sales 
 Quotes**. You will see the **Sales 
 Quotes** browser.
- Highlight the  required sales quote(s)  and select **Options &gt; Purge**.



{:.steps}
To purge sales quotes from the utility menu,  do the following:

- From the main  menu bar, select **Utility &gt; Purge &gt; 
 Sales Quotes**. You will see the **Purge 
 (Sales Quotes)** browser.
- Highlight the  required quote(s)  and select **Options &gt; Purge**.  You will see a confirmation window asking you to confirm the purge command.
- Select **OK** to purge the quote.
