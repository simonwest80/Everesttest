# Back End Processes - Converting Sales Quote to Sales Order


When a sales quote is converted to a sales order, the following back-end  processes occur:

- The sales quote  gets converted to an open sales order. The sales quote is no longer available  in the **Sales Quotes** browser.
- The sequence  number of the sales order is generated automatically by the system.

: ![]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/img/lens.gif) [Document  Information - Document Number]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/sales-docs/docs-profile/contents/doc-info/document_number_document_information_sales_content.html)

- The quantity  on reserve for each line item increases by the quantity contained in the  sales order.
- Available stock  reduces.
