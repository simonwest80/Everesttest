Convert a Sales Quote to a Sales Order

When the customer decides to place an order against an existing quote, you can convert the quote to a sales order. The sales order has an **Open status.**

Document Status

The sales quote can no longer be accessed through the **Sales Quotes browser; however, it appears as a reference number in the sales order created.**

To convert a sales quote into a sales order, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Invoicing > Sales Quote. You will see the Sales Quotes browser.
  • From the Sales Quotes browser, highlight the quote that has to be converted to a sales order.
  • Select Options > Edit > In current Window/In New Window. You will see the Sales Quote profile.
  • From the Sales Quote profile, select Process > Reserve. The sales quote is converted to a sales order.
