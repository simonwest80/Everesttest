Sales Order Processes

The processes that may be carried out from a sales order are:
  • Committing items to the order
  • Detach items from the order
  • Processing an Invoice
  • Marking the order as close/open
  • Canceling the order
  • Accepting deposits on the order
  • Making refunds to customers
  • Generate Purchase Orders
  • Blind Shipment
  • Pricing Analysis
  • Lease Plans
  • Printing
  • Applying discounts
  • Freight Estimation
  • Calculating Exchange Gain/Loss
  • Track Service Items and Repairs
  • Enter Shipment Tracking Information
  • Reduce Document Value Below Paid Amount
