Payment History

The Payment History option gives the details of payments and refunds on a document. The Payment History browser can be accessed from the sales order, sales invoice, credit orders and credit memo profiles (path: Options > Payment > Payment History).

The same information may be accessed from the sales order, sales invoice or credit memo browser by selecting Options > Payment Information.

The following details are displayed in the Payment History browser:

  • Payment date.
  • Amount paid in base and foreign currency.
  • The account code of the Customer Deposit Account for sales orders and the account code of Accounts Receivable Control Account for sales invoices. If the account is credited the amount paid is shown as a negative figure (i.e., within brackets).
  • Whether the transaction is posted.
  • The name of the cash register, if any, in which the payment is received.
  • If payment is received or refunded through a credit card, the card number, address verification details, batch processing details and the authorization responses are also displayed. Credit card numbers are masked using the format XXXXXXXXXXXX when the **Enable Extended Mode** check box is cleared (path: **File > Setup > Preferences > Workstation Settings > Company Settings** tab).
  • The number of the receipt journal that contains the transaction and the transaction number.
  • Transaction rate in the case of foreign currency payments.

Double-click the payment row to see the following payment details:

  • Payment method.
  • Reference number and reference date.
  • Amount paid in base and foreign currency.
  • Account code and description.
  • Document reference (in case of applied amounts).
  • Transaction rate in the case of foreign currency payments.

You can also view the payment details from within the sales order/invoice profile by clicking the payment history button beside the Paid field.
