Receiving Credit Card Payments

Credit card payments may be received on sales orders and sales invoices by selecting the Payment > Receive option from the document profile. Credit card payments are processed by Everest if you select the Use Payment Processor check box in the Credit Card Processing tab of the Payment Method profile (path: File > Setup > Accounting > Payment Method > Options > Edit > In New Window > Credit Card Processing tab).

To receive a credit card payment, do the following:

  • From the document profile (sales order or invoice), select Options > Payment > Receipt. You will see the Receipt dialog box.
  • Select the appropriate credit card payment method in the Payment Method field.
  • Enter the amount of receipt in the Amount field. By default, the amount to be received on the document is displayed in this field.
  • Click OK. You will see the Card Verification dialog box.
