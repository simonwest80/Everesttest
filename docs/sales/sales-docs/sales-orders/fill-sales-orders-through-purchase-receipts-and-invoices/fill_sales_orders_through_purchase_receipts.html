Fill Sales Orders Through Purchase Receipts and Invoices

The **Fill Sales Order option in purchase receipts/invoices displays a list of ‘open’ sales orders that can be populated using the items received on the Purchase Receipt/Invoice. When you select this option, a browser containing the items on the purchase receipt/invoice is displayed depending on your selections in the Parts Fulfillment dialog box. The browser also displays the sales orders that are open for those items, the quantity the order needs and the number of units that can be fulfilled using this purchase.**
