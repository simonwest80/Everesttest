Advantages - Creating a Sales Order

Although a sales invoice can be directly created when the items are issued, creating a sales order has the following advantages:

  • Materials planning - This helps to track the items on order and backorder, and the expected dates of delivery. This allows you to plan your inventory and make decisions on placing new orders.
  • Issuing items for approval - You can issue items against a sales order without your actual stock being reduced. Such stock is merely shown as ‘stock on reserve’. This helps you keep track of items issued to customers for inspection and approval.
  • Deposits from customers - You can receive deposits from customers on sales orders and thereby keep track of such orders.
  • Tracking lead-time - Creating a sales order and then issuing items against it helps to track the lead-time between the sales order date and delivery date.
  • Closing opportunities - When you convert a sales quote to an order, the opportunity connected to the sales quote is automatically marked as closed by the system.
