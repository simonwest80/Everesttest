Expiry Date

The ExpiryDate field displays the date on which a credit quote or credit order will become invalid. In case of a credit quote, it also indicates that the rates at which the returns will be accepted, are valid until the expiry date. This field also helps to filter for expired credit quotes, so that they can be purged using the Purge utility.

In case of credit orders, the expiry date may be used as the date by which the customer must confirm acceptance of the RMA.

A credit quote or order that has expired can still be converted to an order or memo.
