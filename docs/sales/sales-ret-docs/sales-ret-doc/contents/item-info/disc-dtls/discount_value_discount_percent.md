# Discount Value/Discount Percent


Discounts available on an item may be entered either as a percentage  of the price or as a value of discount per unit of purchase measure.


If you enter the discount value, the discount percent is computed automatically  and displayed in the **Discount Percent**  column. Enter the discount value in the sales decimal accuracy of the  item.


If you enter the discount percent, the discount value per unit is computed  and displayed in the **Discount Value**  column.


You can change the discount value or percent if you want to change the  discount at the time of receiving returns from customers.


The amount of discount is credited to the sales discount account.
