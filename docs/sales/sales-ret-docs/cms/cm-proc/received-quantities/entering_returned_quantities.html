Enter Returned Quantities

The procedure for entering the returned quantity for items may be categorized under the following:

  • Serialized Items - If the quantity returned is more than what is committed, or you are creating a credit memo, select Options > Attach/Retrieve Serial Number > Serial Number or Serial Number Range or Manufacturer’s Serial Number to retrieve additional serial numbers. The returned quantity can be more than the requested quantity.

Attaching Serialized items to a Credit Order/Credit Memo

Requested, Committed and Credited columns in Credit Memo

  • Non-serialized Items - Double-click in the Credited column of a line item to view the Attach Quantity profile. The Attach Quantity profile is displayed based on the preference you have set in the Location profile (Stock transfer > Miscellaneous tab). This ** profile displays a list of stock areas and the quantities attached for return. Enter the quantity returned in the required stock area.

The Attach Quantity Profile

  • Non-inventoried Items - The quantity returned can be directly entered in the Credited column for the item.

  • Auto-serialized Items - To attach serial numbers to an auto-serialized item on the credit memo, select Options > Attach/Retrieve Serial # > Serial #. Attach the serial number from an ‘Out’ type of stock area to the credit memo. When you save the credit memo, the item is transferred to a ‘Stock’ type of stock area.
