The Credit Memo Browser

The Credit Memos browser (path: Invoicing > Credit Memos) displays all the credit memos that are created.

By default the Credit Memos browser displays documents that are created in the default period specified for sales document browsers.

Filter Sales Documents based on Dates

Prompts - Sales Documents Tab

You can change the period by selecting the required option in the Dates field of the Credit Memo browser.

This browser contains the options common to all sales return document browsers and to all other browsers in Everest.

Standard Browser Options

In addition, the browser has the following options:

  • Payment information
  • Show Attached Serials (barcodes)
  • Serial Number History

Options in the View Menu

The options under the View menu allow you to view a list of standard, accounting, historical and Point of Sale type of credit memo documents, in the Credit Memos browser. You can choose the type(s) of credit memo document(s) you want to view.

The View menu in the Credit Memos browser contains the following options:

  • My Documents
  • All Documents
  • Point of Sale
  • Standard
  • Accounting
  • Historical
