# The Credit Orders Browser


The **Credit Orders** browser (path:  **Invoicing &gt; Credit Orders**)  displays all the credit orders that are created, but not converted to  credit memos.


By default the **Credit Orders** browser  displays documents that are created in the default period specified for  sales document browsers.


![]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/img/lens.gif) [Filter  Sales Documents based on Dates]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/sales-docs/browser/filtering_sales_documents_browsers_based_on_dates.html)


[Prompts  - Sales Documents Tab]({{site.bp_chm}}/flow-ctrl/ctrl/opt/prompts-tab/prompts_sales_document_tab_step_by_step_business_process_in_everest.html)


You can change the period by selecting the required option in the **Dates** field.


This browser contains the options common to all sales document browsers  and to all other documents in **Everest**.


![]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/img/lens.gif) [Sales  Return Documents - Browser Options]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/sales-ret-docs/sales-return-document-browser/sales_document_browser_options_sales_return_documents_content.html)

**Identify the Credit Order Status**


The **Status** column  in the **Credit Order** browser shows  the status of the credit order. There are three statuses:

- **Open**  – None of the items are converted into a credit memo.
- **Back 
 Order** – Some of the items are pending in the credit order.
- **Closed**  – All of the items in the credit order are converted into a credit memo.
