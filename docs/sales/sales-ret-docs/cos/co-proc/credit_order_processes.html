Credit Order Processes

The processes that may be carried out from a credit order are:

  • Attaching items to a credit order
  • Detaching items from the order
  • Generate Serial Numbers
  • Exchange or Repair of Items
  • Freight Estimation
  • Refunds to customers on a credit order
  • Collections from customers on a credit order
  • Payment History
  • Processing a Credit Memo
  • Marking the order as complete/open
  • Cancelling the order
  • Printing
  • Apply Discounts
  • Pricing Analysis
