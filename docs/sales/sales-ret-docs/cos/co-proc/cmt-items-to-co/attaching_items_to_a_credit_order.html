Attach Items to a Credit Order

You may commit inventoried items to a credit order in the following cases:

  • When you identify the items expected back from the customer and assign them to a separate area.
  • When you physically receive the items but the customer continues to retain ownership of the items.

You cannot commit items on orders belonging to discontinued customers.

The **Committed column in the Credit Order profile displays the quantity of the committed item. These  items are recorded in an Out type of stock area.**

Stock Area Types

The quantity ‘committed’ on a credit order cannot exceed the quantity ‘requested’. However, if the ‘committed’ quantity exceeds the ‘requested’ quantity, **Everest informs you of the same. You are prompted to confirm that you want to proceed. If you choose to continue, the ‘requested’ quantity gets updated to equal the ‘committed’ quantity. This is applicable to all items: serialized, non-serialized and auto-serialized.**
