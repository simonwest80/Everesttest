Auto-serialized Items

In case of an auto-serialized item, the quantity in the Committed column is suffixed with ‘a’.

You can add auto-serialized items to a credit order or memo by using the option Attach/Retrieve Serial # > Serial # or Serial # Range or Mfg. Serial from the document menu.

You can also generate serial numbers in credit order/credit memo for service type of items sent for repair, using the option Attach/Retrieve Serial # > Generate Serial #.
