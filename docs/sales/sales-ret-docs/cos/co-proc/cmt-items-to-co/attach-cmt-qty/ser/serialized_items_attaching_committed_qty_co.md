# Serialized Items


Serial numbers of the item to be attached to the credit order or memo  may be retrieved in any of the following ways:

- [One  by one]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/sales-ret-docs/cos/co-proc/cmt-items-to-co/attach-cmt-qty/ser/retrieving_individual_serial_numbers_co.html)
- [As  a range]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/sales-ret-docs/cos/co-proc/cmt-items-to-co/attach-cmt-qty/ser/retrieving_a_range_of_serial_numbers_co.html)
- [By  manufacturer's serial number]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/sales-ret-docs/cos/co-proc/cmt-items-to-co/attach-cmt-qty/ser/retrieving_manufacturer_s_serial_number_co.html)



**Y**ou can select serial numbers  from stock areas for the logged in location if you select the **Allow 
 Users to access only those stock areas in the logged in location**  check box (path: **File &gt; Set up &gt; 
 Preferences &gt; Flow Control &gt; Other Flow Control &gt; General**  tab).


{:.note}
You will be prompted with the message **Item 
 does not exist on order. Serialize?** when you attach a serial number  of an item not present on the credit order or memo. Click **Yes**  to attach the item on the order or memo.


This prompt is received if the **Prompt 
 when trying to retrieve and serialize an item that is not already present 
 on a document** option (path: **File 
 &gt; Setup &gt; Preferences &gt; Flow Control &gt; Documents &gt; Miscellaneous 
 &gt; Others &gt; Others 2** tab) is selected.

###### Adding  to Items Committed on Credit Orders 


After committing items to a Credit Order, you can commit additional  items by attaching the serial numbers of the additional quantity on the  **Credit Order** profile.
