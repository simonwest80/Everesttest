# View Committed Serial Numbers - Credit Order


You can view the serial numbers of individual items or all the items  that are committed on a credit order.


{:.steps}
To view the serial numbers committed on an  item in a credit order, do the following:

- From the **Credit Order** profile, highlight the  line item.
- Select **Options &gt; Item &gt; Show Serial #**.  You will see the [**Serial 
 #** browser]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/sales-docs/docs-profile/options/items/serial_browser.html) for the selected item.



{:.steps}
To view the serial numbers of all items that  have been committed on a credit order, do the following:

- From the **Credit Order** profile, select **Options 
 &gt; All Items &gt; Show All Serial #**. You will see the [**Serial # for all items** browser]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/misc/serial_for_all_items_browser.html).
