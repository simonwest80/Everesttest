# Back End Processes - Committing Items on Credit Orders


When items are committed on a credit order, the stock status of the  item displays the following changes:

- **Stock 
 Status of the item** – There is an increase in the quantity of **On Credit Committed** stock for the item  by the quantity committed.
- **Item 
 Stock Status in the original stock area** – There is a decrease in  the quantity of the **Free Out** and  **Available** **Out** stock of the item.
- **Item 
 Stock Status in the assigned stock area** – There is an increase  in the quantity of stock in the **Free** **Out**, **Available 
 Out** and **On Credit** column  in the assigned stock area.



![]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/img/lens.gif) [Stock  Status]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/sales-docs/docs-profile/options/items/stock_status_items_option_sales_contents.html)

- **Serial 
 numbers of serialized/auto-serialized items** - The serial numbers  attached to the document are moved to the stock area to which they are  assigned.
