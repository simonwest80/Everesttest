The Collection Dialog Box - Prior Payments

This tab is displayed when you collect payment from a foreign currency customer on a credit order. If you refund advance payments to the customer (over a period of time) at different exchange rates, this tab allows you to indicate the amount that must be received as a collection.

This tab comprises two sections:

  • Advance Documents Available - This panel displays all available advance payments made (i.e., advance payments not applied on a credit memo) on the current credit order.
  • Advance Documents Applied - All advances that you apply to the current document are displayed in this panel. Use the > and » buttons to move the available payments to this column. You can change the amount being applied by editing the Amount column.

The amount applied cannot be greater than the amount available. The total amount applied is displayed in the Amount field (path: Collection dialog box > General tab).

When you collect a deposit, a transaction is automatically created in a receipt journal.
