Refund Payments to Customers Through Credit Card

Customers can be refunded on a credit order by credit card. A credit card payment is processed when you select the Use Payment Processor option in the Credit Card Processing tab (path: File > Setup > Accounting > Payment Method > Options > Edit > In New Window > Credit Card Processing tab).

To make refunds on a credit order by credit card, do the following:

  • From the Credit Order profile, select Options > Payment > Refund. You will see the Refund dialog box.
  • Select the appropriate credit card payment method in the PaymentMethod field.
  • Enter the amount to be refunded in the Amount field.
  • Click OK. You will see the Card Verification dialog box.

The amount is credited to the credit card selected in the Card Verification dialog box.

In the case of credit card refunds, the amount of refund is immediately transferred from your merchant account in the next settlement period.
