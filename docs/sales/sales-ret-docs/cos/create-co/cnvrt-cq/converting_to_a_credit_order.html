Convert to a Credit Order

You can convert the quote to a credit order by using the **Reserve option. Once the order has been converted, the credit quote ceases to exist as a system document and generates a credit order with Open status. The credit quote can no longer be accessed from the Credit Quotes browser; however, it appears as a reference number in the credit order created. The document number for the new credit order is automatically generated by the system.**

To convert a credit quote into a credit order, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Invoicing > Credit Quote. You will see the Credit Quotes browser.
  • From the Credit Quotes browser, highlight the quote that has to be converted to a credit order.
  • Select Options > Edit > In New Window. You will see the Credit Quote profile.
  • From the Credit Quote profile, select Process > Reserve. The credit quote is converted to a credit order.
