Discontinue a Customer Action Group

When you discontinue a record, it is temporarily unavailable for selection. The record is not deleted from the database; it is merely deactivated. A discontinued record can be made available for selection by reactivating it.

To discontinue a customer action group, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select CRM > Opportunity Management > Setup > Customer Action. You will see the CustomerAction browser.
  • From the Customer Action browser, highlight the customer action group that has to be discontinued.
  • Select Options > Discontinue. The record that was selected is no longer visible in the Customer Action browser.

When you discontinue a customer action group, the related customer action choices will also be deactivated in the Customer Action browser.
