The Source of Opportunity Browser

The Source of Opportunity browser (path: CRM > Opportunity Management > Setup > Source) displays all the sources that are defined.

This browser contains the Rank column. The rank identifies the hierarchy of the sources.

If Direct Mailer were the most important method of generating opportunities, you would define its rank as 1.

Use the Up and Down arrows to change the rank of a sales stage.

Options common to all browsers are available in this browser.
