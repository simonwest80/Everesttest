View and Activate an Opportunity Grade

You can make a discontinued opportunity grade available for selection by reactivating the record.

To view and reactivate an opportunity grade, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select CRM > Opportunity Management > Setup > Grade. You will see the OpportunityGrade browser.
  • From the Opportunity Grade browser, select Options > View Discontinued. You will see the DiscontinuedGrade browser listing all the deactivated records.
  • Highlight the record you want to reactivate.
  • From the Discontinued Grade browser, select Options > Activate. You will see the Confirm window.
  • Click OK to reactivate. The reactivated record will be displayed in the OpportunityGrade browser.
