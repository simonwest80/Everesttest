# Opportunity Grades


The grade option allows you to rate whether the opportunity is good,  excellent, etc. Grading enables you to focus on opportunities with good  ratings.


![]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/img/example.gif) An  opportunity from a reputed company who is likely to give you repeat orders  is graded high. Whereas one from a company that does not have a good credit  rating would be graded lower.


{:.steps}
To create a grade, do the following:

- From the main  menu bar, select **CRM 
 &gt; Opportunity Management &gt; Setup &gt; Grade**. You will see  the **Opportunity Grade** browser.
- From the **Opportunity Grade** browser, select **Options &gt; New &gt; In New Window**.  You will see the [**Opportunity 
 Grade** profile]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/opportunity-management/opportunity-grades/the_opportunity_grade_profile.html).



{:.note}
You can also create an opportunity grade from:

- Any profile that  contains the **Grade** field.
- An existing **Opportunity Grade** profile by clicking  **Options** > **New**.
