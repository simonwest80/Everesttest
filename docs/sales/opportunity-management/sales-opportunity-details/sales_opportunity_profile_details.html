Sales Opportunity Profile Details

This section provides you detailed information on the fields contained in the Sales Opportunity profile. You can access the Sales Opportunity profile from the Sales Opportunities browser (path: Invoicing > Sales Opportunities > Options > Edit > In New Window). The information contained in the Sales Opportunity profile is grouped under the following heads:

  • General Information
  • Amount/Date Information
  • Currency Information
  • Closing a Sales Opportunity - Information
  • Customer Action Information
  • Custom Fields Information
  • Other Information
