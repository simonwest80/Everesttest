# Other Information - Sales Opportunity


**Priority**
: This option enables you to prioritize the opportunities  and concentrate on the more important ones. The priorities available are:


| : **Priority** | : **Symbol** |
| : High | : ![]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/img/sales_high_priority_icon_sales_opp.gif) |
| : Low | : ![]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/img/sales_low_priority_button_sales_opportunity.gif) |
| : Normal | : ![]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/img/sales_sales_opp_normal_priority.gif) |
| : Follow Up | : ![]({{site.sp_baseurl}}/img/sales_followup_icon.gif) |



**Include in Sales Funnel**
: This **** check  box identifies whether the sales opportunity must be included in the sales  funnel. You can also filter out such opportunities from the sales opportunity  and sales quote browsers and then view the sales funnel for the records  in the browser.
