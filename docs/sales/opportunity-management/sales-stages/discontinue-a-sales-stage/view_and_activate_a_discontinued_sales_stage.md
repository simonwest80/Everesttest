# View and Activate a Discontinued Sales Stage


You can make a discontinued sales stage available for selection by reactivating  the record.


{:.steps}
To view and reactivate a discontinued customer,  do the following:

- From the main  menu bar, select **CRM 
 &gt; Opportunity Management &gt; Setup** **&gt; 
 Sales Stage**. You will see the **Sales 
 Stage Tracking Setup** browser.
- From the **Sales Stage Tracking Setup** browser,  select **Options &gt; View Discontinued**.  You will see the **Discontinued Sales Stages**  browser listing all the deactivated records.
- Highlight the  record you want to reactivate.
- From the **Discontinued Sales Stages** browser, select  **Options &gt; Activate**. You will  see the **Confirm** window.
- Click **OK** to reactivate. The reactivated record  will be displayed in the **Sales Stage 
 Tracking Setup** browser.
