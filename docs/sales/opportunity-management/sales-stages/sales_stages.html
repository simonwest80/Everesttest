Sales Stages

Every sales opportunity progresses through several stages before it can be converted to a sale. The stage identifies the sales phase for the opportunity.

To create a sales stage, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select CRM > Opportunity Management > Setup > Sales Stage. You will see the Sales Stage Tracking Setup browser.
  • From the Sales Stage Tracking Setup browser, select Options > New. You will see the Sales Stage profile.

You can also create a sales stage ** from:

  • Any profile that contains the Sales Stage field.
  • An existing Sales Stage profile by clicking Options > New.
