The Sales Stage Tracking Setup Browser

The Sales Stage Tracking Setup browser (path: CRM > Opportunity Management > Setup > Sales Stage) displays all the sales stages that are defined.

The browser contains the Rank column. The rank identifies the hierarchy of the sales stages.

If Initial Contact were the first stage, its rank would be 1.

Use the Up and Down arrows to change the rank of a sales stage.

Options common to all browsers are also available in this browser.
