Discontinue a Reason Closed Choice

When you discontinue a record, it is temporarily unavailable for selection. The record is not deleted from the database; it is merely deactivated. A discontinued record can be made available for selection by reactivating it.

To discontinue a reason closed choice, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select CRM > Opportunity Management > Setup > Reason Closed. You will see the ReasonClosed Setup browser.
  • From the Reason ClosedSetup browser, highlight the reason closed selection that has to be discontinued.
  • Select Options >Discontinue. The record that was selected is no longer visible in the ReasonClosed Setup browser.
