View and Activate a Discontinued Reason Closed Choice

You can make a discontinued reason closed selection available for selection by activating the record.

To view and activate a discontinued reason closed choice, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select CRM > Opportunity Management > Setup > Reason Closed. You will see the ReasonClosed Setup browser.
  • From the Reason ClosedSetup browser, select Options > View Discontinued. You will see the DiscontinuedReason ClosedChoices browser listing all the deactivated records.
  • Highlight the record you want to activate.
  • From the Discontinued ReasonClosed Choices browser, select Options > Activate. You will see the Confirm window.
  • Click OK. The activated record will be displayed in the Reason Closed Setup browser.
