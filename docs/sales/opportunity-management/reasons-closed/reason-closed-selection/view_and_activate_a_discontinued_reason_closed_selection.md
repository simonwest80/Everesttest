# View and Activate a Discontinued Reason Closed Choice


You can make a discontinued reason closed selection available for selection  by activating the record.


{:.steps}
To view and activate a discontinued reason  closed choice, do the following:

- From the main  menu bar, select **CRM 
 &gt; Opportunity Management &gt; Setup** **&gt; Reason Closed**. You will see the **Reason****Closed** **Setup**  browser.
- From the **Reason** **Closed****Setup** browser, select **Options 
 &gt; View Discontinued**. You will see the **Discontinued****Reason** **Closed****Choices** browser listing all the  deactivated records.
- Highlight the  record you want to activate.
- From the **Discontinued** **Reason****Closed** **Choices**  browser, select **Options** **&gt; Activate**. You will see the **Confirm** window.
- Click **OK**. The activated record will be displayed  in the **Reason Closed Setup** browser.
