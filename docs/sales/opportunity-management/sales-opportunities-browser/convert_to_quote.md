# Convert to Quote


The **Convert to Quote** option  allows you to convert the sales opportunity into a sales quote. Converting  an opportunity to a quote has the following advantages:

- The opportunity  details are automatically copied into the sales quote. Thus the details  need not be replicated in the sales quote when you issue a detailed quote  to the customer.


- The same opportunity  is not duplicated in the **Sales Analysis** reports, i.e., once under opportunities and once under sales quotes.



A sales opportunity may be converted to a sales quote in any of the  following ways:

- From the **Sales Opportunity** browser.
- From the **Sales Opportunity** profile.



{:.steps}
To convert a sales opportunity to a sales  quote from the sales opportunity browser, do the following:

- From the main  menu bar, select **Invoicing &gt; Sales 
 Opportunities**. You will see the **Sales 
 Opportunities** browser.
- From the **Sales** **Opportunities**  browser, highlight the required sales opportunity and select **Options 
 &gt; Convert to Quote**. You will see the **Sales 
 Quote** profile.
- You can then  add the required items to the sales quote and save the details.



{:.steps}
To convert a sales opportunity to a sales  quote from the sales opportunity profile, do the following:

- From the main  menu bar, select **Invoicing &gt; Sales 
 Opportunities**. You will see the **Sales 
 Opportunities** browser.
- From the **Sales Opportunities** browser, highlight  the required sales opportunity and select **Options 
 &gt; Edit &gt; In New Window**. You will see the **Sales 
 Opportunity** profile.
- From the **Sales Opportunity** profile, select **Options &gt; Convert to Quote**. You will  see the **Sales Quote** profile.
- You can then  add the required items to the sales quote and save the details.



{:.note}
You cannot convert a sales opportunity directly to a  sales order. It must first be converted to a sales quote which can then  be converted to a sales order.
