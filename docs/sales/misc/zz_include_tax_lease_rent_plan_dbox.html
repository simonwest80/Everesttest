Include Tax

Select this check box to include the sales tax amount displayed in the Tax field of the document in computing the lease/rent. Deselect this box if you do not want to include the amount of sales tax in the computation of lease/rent.

If the Taxable amount in the document is $1000, the Exempt amount is $500 and the Tax amount is $50, the document total will be computed as $1550 when the Include Tax check box is selected. If this check box is not selected, the document total will be $1500.
