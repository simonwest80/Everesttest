Status Bar Information

Zoom Out/In
Click the Zoom Out ** button to hide the lower half of the credit memo. Zooming out enables you to view all the items contained in the document. This view displays the Customer Information, Item Detail Grid and Document Totals. This is a toggle button. Click the **Zoom In button ** to view all the document details.
Hide/Show
Click the Hide button to conceal the display of lines that are marked hidden. Click the Show to display all lines including hidden lines.
Hide
Preview On/Off
Click the Preview On button to view the extended notes in the new line and click the Preview Off button ** to hide the same.
Preview On/Off
Posted/Unposted
This section displays whether the journal entry pertaining to the sales return transaction has been posted. When you click in this section, the Journal Transactions browser is displayed which displays the accounting entry pertaining to the sale.
Journal Transactions Browser
Document Policy
Click the icon to view and edit the document’s policy.
Document Policy
Document Profitability
The Document Profitability icon displays the profitability of the credit memo document. Click the Document Profitability icon to view the Pricing Analysis dialog box for the entire credit memo document. The profitability of the credit memo document is encrypted in the icon.
Document Profitability
Line Item Profitability
This Line Item Profitability icon displays the profitability of the line item. Click the Line Item Profitability icon to view the Pricing Analysis dialog box for the line item. The profitability of the line item is encrypted in the icon.
Line Item Profitability
Item Notes
This option brings up the item notes for the selected line item, as specified in the Item profile (path: Inventory > Items > Options > Edit > In New Window > Notes Tab).
Item Notes
Customer Status
Place your cursor over the customer status indicators in the status bar of the credit memo document to view the status of the customer.
Customer Status
Customer Balance
Click the customer balance icon displayed in the status bar of the credit memo document to view the

Customer Aging Information browser. The Customer Aging Information browser displays the sales or sales return documents that have outstanding payments or deposits.

Customer Balance
Stock Status
Place your cursor over the stock status icon in the status bar of the credit memo document to view the stock status of the items in the document. Click the item stock status icon to view the

Item Stock Status dialog box that displays detailed information about the stock status of the items in the credit memo document.

Stock Status
