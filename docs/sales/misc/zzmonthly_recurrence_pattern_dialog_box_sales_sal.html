Select the Monthly option to set a monthly schedule for creating sales orders.

You can set the monthly schedule in two ways:

  • Based on the day of the month.

: To create sales orders on the 30th day of every third month specify ‘30’ in Day <number of> days and ‘3’ in every <number of> Month(s).

  • Based on the week day.

: To create sales orders on the last Friday of every third month specify ‘Last Friday’ and 3 in The <number> of every <number> month(s) field.

The maximum number of weeks that can be specified is 100
