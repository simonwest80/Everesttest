---
title: Opportunity Closed
---


This option allows you to search for invoicing documents based on the closed status of the sales opportunity associated with the document. The options available are:

- **All** - Looks for all documents (i.e., whether the opportunity is closed or not).
- **Yes** - Looks for documents for which the sales opportunity has been closed.
- **No** - Looks for documents for which sales opportunity has not been closed.
