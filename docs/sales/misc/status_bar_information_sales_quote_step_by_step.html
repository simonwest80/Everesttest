Status Bar Information

Zoom In/Out
Click the Zoom In button to view all the document details. Click the Zoom Out button to hide the lower half of the sales quote. Zooming out enables you to view all the items contained in the document. This view displays the Customer Information, Item Detail Grid and Document Totals. This is a toggle button.
Hide/Show
Click the Hide button to hide lines that are marked hidden. Click the Show button ** to display all lines including hidden lines.
Hide
Preview On/Off
Click the Preview On button to view the extended notes in the new line and click the Preview Off button to hide the same.
Preview On/Off
Document Policy
Click the icon to view and edit the document’s policy.
Document Policy
Document Profitability
The Document Profitability icon displays the profitability of the sales quote document. Click the Document Profitability icon to view the Pricing Analysis dialog box for the entire sales quote document. The profitability of the sales quote document is encrypted in the icon.
Document Profitability
Line Item Profitability
This Line Item Profitability icon displays the profitability of the line item. Click the Line Item Profitability icon to view the Pricing Analysis dialog box for the line item. The profitability of the line item is encrypted in the icon.
Line Item Profitability
