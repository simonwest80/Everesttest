One by One

You can pick serialized inventory only from the stock areas selected in the Stock Area Selection section of the Pull Sheet Wizard.

To pick a serialized item, do the following:

  • From the Pull Sheet window, select Options > Attach Serial #. You will see the Retrieve Serial Numbers dialog box.
  • Specify the serial number of the item and click Apply.

The serial numbers are attached to the line item and can be scanned into the system using your bar code scanner /the Commit Inventory option.

You cannot attach serial numbers for items that do not exist on the Pull Sheet.
