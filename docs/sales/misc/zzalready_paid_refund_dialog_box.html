This is the amount of the document that has already been paid. In case of orders, the amount already paid is the deposit received against the order. In the case of sales invoices, the amount already paid on the document is displayed in this field. The already paid amount also includes deposit(s) that has/have been applied to the sales invoice. ** The amount paid includes both receipts whose journal has and has not been posted. This is a display field.

Click the Already Paid label to view the Payment History.
