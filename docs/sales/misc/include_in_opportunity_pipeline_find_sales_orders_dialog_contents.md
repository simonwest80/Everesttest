# Include in Sales Funnel


This option allows you to search for sales orders that have been marked  to include in the sales funnel from the **Sales 
 Opportunity** profile.


The following options are available:

- **All**  - All sales orders are searched (i.e., irrespective of whether the documents  are included in the sales funnel or not).
- **Yes**  - Sales orders that have been marked to be included in the sales funnel  are searched.
- **No**  - Sales orders that have NOT been marked to be included in the sales funnel  are searched.
