---
title: Weight
---


Enter the weight of the items contained in the document. By default,  the weight of all the items on the document duly converted to the weight  measure (such as Kg/Lb) of the country pertaining to the document's location  is displayed. This weight may be modified if required. The weight of the  shipment determines the transportation cost.


{:.note}
Freight is computed on an item only if the **Include 
 in Freight Calculation** option is selected on the **Item** profile (path: **Inventory &gt; 
 Items &gt; Options** > **Edit**  > **In New Window** > **Sales** tab).
