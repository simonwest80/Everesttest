Locking and Unlocking the Cash Drawer

If you are temporarily leaving your workstation, you can lock the cash drawer and unlock it later. Locking ensures that an unauthorized person does not access the cash drawer.

To lock a cash drawer, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Invoicing > Point of Sale. You will see the POS Cash Registers browser.
  • From the POS Cash Registers browser, highlight the required cash register and select Options > Lock/Unlock.

A cash drawer that is locked displays the status ‘Locked’ in the Status column of the POS Cash Registers browser. You cannot create transactions from a locked cash register. You cannot lock a cash register that is in use. To create more transactions, unlock the register by selecting the Lock/Unlock option from the POS Cash Registers browser. A cash register with the status New cannot be locked.
