Flow Control Preferences

The Flow Control Preferences associated with Point of Sale transactions can be defined in the Documents dialog box (path: File > Setup > Preferences > Flow Control > Documents > Miscellaneous > Point of Sale tab).

Point of Sale

  • Auto-print invoice in Point-of-Sale document
  • Allow changing the POS document type to sales after credit memo is saved
  • When out of stock non-serialized items are inserted in a POS document
  • Force sales representative to give reason for refunds on credit document at POS
  • Prompt to select sales person when creating point of sale document
