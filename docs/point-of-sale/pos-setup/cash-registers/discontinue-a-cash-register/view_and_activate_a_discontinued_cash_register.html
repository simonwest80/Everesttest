View and Activate a Discontinued Cash Register

To view and activate a discontinued cash register, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select File > Setup > Miscellaneous > Cash Register. You will see the Cash Register browser.
  • From the Cash Register browser, select Options > View Discontinued. You will see the discontinued record in the Discontinued Cash Register browser.
  • From the Discontinued Cash Register browser, highlight the record.
  • Select Options > Activate. You will see the Confirm window
  • Click OK. The activated record will be displayed in the Cash Register browser.
