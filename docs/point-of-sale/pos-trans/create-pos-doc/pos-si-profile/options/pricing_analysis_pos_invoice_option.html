Pricing Analysis

Pricing analysis is mainly for sales representatives to evaluate what would be the ideal price to sell the products. Pricing analysis and commissions for sales representatives are closely interrelated and Everest warns you if the cost for commission calculation is different from the cost setting for pricing analysis defined in Other Flow Control preferences.

The Pricing Analysis option (path: Point of Sale (sales invoice) profile > Options > Pricing Analysis) provides the sales department with instant and accurate profit margin calculations for the entire document. You can view the sales price of an item at progressively increasing rates of margin or mark-up in the Pricing Analysis browser.

Pricing Analysis

The Pricing Analysis browser displays profits for all the items contained in the document at the current selling price based on different cost computations such as average cost, last cost, user-defined cost, and actual/average cost. You can choose the best selling price suitable to you and your customer and click Apply to change the selling price of the item.

Everest allows you to select the pricing analysis for the entire document:

  • Profit breakup document - Displays the pricing analysis statistics and calculations for the entire document.

Profit Breakup Document

  • Profit breakup current item - Displays the pricing analysis statistics and calculations for the selected item.

Profit Breakup Current Item

When you select either, the Profit Breakup grid displays the pricing analysis and you can choose to use the Markup or Margin settings to display the profits. You can also change the markup or margin setting and click Apply to change the pricing analysis.

The costs are computed per sales measure of the item.
