# Point of Sale Invoicing


The Point of Sale invoicing system has been designed for touch screen  compatibility. The user interface has been designed to facilitate the  following:

- Speed of operations.
- Minimal orientation  - The employees manning Point of Sale counters are often part timers or  temporary personnel. Hence, the invoice screen is as user-friendly and  simple as possible.
- Minimization  of mistakes and errors, especially while handling of cash.
