Customer Tracking

The Customer Tracking option (path: Point of Sale (sales invoice) profile > Options > Customer > Customer Tracking) allows you to access the custom tracking information for the customer selected in the document. You can also make changes required to the tracking information.

If a customer is tagged to e-mail in the group ‘Contact us via’, but wants to be contacted henceforth by telephone, you can change the tracking.

This option is not enabled if you select a default Point of Sale customer (POS_CUST).
