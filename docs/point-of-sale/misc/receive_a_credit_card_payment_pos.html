Receive a Credit Card Payment

To receive a credit card payment, do the following:

  • From the Point of Sale (sales invoice) profile, select Options > Payment > Credit Card. You will see the Payment details dialog box.
  • Select the appropriate credit card payment method in the Pay By column and the card number in the Ref. #/Card Number column.
  • Enter the amount of receipt in the Amount field. By default, the amount to be received on the document is displayed in this field.
  • Click OK. You will see the Card Verification dialog box.
