Receiving Credit Card Payments - Point of Sale

Credit card payments may be received on Point of Sale invoices by selecting the Payment > Credit Card option from the Point of Sale document profile. Credit card payments are automatically processed by Everest only if the Use Payment Processor option is selected in the Credit Card Processing tab of the Payment Method profile (path: File > Setup > Accounting > Payment Methods > Options > Edit > In New Window > Credit Card Processing tab).

To receive a credit card payment, do the following:

  • From the Point of Sale (sales invoice) profile, select Options > Payment > Credit Card. You will see the Payment Details dialog box.
  • Select the appropriate credit card payment method in the Pay by column.
  • Enter the amount of receipt in the Amount column.
  • Click OK. You will see the Card Verification dialog box.
