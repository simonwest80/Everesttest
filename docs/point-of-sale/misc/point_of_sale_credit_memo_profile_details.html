Point of Sale (Credit Memo) - Profile Details

Information available in a Pointof Sale (credit memo) profile is listed below:

  • Customer Information
  • Cash Register Details
  • Sales Rep and Cashier
  • Document Information
  • Item Information
  • Document Tab View Details
  • Foreign Currency Information
  • The Point of Sale Outlook Bar
  • Status Bar Information

Information contained in the Pointof Sale(credit memo) profile is identical to the Point ofSale (sales invoice) profile.
