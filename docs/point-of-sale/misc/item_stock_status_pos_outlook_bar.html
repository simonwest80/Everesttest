Item Stock Status

The Item Stock Status option displays the stock status of the inventoried items on the document.

To access the Item Stock Status dialog box, do the following:

  • From the Point of Sale (sales invoice) profile, select an item from the Item Details Grid and choose Options > Item > Item Stock Status. You will see the Item Stock Status dialog box.

OR

  • From the Point of Sale (sales invoice) profile, select an item from the Item Details Grid and click the Item Stock Status icon from the Inventory section of the Outlook Bar. You will see the Item Stock Status dialog box.

You can view the quantities in the Stock Status dialog box either by purchase measure or sales measure of the item.
