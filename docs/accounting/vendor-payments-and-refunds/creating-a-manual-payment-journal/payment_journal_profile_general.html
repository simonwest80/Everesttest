Payment Journal Profile - General

The Payment Journal profile displays the details of payment and refund transactions for vendors. The status bar of the Payment Journal profile displays the Transaction status and Payment status that have been used for the journal.

This profile comprises the following sections:

  • Journals ** - This section comprises the header information for the journal that is common to all transactions within the journal.
  • Transactions ** - This section displays details regarding the payment or refund. The payment information is displayed in the form of a check.
  • Transaction entries ** - This section displays details of the accounting entries created for the transaction.
