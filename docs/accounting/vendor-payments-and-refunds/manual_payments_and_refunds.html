Manual Payments and Refunds

Manual payments and refunds refer to accounting entries made in an existing or new payment journal for vendor payments and refunds. Although the accounting entries are made manually, Everest updates the documents against which these payments or refunds are made.

 If payments or refunds are made from a document, then Everest automatically calculates the discount available for the payment term. If you make a manual payment entry, then you also have to manually calculate the discount available.
