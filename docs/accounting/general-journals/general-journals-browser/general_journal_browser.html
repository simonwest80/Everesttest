Unposted General Journals Browser

The Unposted General Journals browser (path: Accounting > General Journal) lists all the general journals that are not posted.

The options available in the general journals browser may be categorized as:

Information Available

The options that provide information in the Unposted General Journals browser are:

  • List Transaction
  • Posted Journals

Other Options

The other options available in the Unposted General Journals browser are:

  • Select / Unselect All
  • Purge journal
  • Open Journal(s)
  • Lock Journal(s)
  • Print Journal(s)
  • Post Journal(s)
  • Print Stock Transfer Journal

The options common to all browsers in Everest are also available in this browser.
