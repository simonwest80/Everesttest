View Posted Purchase Journals

To view the posted purchase journals, do the following:

  • From the main menu, select Accounting > Account Payable > Purchase Journal. You will see the Unposted Purchase Journals browser.
  • From the Unposted Purchase Journals browser, select Options > Posted Journals. You will see the Posted Purchase Journals browser.
