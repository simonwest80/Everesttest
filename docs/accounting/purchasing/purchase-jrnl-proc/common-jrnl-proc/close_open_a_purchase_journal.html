Lock/Open a Purchase Journal

A journal can have the status ‘Open’ or ‘Locked’ which is displayed in the Status column of the Unposted Purchase Journals browser.

To lock or open a purchase journal, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Accounting > Account Payable > Purchase Journal. You will see the Unposted Purchase Journals browser.
  • From the Unposted Purchase Journals browser, highlight the required journal.
  • Select Options > Lock Journal(s) to lock a journal and Options > Open Journal(s) to change the status of a journal to ‘open’.
