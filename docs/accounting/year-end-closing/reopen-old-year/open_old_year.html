Open Old Year

Accounts for an old year can be reopened to:
  • Modify, view and print old transactions
  • Add new transactions.
Opening a closed year will delete the accounting entries created during the year closing process. You can only reopen the latest year that was closed.

 If you close all years upto 2002, then you cannot reopen the year 2000 without first reopening 2001.

In order to maintain an audit trail, when you open a closed year, the “Closed year reopened” event is triggered.

To open an old year, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Accounting > Year End > Open Old Year. You will see the Open Year dialog box.
  • From the Open Year dialog box, click Open Year.
