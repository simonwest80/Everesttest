The Payment Register Wizard

The Payment Register Wizard contains the following sections:

  • Welcome
  • Bank account Selection
  • Vendor/Customer Selection
  • Payment Method Selection

 You can navigate within the wizard by clicking the Next button or double-clicking a section in the left panel.

 You are recommended to follow the steps in the wizard sequentially. This ensures that all the required data for generating the payment register has been specified.
