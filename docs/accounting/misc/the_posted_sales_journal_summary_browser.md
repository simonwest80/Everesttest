# The Posted Sales Journal Summary Browser


The **Posted Sales Journal Summary** browser is displayed when **Options &gt; General Ledger** (**Summary**) is selected in the **Posted Sales Journals** browser.


This browser lists all the accounts debited or credited in the journal and the net amount of debit and credit for the account.


The options common to all browsers are also available in this browser.
