The Posted Purchase Journals Browser

When the Posted Journals option is selected, the Posted Purchase Journals browser is displayed. The Transactions menu options (path: Posted Purchase Journals browser > Options > Transactions) in the Posted Purchase Journals browser allows you to view details of transactions in the journal or a summary of the accounts debited or credited in the journal.

The Transactions menu has the following sub menu options:

  • General Ledger (Detail)
  • General Ledger (Summary)

Options common to all browsers are also available in this browser.
