Example - Accounting Entries - Purchase Invoice

A Purchase Invoice is created with the following details:

- Item 1  Inventory Control Account  Location/Sub-Location Main Job 1 $1000
- Item 2  Inventory Control Account Location/Sub-Location DEP1 Job 1  $500
- Item 2  Inventory Control Account Location/Sub-Location DEP1 Job 2  $500
- Item 3  Shipping Charges Account  Location/Sub-Location DEP1 Job 1  $600
- Item 4  Shipping Charges Account Location/Sub-Location Main Job 2 $750
- Item 2  Shipping Charges Account Location/Sub-Location Main Job 2  $500
