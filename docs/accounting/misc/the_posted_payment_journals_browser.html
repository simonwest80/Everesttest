The Posted Payment Journals Browser

When the Posted Journals option is selected, the Posted Payment Journals browser is displayed. The Transactions menu options in the Posted Payment Journals browser (path: Posted Payment Journals browser > Options > Transactions) allow you to view details of transactions in the journal or a summary of the accounts debited or credited in the journal.

The Transactions menu has the following sub menu options:

  • General Ledger (Detail)
  • General Ledger (Summary)
  • Payment Method (Summary)

The options common to all browsers in Everest are also available in this browser.
