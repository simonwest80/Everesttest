The Posted Sales Journals Browser

When the Posted Journals option is selected, the Posted Sales Journals browser is displayed.

The Transactions menu options (path: Posted Sales Journals browser > Options > Transactions) in the Posted Sales Journals browser allows you to view details of transactions in the journal or a summary of the accounts debited or credited in the journal.

The Transactions menu has the following sub menu options:

  • General Ledger (Detail)
  • General Ledger (Summary)

The options common to all browsers are also available in this browser.
