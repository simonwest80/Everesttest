Transactions

The Transactions menu options (path: Unposted Receipt Journals Browser > Options > Transactions) allow you to view a summary of accounts debited or credited in the journal, and the payment method through which payments have been received from or refunds made to customers.

The Transactions menu has the following sub menu options:

  • General Ledger (Summary)
  • Payment Method (Summary)
