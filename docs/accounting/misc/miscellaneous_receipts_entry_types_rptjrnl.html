Miscellaneous Receipts

Select this option when you want to receive payment from the customer by selecting a General Ledger account of your choice without creating a sales invoice.
 Such payments are not recorded in the customer transaction history since the receipt is not associated with any document.

 Sale of recycled paper can be accounted as miscellaneous receipts.

When this entry type is selected:

  • The required account may be selected and debited or credited.
  • A document number cannot be specified since this is a direct receipt for an expense and the Account Receivable account is not credited.
