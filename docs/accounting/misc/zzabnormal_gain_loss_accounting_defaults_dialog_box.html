Specify the account in which abnormal gains during manufacture of assembly type of items is accounted. You can select either an expense or revenue account in this field.

If this account is not defined, then when a work order is put into process or built, then Everest forces you to select this account. You can only select an account that has been set up in the base currency.

 Depending on the statutory accounting regulations and business practices in your country, you may choose to:

  • Account for abnormal losses in an expense account and abnormal gains in a revenue account OR
  • Account both abnormal losses and gains in the same account.
