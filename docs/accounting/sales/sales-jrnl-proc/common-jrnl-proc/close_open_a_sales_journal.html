Lock/Open a Sales Journal

A journal can have the status ‘Open’ or ‘Locked’. The status of a journal is displayed in the Status column of the Unposted Sales Journals browser.

To lock or open a sales journal, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Accounting > Account Receivable > Sales Journal. You will see the Unposted Sales Journals browser.
  • From the Unposted Sales Journals browser, highlight the required journal.
  • Select Options > Lock Journal(s) to lock a journal and Options > Open journal(s) to change the status of a journal to open.
