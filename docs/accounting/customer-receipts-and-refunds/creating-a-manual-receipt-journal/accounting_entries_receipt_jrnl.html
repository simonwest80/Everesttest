Accounting Entries for Receipts

This section describes the accounting entries that are created when payments are received against documents.

The accounting entries created on receiving or applying payments from customers are categorized under the following:

  • When payment is received on a sales invoice
  • When the deposit received on a sales order is applied against the sales invoice
  • When a credit memo is applied to the sales invoice
  • When a deposit is received on a sales order
  • When a credit memo is applied to a sales order
