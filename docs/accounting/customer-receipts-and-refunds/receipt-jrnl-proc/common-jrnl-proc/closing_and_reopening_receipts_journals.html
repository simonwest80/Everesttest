Lock/Open Receipt Journals

To lock or open a receipts journal, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Accounting > Account Receivable > Receipts Journal. You will see the Unposted Receipt Journals browser.
  • From the Unposted Receipt Journals browser, highlight the required journal.
  • Select Options > Lock Journal(s) to lock a journal and Options > Open journal(s) to change the journal status to ‘open’.

The status of a journal is displayed in the Status column of the Unposted Receipt Journals browser.
