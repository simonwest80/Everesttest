View Items Imported from Third Party Catalogs

You can view the imported third party items from the Third Party Catalog browser

To view the imported items, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Inventory > Third Party Catalogs. You will see the Third Parties Catalogs browser.
  • From the Third Parties browser, highlight the third party catalog you want to review and select Options > Items View. You will see the Items browser, which displays the details of the items imported from the third party catalog.

You can differentiate New Items, Active items, Phased Out Items, Allocated Items and Special Order Items on the online store based on the images attached to each of these item types.

You can also view the item type while retrieving these items into a sales order.
