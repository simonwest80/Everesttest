---
title: Filter Field
---


This section displays the selected filters. Use the ![]({{site.utl_baseurl}}/img/utility_right_arrow_button.gif) button to select the filter. The caption for the **Filter**  field shows the column selected on the **Map 
 the Source Fields** screen.


{:.example}
A **Filter**  field caption of **Filter: ITEM_CODE**  means that the **ITEM_CODE** column  is selected on the **Map the Source Fields**  screen.
