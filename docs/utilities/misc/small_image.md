# Small Image


Select the image that is to be associated as a small image. Click the  ![]({{site.utl_baseurl}}/img/utility_lens_button.gif) button beside the field, to browse for the image file.  The small images are displayed in the **View 
 Cart** page of your online store.


{:.note}
The small image attached should be of dimension  20x20 pixels.
