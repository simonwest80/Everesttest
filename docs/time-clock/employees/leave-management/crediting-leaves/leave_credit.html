Leave Credit

The Leave Credit process in Everest helps you to credit leave for all the employees in the organization.

  • This process is carried out only for the leaves that are assigned to an employee in the Leave Balance profile.

If paid leave and sick leave are assigned to an employee, only these leaves are credited by the Leave Credit process.

  • The leaves credited to the employees are based on the settings in the Leave Types Profile.
