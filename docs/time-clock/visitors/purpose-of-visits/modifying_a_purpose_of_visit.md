# Modify a Purpose of Visit

- The **Purpose 
 of Visits** browser (path: **Time 
 Clock** > **Setup** > **Purpose of Visit &gt; Options** > **Edit** > **In New Window**).
- The **Purpose 
 of Visit** profile - Enter the code of the Area to be modified.



{:.note}
You cannot modify the **Code**  field.
