Expenses and Overheads

You can add items of expenses and overheads as non-inventoried items to the Bill Of Material (path: Inventory > Items > Options > Edit > In New Window > Assembly > Inputs tab). Thus, the standard cost is specified in the Bill of Material.

Non-inventoried Items


Assembly Item Profile - Assembly- Input Tab

The actual expense on the item is allocated to the work order (by entering the allocated expense in the Actual Cost column of the Work Order profile). You can also add items of expense/overheads directly into the work order.
