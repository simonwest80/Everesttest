Stock Status of Assembled/Built Items

When an item is assembled, the stock status of the assembled items displays the following changes:

  • Stock of the Item - The ‘Total Stock’, ‘Net Free Stock’ and ‘Available Stock’ of the assembly item is incremented by the quantities built.
  • Stock in the Default Stock Area - The ‘Stock’ and the ‘Free Stock’ of the Assembly item is incremented in the default stock area.
