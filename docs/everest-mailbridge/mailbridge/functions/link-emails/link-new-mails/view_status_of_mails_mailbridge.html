View Status of E-mails

To view the number of mails that have/have not been linked, since logging on to Everest MailBridge, place your cursor over the MailBridge icon on your status bar. You will see the number of:

  • Inbound mails that are linked
  • Inbound mails that are not linked
  • Outbound mails that are linked
  • Outbound mails that are not linked
