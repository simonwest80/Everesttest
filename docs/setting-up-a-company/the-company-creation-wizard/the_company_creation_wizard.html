The Company Creation Wizard

The Company Creation Wizard guides you through the process of setting up a company. The wizard is divided into the left and right panels.

The left panel lists the various sections in the wizard.

Sections that pertain to a particular function are grouped under categories.

All steps relating to accounting are grouped under the category Accounting.

The right panel displays details of a section selected in the left panel. When you finish specifying the information for a section, a red check mark appears beside the section name in the left panel.

You can navigate within the wizard by clicking the Next button or double-clicking a section in the left panel.

You can exit the Company Creation Wizard at any point of time and return to it later. Though it is not mandatory, it is better to set up the areas that you will be using in Everest, at the onset.

The Company Creation Wizard comprises the following:

  • Welcome Section
  • Company Information
  • Accounting Information
  • Setup Inventory Details
  • Setup Customers Details
  • Setup Vendor Details
  • Setup Other Preferences
  • Conclusion Section
