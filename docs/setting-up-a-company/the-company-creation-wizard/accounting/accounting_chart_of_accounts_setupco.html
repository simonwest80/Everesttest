Accounting - Chart of Accounts

The Chart of Accounts is a list of all your accounts and is the heart of your accounting system. All transactions are posted to accounts. The financial result of a company’s operations is derived from the values accumulated in these accounts.

Use this section to either set up your own chart of accounts or select a predefined chart of accounts.

You can discontinue accounts from the Chart of Accounts after you create them by selecting Accounting > Chart of Accounts. You will see the Chart of Accounts browser. Highlight the required accounts and select Options > Discontinue.

You cannot discontinue a chart of account that:

  • has a non-zero balance
  • has a detail account beneath it
  • is assigned to a posting group
  • is assigned to a payment method
  • is included in an unposted journal
  • is used in Everest US Payroll

Click Next after creating or selecting your chart of accounts. The Accounting - Defaults section is displayed.

: If the Copy setup information from an existing company option is selected from the Company - Template screen, you will see the Accounting - Chart of Accounts - Template screen.
