# Customer Information


To set up information pertaining to customers, click the **Customers** section of the **Company Creation 
 Wizard**.


Customer information comprises the following:

- <font color="#000000" class="hcp2">Specifying </font>[customer  details]({{site.sc_baseurl}}/the-company-creation-wizard/customers/customers_customer_details.html)<font color="#000000" class="hcp2"> &nbsp;</font>
- Specifying  the [balance]({{site.sc_baseurl}}/the-company-creation-wizard/customers/customers_account_balances.html) outstanding from  each customer.
