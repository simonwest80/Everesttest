# Multimedia 


Throughout **Everest**, profiles  (such as customer, vendor, user and item) can have multiple multimedia  files attached. Multimedia files can be anything like a map to the vendor's  location, a photo of a user, a product specification or even a video giving  a product overview, enabling even new sales people to sound like seasoned  professionals.
