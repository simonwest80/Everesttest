Set up and View Stock Areas

Inventory that you buy or sell can move through different areas called stock areas.

Defining different stock areas helps keep track of the inventory in each area. A stock area may be a physical location such as a warehouse or a virtual area such as stock-in-transit.

You can allocate items to stock areas from where they are retrieved at the time of sale.

Click Click here to setup stock areas to create a stock area. You will see the Stock Area profile.

Stock Areas Profile

You can also set up stock areas by selecting the option Stock Area from Inventory > Stock Areas > Options > New > In New Window.

You can view the stock areas that are defined by clicking the option Click here to view existing Stock Areas.
