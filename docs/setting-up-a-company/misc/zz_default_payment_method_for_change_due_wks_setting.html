Specify the default payment method to be used for change due to customers.

When you receive an excess amount of money from a customer on account of a Point of Sale transaction, you have to refund the change due to the customer.

You can set the default payment method for change due to ‘Cash’.

You can also specify the default payment method for change due to customers in the Accounting dialog box (path: File > Setup > Preferences > Defaults > Accounting > Miscellaneous tab).
