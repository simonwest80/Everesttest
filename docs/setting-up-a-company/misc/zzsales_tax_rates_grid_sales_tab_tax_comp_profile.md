---
title: Sales Tax Rates Grid
---


Use this section to enter the tax rate for a range of item prices. The  first tier’s starting value displays zero (base currency). Enter the ending  range for the item price for the first tier in the **Item 
 Price To** field. The price tier value cannot be negative and the  **Item Price To** value cannot be  less than the corresponding **Item Price 
 From** value.


After entering the details for the first tier, press the Insert key  to insert a new price tier in the grid. The **Item 
 Price From** value displays the previous tier’s **Item 
 Price To** value + 1 subunit of the base currency.
