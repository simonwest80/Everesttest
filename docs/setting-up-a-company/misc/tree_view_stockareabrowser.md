# Tree-view


To get the tree-view of the **Stock****Areas** browser, click the **Show Treeview** ![]({{site.sc_baseurl}}/img/setup_show_treeview.gif) icon  on the toolbar. From the tree-view of the **Stock****Areas** browser you can reorganize  the stock area levels by dragging and dropping the parent or child levels  within the same location.


To revert to the normal browser view, click the **Hide 
 Treeview** ![]({{site.sc_baseurl}}/img/setup_hide_treeview.gif) icon.
