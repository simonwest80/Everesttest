Select one of the two options to build your company’s Chart of Accounts:

  • Select a COA from a list of pre-defined COAs - If you select this option Everest provides you with a chart of accounts that is tailor made for the kind of business you have defined.
  • Creating your own COA - If you select this option and click Next, the Chart of Accounts - User-defined section is displayed where you can define your own chart of accounts.
