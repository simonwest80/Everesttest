---
title: Stock area order for auto-picking
---


The order in which the stock areas are selected for auto-picking must  be defined in this section. The columns included in this section are **Location**, **Parent 
 Stock Area**, **Stock Bin** and  **Stock Bin Position**.


All the stock areas with the stock type as "Normal" are listed  here. You can change the sequence of the stock areas from which you want  the items to be picked by clicking the **Up**  and **Down** arrow buttons.
