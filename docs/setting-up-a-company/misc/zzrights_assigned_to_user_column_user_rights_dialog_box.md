---
title: Rights assigned to user column
---


The **Rights assigned to user**  column displays all the rights that have been assigned to the user. To  remove the rights assigned to the user, highlight the specific right in  this column and select Remove  from the right-click menu or click the ![]({{site.sc_baseurl}}/img/setup_move_right.gif) icon.


To remove all the rights assigned to a user, select **Remove 
 All** from the right-click menu or click the ![]({{site.sc_baseurl}}/img/setup_moveall_right.gif) icon.
