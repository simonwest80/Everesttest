The Initial Item Quantities Dialog Box

The Initial Item Quantities dialog box displays all the items that are created in your database. Enter the stock of items in the Quantity column and the average cost per unit of the item in the Average Cost column. You cannot enter fractional and negative quantities in the Quantity column.

The stock area can be changed for the required items by clicking the Stock Area column and selecting a stock area from the drop-down list.

The stock value is automatically computed based on the average cost and the quantity of the items. In the case of serialized items, serial numbers are generated automatically and the average cost is used as the actual cost of each serial number of the item.

You can enter the above details only at the time of creating a company. You cannot modify the quantities displayed in this dialog box once you have made transactions in the company.

Enter the quantities and the cost of the items, and click OK to return to the Company Creation Wizard.

When you click OK, an accounting entry is automatically created where the Inventory Control account is debited and the Unreconciled Difference account is credited with the value of the inventory.

Once the details are saved, you can neither edit the quantities and values entered here, nor can you make further entries.
