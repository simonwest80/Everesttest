Enter the next sequence number for recurring purchase orders, for the selected location/sub-location. Recurring purchase orders need to be created continuously for a certain period of time.

A vendor may want to sell you services for the next one year. You need to create purchase orders at the beginning of every month for a year. Instead of creating a purchase order at the beginning of every month, you can choose Options> Recurring Pattern from a purchase order document, to automatically create purchase orders. The purchase order created will take the recurring number you have specified for the selected location/sub-location.

Recurring documents can only be created for non-inventoried items. This field is not functional since it has been added to provide compatibility with future releases of Everest.
