---
title: zzmonthly_event_based_email_scheduling
---


Select the **Monthly** option to  set a monthly schedule for sending e-mails. The maximum number of months  that can be specified is 100.


You can set the monthly schedule in two ways:

- Based on the day  of the month



{:.example}
E-mails can be sent on the 30th day of third month.

- Based on the week  day



{:.example}
E-mails can be sent on the last Friday of every month.
