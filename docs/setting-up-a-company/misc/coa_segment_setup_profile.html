COA Segment Setup Profile

Use the COA Segment Setup profile to create segments for your chart of accounts structure. Accounts are created later using your defined segments. You can access the COA Segment Setup profile from the Company Creation Wizard when you are setting up your company or from the Everest main menu bar after your company has been created.
