---
title: Groups Not Assigned
---


The **Groups Not Assigned** column  displays all the groups not assigned to the selected right. A right may  be assigned to a group by highlighting the group and choosing **Select**  from the right-click menu or by clicking the ![]({{site.sc_baseurl}}/img/setup_move_left.gif) icon.


To assign groups to the security right, choose the **Select 
 All** option from the right-click menu or click the ![]({{site.sc_baseurl}}/img/setup_moveall_left.gif) icon.
