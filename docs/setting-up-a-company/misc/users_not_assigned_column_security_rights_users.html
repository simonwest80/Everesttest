Users Not Assigned Column

Users to whom the right has not been assigned are listed in this ** column.

User(s) may be assigned a right by selecting the user(s) and choosing Add from the right-click menu or by clicking the icon.

To grant all users the selected right, choose the Add All option from the right-click menu or click the icon.
