Rate Quotations

The buy and sell rates are expressed in terms of the quote type and quote quantity for the foreign currency, as set up in the Foreign Currency profile.

Your base currency is the US Dollar (USD) and the foreign currency is the Japanese Yen (JPY). You have set up the quote type in the JPY profile as **Foreign to Base with the quote quantity as 100. If the rate for 1 JPY = 0.00872, the rate you must enter in the profile must be 0.872 for 100 JPY.**
