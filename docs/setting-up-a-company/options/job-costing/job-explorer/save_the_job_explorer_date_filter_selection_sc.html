Save the Job Explorer Date Filter Selection

To view the same date filter every time you open the Job Explorer, save your date filter conditions along with your Job Explorer settings.

To save your Date Filter settings in the Job Explorer, do the following:

  • Select the date filter condition. To do this, select an option in the Document Dates field. If you select “Custom”, enter the To and From dates for your custom date range.
  • Right-click the title bar of the Job Explorer.
  • Select Save Now. Your date filter condition selection saves along with your existing Job Explorer settings. When you open the Job Explorer again, your date filter settings appear in the filter.
