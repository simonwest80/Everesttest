Sales Taxes

The sales tax feature provides you with a highly flexible design that supports a wide range of sales tax structures.

To use the sales tax feature, do the following:

  • Set up sales tax details.
  • Login with the required jurisdiction.

Default Jurisdiction and Location/Sub-Location Dialog Box

  • Raise sales documents for the required tax to be applied automatically.

Creating a sales invoice

To set up sales tax details, do the following:

  • Create individual Tax Components.
  • Create Tax Codes and associate the appropriate tax components.
  • Create Jurisdictions and assign the relevant tax code.
  • Create Special Tax Rules, for items that have a tax rate different from the common tax rate which is applicable to the jurisdiction or state.
You can levy a different tax rate for each line item in a document.
