Gift Card Processing

Everest supports the selling and redemption of Gift Cards. Everest processes gift cards through the payment processor PayBridge. The PayBridge application acts as an intermediary to process gift card payments between Everest and PCCharge. PCCharge is the payment processing software that supports many payment networks. Everest allows you to sell and activate gift cards.

A license is required to process gift cards. Since a Gift Card license is not a user-based license, only one license is needed for the entire company. The Gift Card feature is available in the Sample Company and in evaluation mode.
