# <font style="color: #000000;" color="#000000">Credit Card Processing</font>


Credit card payments are one of the most popular methods of payment,  especially in retail sales and web sales. To optimize the advantages of  receiving payments through credit cards, the processing has to be integrated  with the business software.


**Everest** supports credit card  processing for credit card payments, as it has the following advantages:

- It eliminates  the cost of purchasing separate terminals for processing credit card information.
- It minimizes  data entry errors by verifying the credit card number.
- It maintains  a history of credit cards, which accelerates service of repeat orders.
- It helps in  address verification in case of MO/TO (Mail Order, Telephone Order) Sales.
- A user can  obtain approval from the workstation without having to go to a credit  card terminal.
