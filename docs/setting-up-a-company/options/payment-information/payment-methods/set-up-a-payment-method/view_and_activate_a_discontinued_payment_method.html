View and Activate a Discontinued Payment Method

To view and activate a discontinued payment method, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select File > Setup > Accounting > Payment Methods. You will see the Payment Methods browser.
  • From the Payment Methods browser, select Options > View Discontinued. You will see the discontinued record in the Discontinued Payment Methods browser.
  • From the Discontinued Payment Methods browser, highlight the record.
  • Select

    Options > Activate. You will be prompted for confirmation.

  • Click

    OK. The activated record will be displayed in the Payment Methods browser.
