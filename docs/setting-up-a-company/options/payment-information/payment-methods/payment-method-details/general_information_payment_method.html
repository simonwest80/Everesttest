General Information

Code and Description
Code is the unique identifier of the payment method while the description provides the full name. Once defined, you cannot change the code.

For American Express payments, the code can be ‘AE’ while the description can be ‘American Express’.

Account
This option allows you to specify the account in your chart of accounts that will be affected, when payments are made or received. Use your payment clearing account for collections from customers, and your checking account for payments made to vendors.
The account is debited when payments are received from customers or refunds are received from vendors, and credited with customer refunds or vendor payments.
Only an account set up as a bank account is available for selection.
For Gift Cards, specify a liability account in your chart of accounts that will be affected, when payments are made or received for gift cards. This account should be the same as the liability account you set up for gift cards initially.
