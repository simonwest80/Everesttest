View and Activate a Discontinued Stock Bin

To view and activate a discontinued stock bin, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Inventory > Stock Areas. You will see the Stock Areas browser.
  • From the Stock Areas browser, select View > Discontinued. You will see the discontinued record in the Discontinued Stock Areas browser.
  • From the Discontinued Stock Areas browser, highlight the record.
  • Select

    Options > Activate. You will be prompted for confirmation.

  • Click

    OK. The activated record will be displayed in the Stock Areas browser.
