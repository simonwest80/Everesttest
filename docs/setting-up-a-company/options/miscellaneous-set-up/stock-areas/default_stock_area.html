Default Stock Area

Everest allows inventory to be automatically transferred to a stock area when a document is processed.

Inventory can be automatically moved into the Main Stock Area when a Purchase Receipt or Invoice is created, or inventory can be moved into the Released area when a Sales Invoice is created.

Stock areas are affected in the following cases:

  • Inventory is received on a purchase receipt or invoice, or returned on a debit memo.
  • Inventory is issued on a sales invoice or received on a credit memo.
  • Inventory is assigned or unassigned on an order.

You can specify default stock areas for each of these functions in the Stock Transfer tab of the Location profile (path: File > Setup > Accounting > Location/Sub-Location > From the Location/Sub-Locations browser, double-click on the required record).

The stock area can be changed on each document at the time of receipt or sale.

The Location Profile - Stock Transfer Tab - Purchase Document

This profile is used to specify the stock areas to which items should be transferred when a purchase document such as purchase order, purchase receipt, purchase invoice, debit order and debit memo are created and items are received/returned on them.

The Location Profile - Stock Transfer - Purchase Documents

The Location Profile - Stock Transfer Tab - Sales Documents

This profile is used to specify the stock areas to which items should be transferred when a sales document such as sales order, sales invoice, credit order and credit memo are created and items are dispatched/returned on them.

If you have indicated CSHIP (customer shipment expected) as the default stock area when items are attached to a sales order, Everest will automatically transfer items to CSHIP whenever a sales order is created and items are assigned to it.
