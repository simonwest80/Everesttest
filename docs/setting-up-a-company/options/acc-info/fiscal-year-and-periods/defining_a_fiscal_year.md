# Create Fiscal Year


A fiscal year may be defined from the **Fiscal 
 Period Setup** dialog box.


{:.steps}
To define a fiscal year, do the following:

- From the main  menu bar, select **File &gt; Setup &gt; 
 Accounting &gt; Periods**. You will see the **Fiscal 
 Period Setup** dialog box.
- From the **Fiscal Period Setup** dialog box, click  **New**. You will be able to enter  the details for the new fiscal year in the [**Fiscal Period Setup** dialog box]({{site.sc_baseurl}}/options/acc-info/fiscal-year-and-periods/fiscal_period_setup_dialog_box.html).
