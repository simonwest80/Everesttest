Create Fiscal Year

A fiscal year may be defined from the Fiscal Period Setup dialog box.

To define a fiscal year, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select File > Setup > Accounting > Periods. You will see the Fiscal Period Setup dialog box.
  • From the Fiscal Period Setup dialog box, click New. You will be able to enter the details for the new fiscal year in the Fiscal Period Setup dialog box.
