# The Chart of Accounts Browser


The **Chart of Accounts** browser  (path: **Accounting &gt; Chart of Accounts**)  displays the account balances. These balances comprise the journals that  are posted to the general ledger.


The browser options are listed below:

- [Breakup]({{site.sc_baseurl}}/misc/break-up.html)
- [Budget]({{site.sc_baseurl}}/misc/budget.html)
- [Period  Balances]({{site.sc_baseurl}}/misc/period_balances_setupco.html)
- [Detail  Activity]({{site.sc_baseurl}}/misc/detail_activity.html)
- [Summary]({{site.sc_baseurl}}/misc/summary.html)
- [Add  Segment to Account]({{site.sc_baseurl}}/misc/add_segment_to_account_coa_browser_option.html)
- [Merge/Rename]({{site.sc_baseurl}}/misc/merge_and_rename_coa_accounts_coa_browser_option_sc.html)



Options common to all browsers are also available in the **Chart 
 of Accounts** browser.
