The Chart of Accounts Browser

The Chart of Accounts browser (path: Accounting > Chart of Accounts) displays the account balances. These balances comprise the journals that are posted to the general ledger.

The browser options are listed below:

  • Breakup
  • Budget
  • Period Balances
  • Detail Activity
  • Summary
  • Add Segment to Account
  • Merge/Rename

Options common to all browsers are also available in the Chart of Accounts browser.
