Geographical Mapping

The Geographical Mapping option helps locate a customer’s address on a map.

To view the customer’s address on a map, do the following:

  • From the Customers browser, highlight the required customer record.
  • Click the icon on the browser toolbar. You can also access this icon from the Customer profile. Your Web browser opens and the customer’s address is displayed on the map.

 Everest uses Mapquest to display the location on a map. You must be connected to the internet to use this feature.
