Multiple Receipts

This option displays the Multiple Receipt Wizard that enables you to receive multiple payments from customers, and make refunds, without having to make individual payments in the receipt journal or from within documents.

 From the Customers browser, you can select multiple customers, from whom you want to receive payments. You do not need to specify Receipt Journal Information and Document Criteria such as Payment Terms and Document Aliases in the Multiple Receipts Wizard. These fields are automatically populated with the selected customers information.
