# Exempt Amount


Document Amount


Specify the exempt amount to base your search on. This amount appears  in the **Exempt** field of purchase  documents.


Received on Invoice Amount


Specify the exempt amount to base your search on. This amount appears  in the **Exempt** field of a purchase  invoice.
