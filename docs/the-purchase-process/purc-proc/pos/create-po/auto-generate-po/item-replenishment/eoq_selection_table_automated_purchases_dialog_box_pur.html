*Required Quantity (Calculated) = Planned Quantity – Current Supply

Current Supply = Total Stock – Open SO + Open PO

**Planned Quantity = The selected replenishment quantity (Max / Reorder Point / Min)

If **Purchase in Increments of Vendor EOQ is selected, Planned Quantity is adjusted with open sales orders and open purchase orders and is compared with increments of the EOQ to get the required quantity. If you see a zero (0) in the Required quantity column of the Item Replenishment browser, the item is needed but you must make the decision on if or how many should be purchased. This can occur when the Vendor EOQ is higher than the calculated required quantity, increments of Vendor EOQ are used, and the planned quantity cannot be exceeded.**
