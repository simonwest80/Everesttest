Edit Manufacturer’s Serial Numbers

You can edit the manufacturer’s serial number from the Serial Number for all Items browser (path: purchase document profile > Options > ShowAll Serial #) OR the Serial # browser (path: purchase document profile > Options > Items > ShowSerial #). You can also edit the serial number from the Assign Manufacturer’s Serial Number dialog box.

To edit the manufacturer’s serial number, do the following:

  • From the Serial # browser or Serial Number for all Items browser, click the manufacturer’s serial number column for the required item.
  • Enter the new serial number.
  • Click OK.
