Overview of the Purchase Process in Everest

The purchase process in Everest involves the following purchase documents:

  • Purchase Quotes
  • Purchase Orders
  • Purchase Receipts
  • Purchase Invoices

The Purchase Process Flowchart presents an overview of the purchase process in Everest. It depicts each step in the process, the documents created and the system journals in which accounting entries are generated.
