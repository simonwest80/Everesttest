Back End Processes - Convert a Purchase Quote to a Purchase Order

When a purchase quote is converted to a purchase order, the following back end processes ** occur:

  • The purchase quote is converted to an ‘open’ purchase order.
  • The sequence number of the purchase order is generated automatically by the system.

Document Number

  • The total quantity On Order for each item increases by quantity of the item ordered.

Item Stock Status
