# Purge a Purchase Quote


The **Purge** utility allows you  to permanently delete purchase quotes. You cannot cancel a purchase quote,  you can however, purge it. There are two ways to purge purchase quotes:

1. From the **Purchase Quotes** browser (path: **Options** > **Purge**) - Highlight the relevant quote and select **Options**  > **Purge**.
- From the **Utility** menu (path: **Utility** > **Purge** > **Purchase Quotes**).



![]({{site.pp_baseurl}}/img/steps.gif)To purge a purchase quote from the purchase  quotes browser, do the following:

- From the main  menu bar, select **Purchasing** >  **Purchase** **Quotes**.  You will see the **Purchase** **Quotes** browser.
- Highlight the  required purchase quote(s) and select **Options**  > **Purge**.



![]({{site.pp_baseurl}}/img/steps.gif)To purge multiple purchase quotes, do the  following:

- From the main  menu bar, select **Utility** > **Purge** > **Purchase Quotes**. You will see the **Purge 
 [Purchase Quotes]** browser.



{:.hint}
Select **Options** > **Expired Quotes** to  view quotes past their expiry date.

- Using the **Shift**/**Ctrl**  keys, select multiple quotes.
- Select **Options** > **Purge**.
