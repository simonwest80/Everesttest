# PO#, PO/PR# Columns in Purchase Receipts and Invoices


A purchase receipt displays the PO# column to indicate the related purchase  order. A purchase invoice will display the PO# and PR# columns to indicate  the related purchase orders and receipts. You can click on the number  within the column to open the relevant purchase document.
