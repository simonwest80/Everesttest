Other Item Details

Other information available in a purchase document are listed below:

  • Line Number
  • Job Code
  • Add-on Costs
  • Landed Cost
  • Landed Cost Amount
  • Customer PO No. and Date
  • SO #
  • Hide
  • Custom Fields
  • Amount
  • Vendor Part #
  • Bin
  • PO#, PO/PR#
  • EOQ
  • Third Party Part #
  • D[O Reference](/versata-everest-docs/the-purchase-process/misc/eoq_column_in_purchase_documents_pur.html)

Column displayed in purchase quotes:

  • Quantity

Columns displayed in purchase orders:

  • Committed
  • Ordered
  • Received
  • Invoiced

Columns displayed in purchase receipts:

  • Ordered
  • Ordered but not Received
  • Received
  • Invoiced
  • Invoiced but not Received

Columns displayed in purchase invoices:

  • Ordered
  • Ordered but not Invoiced
  • Received
  • Received to Date
  • Received but not Invoiced
  • Invoiced
  • Tax Amount and Recoverable Tax (In Purchase Invoices)
  • Amount (In Purchase Invoices)
