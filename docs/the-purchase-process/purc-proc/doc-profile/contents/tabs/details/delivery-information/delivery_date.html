Delivery Date

When you create a purchase document, by default the Delivery Date field displays the login date. If required, you can modify the Delivery Date.

Delivery Date on a Purchase Quote or Order

The Delivery Date field (in a purchase quote or order) allows you to specify the expected delivery date of the shipment. The delivery date on purchase orders helps you plan inventories.

Delivery Date on a Purchase Receipt

The delivery date on a purchase receipt identifies when the items were actually received, as there could be a time lag between the receipt of items and the creation of the purchase receipt.

Processed Documents

When you convert a purchase/debit quote to an order or an order to a purchase invoice/debit memo, the following will occur:

  • The converted document displays the login date, if the delivery date on the quote/order is blank.
  • The converted document displays the date on the quote/order, if a delivery date has been specified in the quote/order.
