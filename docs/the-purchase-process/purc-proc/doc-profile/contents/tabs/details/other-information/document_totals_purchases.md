# Document Totals


The following totals are summed, automatically updated and displayed  in all purchase documents. You cannot edit any of these fields.


| **Field** | **Description** |
| Taxable | This field displays the total value of items that  are subject to tax. This amount excludes discounts allowed, if any. These  items have a Tax Code associated in the Item Details Grid.<br/><br/><br/>![]({{site.pp_baseurl}}/img/lens.gif) [Tax  Code]({{site.sc_chm}}/options/sales-tax/tax-jurisdictions/jurisdiction-details/tax_code.html)<br/><br/><br/>![]({{site.pp_baseurl}}/img/note.gif)<font style="color: #0000ff;" color="#0000FF"> </font>Tax inclusive items are included  in this total if they have a purchase tax associated in the item details  grid but the tax is stripped out.<br/><br/><br/>Manual Purchase Invoices<br/><br/><br/>This field displays  the tax-exclusive total amount of all the “Taxable Purchase” entry types. |
| Tax | This field displays the total purchase tax on items  (the sum of all the values in the Tax Amount column of the item details  grid). Click the button ![]({{site.pp_baseurl}}/img/pur_tax_breakdown.gif) beside this field to view the  **Tax Breakdown** window that displays  a detailed break up of the tax charged.<br/><br/><br/>Manual Purchase Invoice<br/><br/><br/>This field displays the total purchase tax of all  recoverable and non-recoverable entries automatically generated by the  system. The Tax Breakdown is not available on manual documents. |
| Exempt | This field displays the total amount (after reducing  the discount, if any) for the line items that have a non-taxable tax code  or that do not have a tax code associated with them.<br/><br/><br/>Manual Purchase Invoice<br/><br/><br/>This field displays the total amount of all the  “Exempt Purchase” and “None” entry types. |
| Payment discount | This field displays the discount applied to a purchase  invoice when payment is made on a document.<br/><br/><br/>Payment discount is the discount allowed by a vendor  when payment is made before the due date. This discount is calculated  based on the rate and calculation method specified for the payment term  for the document.<br/><br/><br/>![]({{site.pp_baseurl}}/img/lens.gif) [Payment  Terms]({{site.sc_chm}}/options/payment-information/payment-terms/payment_terms.html)<br/><br/><br/>![]({{site.pp_baseurl}}/img/note.gif)<font style="font-family: Verdana;" face="Verdana"> &nbsp;</font>This  field is displayed only on a purchase invoice when it is saved.<br/><br/><br/>If the payment entry, having a payment discount,  is deleted, the amount of discount is reduced from the total payment discount  on the purchase invoice. |
| Total | This field displays the sum total of the Taxable,  Tax and Exempt amounts.<br/><br/><br/>Manual Purchase Invoice<br/><br/><br/>This total matches the amount specified in the Accounts  Payable entry. |
| Paid | This field displays the total amount paid on the  document. Click the ![]({{site.pp_baseurl}}/img/pur_document_alias.gif) button beside this field to view  the **Payment** **History**  of the document. This field does not display in a purchase receipt.<br/><br/><br/>![]({{site.pp_baseurl}}/img/lens.gif) [Payment  History]({{site.pp_baseurl}}/purc-proc/pos/po-processes/payments/payment-history/payment_history_pur.html) |
| Balance | This field displays the balance amount (total amount  - paid amount) to be paid on the document. This field does not display  in a purchase receipt. |
