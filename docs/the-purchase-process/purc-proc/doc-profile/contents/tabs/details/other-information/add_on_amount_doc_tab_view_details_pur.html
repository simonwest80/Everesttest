Add-on Amount

This display-only field indicates the total Add-on Amount (if available in the document) regardless of the FOB type. It updates every time you make a change that affects the add-on amount.. You may click on the amount in this field to open the Add-on Costs Distribution dialog box.

In the case of a purchase invoice with the type FOB Origin and where no quantity has been received in the purchase invoice, the field displays $0.00 because you cannot include add-on costs.
