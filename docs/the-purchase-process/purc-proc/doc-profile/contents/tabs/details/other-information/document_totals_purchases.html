Document Totals

The following totals are summed, automatically updated and displayed in all purchase documents. You cannot edit any of these fields.

Field Description
Taxable This field displays the total value of items that are subject to tax. This amount excludes discounts allowed, if any. These items have a Tax Code associated in the Item Details Grid.


Tax Code


Tax inclusive items are included in this total if they have a purchase tax associated in the item details grid but the tax is stripped out.


Manual Purchase Invoices


This field displays the tax-exclusive total amount of all the “Taxable Purchase” entry types.
Tax This field displays the total purchase tax on items (the sum of all the values in the Tax Amount column of the item details grid). Click the button beside this field to view the Tax Breakdown window that displays a detailed break up of the tax charged.


Manual Purchase Invoice


This field displays the total purchase tax of all recoverable and non-recoverable entries automatically generated by the system. The Tax Breakdown is not available on manual documents.
Exempt This field displays the total amount (after reducing the discount, if any) for the line items that have a non-taxable tax code or that do not have a tax code associated with them.


Manual Purchase Invoice


This field displays the total amount of all the “Exempt Purchase” and “None” entry types.
Payment discount This field displays the discount applied to a purchase invoice when payment is made on a document.


Payment discount is the discount allowed by a vendor when payment is made before the due date. This discount is calculated based on the rate and calculation method specified for the payment term for the document.


Payment Terms


 This field is displayed only on a purchase invoice when it is saved.


If the payment entry, having a payment discount, is deleted, the amount of discount is reduced from the total payment discount on the purchase invoice.
Total This field displays the sum total of the Taxable, Tax and Exempt amounts.


Manual Purchase Invoice


This total matches the amount specified in the Accounts Payable entry.
Paid This field displays the total amount paid on the document. Click the button beside this field to view the Payment History of the document. This field does not display in a purchase receipt.


Payment History
Balance This field displays the balance amount (total amount - paid amount) to be paid on the document. This field does not display in a purchase receipt.
