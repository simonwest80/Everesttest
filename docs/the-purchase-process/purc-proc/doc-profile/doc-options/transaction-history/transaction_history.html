Transaction History

Everest records the entire history of a transaction including the inception (purchase quote/debit quote), order creation, deposits placed, refunds received, items received, closing of the order, opening of the closed order, payments applied, conversion to purchase invoice/debit memo, and modifications to the document.

You can view the TransactionHistory browser from any of the following locations:

  • From the Purchase Documents browser by selecting Options > Transaction History.
  • From the Purchase Document profile by selecting Options > Transaction History.
  • From the Purchase Document profile by selecting the History tab.

The Transaction History browser displays the following:

  • Details of purchase quote/order/receipt/invoice creation such as date and time of creation, amount of document and the user who created the document.
  • Details of payments or deposits made and refunded on the document such as the payment type and reference number; details of payments applied to the document.  Credit card numbers are masked using the format XXXXXXXXXXXX when the **Enable Extended Mode** check box is cleared (path: **File > Setup > Preferences > Workstation Settings > Company Settings** tab).
  • Details of modifications made to the status of the purchase order, the vendor contact and shipping addresses, the document alias, pay terms and delivery method, location/sub-location and jurisdiction.
  • Displays whether the transaction status is Normal, Void, Reversed, or Reversal.

To open the documents displayed in the browser, select OpenDocument from the right-click menu. The Transaction History browser has standard options common to all browsers.

To maintain transaction history for documents, you must select the option Maintain Transaction Historyfor Documents (path: File > Setup > Preferences > FlowControl > Documents > Miscellaneous > Others > Others 1 tab).

You can also ensure that transaction history attached to a quote, order, or receipt is copied to the new document when it is converted to order, receipt, or invoice (path: File > Setup > Preferences > Flow Control > Documents > Miscellaneous > Others > Others 1 tab).
