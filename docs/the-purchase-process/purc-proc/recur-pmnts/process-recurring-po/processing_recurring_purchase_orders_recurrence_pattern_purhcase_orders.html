Process Recurring Purchase Orders

Processing involves conversion of the purchase orders to purchase invoices.

Document Dates

  • The document date of the purchase order must be earlier or equal to login date at the time of converting the order.
  • If the invoice date must be the same as the order date, the document date of the order must belong to an active period or an open one (if there is no active period). If the invoice date is the login date at the time of converting, the login date must belong to the active period, or to an open period, if there is no active period.

 Accounting Periods

  • You can process multiple recurring purchase orders at the same time, provided these orders bear the login date or a date prior to the login date.

To view all orders that need to be converted to a invoice on a particular day, group the documents by document date.

To process recurring documents, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Purchasing > Purchase Orders. You will see the Purchase Orders browser.
  • From the Purchase Orders browser, select Options > Recurring documents.

Only recurring purchase orders that are not converted to a purchase invoice are displayed in the browser.

  • Select the document(s) to be processed.
  • From the Purchase Orders browser, select Options > Process recurringdocuments.

Everest converts the selected purchase orders to invoice and saves the purchase invoice automatically.
