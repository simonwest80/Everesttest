# Payments made on Recurring Purchase Orders


Payments can be made on recurring purchase orders:

- In advance for all the orders in the series using a credit card type of payment method.



![]({{site.pp_baseurl}}/img/lens.gif)[When Deposit is made in Advance]({{site.pp_baseurl}}/purc-proc/recur-pmnts/pmnts-on-recurring-po/when_deposit_is_made_in_advance_recurring_purchase_docs_content.html)

- As a deposit against individual orders.



![]({{site.pp_baseurl}}/img/lens.gif)  Payments made on Purchase Orders

- When the recurring purchase order is converted to a receipt.
