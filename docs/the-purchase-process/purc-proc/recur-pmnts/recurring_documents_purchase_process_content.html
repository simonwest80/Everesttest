Recurring Documents

Recurring documents are as documents that you create for sales and purchases on a recurring or repeated basis.

Characteristics of Recurring Documents

Recurring documents are characterized by the following:

  • A schedule for billing - Recurring documents have a regular billing schedule.

Rent is paid on the 5th of every month.

  • A time period of contract - Recurring documents are normally created for a fixed time period based on the contract for purchase or sale.

Lease of building for one year during which rent has to be paid.

  • Identical items, quantity and price - The items, quantity and price of the documents in a recurring series are the same.

If recurring documents are created for rent, then the item code for rent, the price and the amount will be identical for all the 12 documents created during the lease period.

  • Identical billing information - The billing information such as customer or vendor information, taxes, delivery method, payment terms etc. are the same for all documents in the series.
