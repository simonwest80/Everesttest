View Recurring Purchase Orders

Recurring purchase orders are displayed in the PurchaseOrders browser (path: Purchasing > Purchase Orders).

To view only the recurring purchase documents, from the Purchase Orders browser, select Options > Recurring Documents.

All recurring purchase orders that are not processed into purchase receipts are displayed.

Group the Purchase Orders browser by the Recurringdoc. ID column to view the purchase orders pertaining to each series.
