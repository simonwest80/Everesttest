Accounting Entries - Edit a Purchase Receipt

The accounting processes that occur when you edit a purchase receipt depend on the purchase receipt’s FOB Type. In addition, the process differs for different scenarios.

  • When the system debits or credits the Accrued Purchases account with the balance of the Accrued Purchase account for a multicurrency transaction, if the variance in base or multicurrency is zero the system debits/credits the variance to the gain or loss accounts defined in the accounting defaults.
  • Changes to the item cost occur when you edit the basic item cost, the discount applied, the taxes, or any add-on costs.

Inventoried Items

  • Entry for Increase in Item Price
  • Entry for Decrease in Item Price
  • Entry for Increase in Item Price, Quantity Not in Stock
  • Entry for Decrease in Item Price, Quantity Not in Stock

Non-inventoried Items

  • Entry for Receiving Non-inventoried Line Items
  • Entry for Increasing Amount (positive and negative)
  • Entry for Decreasing Amount (positive and negative)
