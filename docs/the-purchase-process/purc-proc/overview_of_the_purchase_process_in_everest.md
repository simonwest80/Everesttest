# Overview of the Purchase Process in Everest


The purchase process in **Everest**  involves the following purchase documents:

- Purchase Quotes
- Purchase Orders
- Purchase Receipts
- Purchase Invoices



The [Purchase Process  Flowchart]({{site.pp_baseurl}}/purc-proc/flowchart_of_the_purchase_process.html) <font style="color: #000000;" color="#000000">presents an overview of the purchase 
 process in <span class="hcp1">Everest</span>. It depicts 
 each step in the process, the documents created and the system journals 
 in which accounting entries are generated.</font>
