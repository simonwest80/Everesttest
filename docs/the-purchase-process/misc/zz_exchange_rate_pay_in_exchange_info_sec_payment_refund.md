---
title: Exchange Rate
---


The **Exchange** **Rate**  field displays the current rate of exchange defined in the **Exchange 
 Rate** profile (path: **File**  > **Setup** > **Accounting**  > **Multic****urrency** > **Exchange 
 Rates**), between payment currency and the base currency.


You can change this rate if required. Correspondingly the **Amount**  field of the Payment currency section also changes.
