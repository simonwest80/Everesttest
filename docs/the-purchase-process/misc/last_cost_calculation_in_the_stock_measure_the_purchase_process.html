Last Cost Calculation in the Stock Measure

After a purchase, the Last Cost on the Item profile Purchases tab is updated in the purchase measure. After that, the Last Cost on the Item profile Inventory tab is updated in the stock measure using the conversion factor for the measures.

Stock Measure: PCS

Purchase Measures: Box and Carton

1 Box = 20 PCS

1 Carton = 50 PCS

After you purchase 10 boxes @$30 per box, the Last Cost in the Purchases tab updates to $30 for the Box purchase measure. The Last Cost in the Inventory tab updates to $1.50 ($30/20).

You decide to also purchase 10 Cartons @$60 per Carton. The Last Cost in the Purchases tab updates to $60 for the Carton purchase measure. The Last Cost in the Inventory tab updates to $1.20 ($60/50).
