---
title: Sales by Customer
---


Check this box, if you want to view the sales details along with details  of the customers to whom sales were made. Selecting the Sales by customer  option gives the total sales of the selected items (during the selected  period) to each customer.


![]({{site.pp_baseurl}}/img/note.gif) If  you select this option, you can view only the analysis of sales; the **purchase** analysis option will be disabled.
