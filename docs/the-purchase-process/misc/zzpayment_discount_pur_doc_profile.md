---
title: Payment Discount
---


This field displays the discount applied to a purchase  invioce when payment is made on a document.


Payment discount is the discount allowed by a vendor  when payment is made before the due date. This discount is calculated  based on the rate and calculation method specified for the payment term  for the document.


![]({{site.pp_baseurl}}/img/note.gif)<font style="font-family: Verdana;" face="Verdana"> &nbsp;</font>This  field is displayed only on a purchase invoice when it is saved.
