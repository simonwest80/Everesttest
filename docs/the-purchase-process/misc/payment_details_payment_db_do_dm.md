# Payment Details


****Document 
 Amount****
: This field displays the total value of the document.  You cannot edit this field.


**Already Paid**
: This field displays the amount that is already refunded  by the vendor. It includes both posted and unposted amounts. You cannot  edit this field.
: Click the **Already 
 Paid** label to view the [Payment  History]({{site.pp_baseurl}}/purc-proc/pos/po-processes/payments/payment-history/payment_history_pur.html) browser.


**Amount**
: Enter the amount you are paying your vendor. You  cannot make a payment greater than what you received as refund from your  vendor.
: If you make a payment to a foreign currency vendor,  you cannot enter an amount in this field. In this case, specify the amount  of payment in the [**Prior Payments** tab]({{site.pp_baseurl}}/return-proc/dos/do-processes/collection-payment/the_payment_dialog_box_debit_order.html).


**Balance**
: This field displays the maximum amount that can  be paid on the document. You cannot edit this field.
