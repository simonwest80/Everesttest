Payment Details

**Document
Amount**
This field displays the total value of the document. You cannot edit this field.
Already Paid
This field displays the amount that is already refunded by the vendor. It includes both posted and unposted amounts. You cannot edit this field.
Click the Already Paid label to view the Payment History browser.
Amount
Enter the amount you are paying your vendor. You cannot make a payment greater than what you received as refund from your vendor.
If you make a payment to a foreign currency vendor, you cannot enter an amount in this field. In this case, specify the amount of payment in the Prior Payments tab.
Balance
This field displays the maximum amount that can be paid on the document. You cannot edit this field.
