Quick Aging

The Quick Aging option (path: Purchase Document profile > Options > Vendor > QuickAging) brings up the VendorAging Information browser for purchase and purchase return documents that have outstanding payments or deposits.

The Vendor AgingInformation browser groups all outstanding documents by their document type.

Outstanding purchase receipts and its aliases are grouped under purchase receipts.

Deposits outstanding on purchase orders or amounts outstanding on debit memos are shown as negative amounts.

Aging Analysis

An aging analysis is displayed for the amounts outstanding based on the total days outstanding which is calculated from the document’s due date.

Available Credit

Available Credit Limit = Credit Limit - Net Outstanding

Posted and Unposted amounts are considered when calculating available credit.

Foreign Currency Vendors

In case of foreign currency vendors, all amounts are displayed in the vendor’s currency even if the deposit on a purchase order is made in a different currency.
