Modify Add-on Costs Distribution

  • You can modify the add-on costs distributed on a purchase/debit order at any time till it is received/invoiced.

It is not possible to edit the add-on cost for a purchase order, once if it has been received/invoiced partially or fully.

  • If some ordered items have been received/returned, changing the Add-on costs distribution in the original document will not change the add-on costs on the items already received/returned.
  • You cannot modify the add-on costs distributed on a saved debit memo
  • You can modify the add-on costs distributed on a saved purchase invoice. When you distribute the costs on the Invoiced quantity, this creates entries in the Accounts Payable and Purchase Price Variance accounts. When you distribute costs on the Received on Invoice quantity, this creates entries in the Inventory Control and Purchase Price Variance accounts.

When you convert a purchase order to a purchase invoice, if the invoiced/received on invoice quantity is less than the ordered quantity and you have add-on costs distributed in the purchase order, the system asks if you want to redistribute the add-on costs to reflect the cost for the invoiced/received on invoice quantity. If you select Yes, the add-on cost distribution profile displays so you can edit the costs. If you select No, the add-on cost does not change but the invoiced/received on invoice amount includes the costs.

When you convert a purchase receipt to a purchase invoice, if the invoiced quantity is less than the received quantity and you have add-on costs distributed in the purchase receipt, the system asks if you want to redistribute the add-on costs to reflect the cost for the invoiced quantity. If you select Yes, the add-on cost distribution profile displays so you can edit the costs. If you select No, the add-on cost does not change but the invoiced amount includes the costs.
