Back End Processes - Commit Items to a Debit Order

When quantities of an item are committed to a debit order, the stock status of the item displays the following changes:

  • Stock Status for the item - There is an increase in the quantity of On DebitCommitted for the item.
  • Stock Status in the assigned stock area - There is an increase in the quantity of item On Debit in the stock area to which the item is assigned.
  • Net Free Stock - The Net Free Stock for the item decreases.
