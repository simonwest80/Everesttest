Retrieve a Range of Serial Numbers

You can attach serialized inventory only from ‘stock’ type of stock areas to a debit order/memo.

To retrieve a range of serial numbers into a debit order/memo, do the following:

  • From the Debit Order/Debit Memo profile, select Options > Attach/RetrieveSerial # > Serial # Range. You will see the Retrieve Serial Numbers dialog box.
  • Specify the starting and ending serial numbers to be attached to the order/memo in the Serial # From and Serial # To fields and click Apply.
  • The serial numbers are attached to the line item.

You can scan the serial numbers into the system using a bar code scanner.

Select the button to view details of the serial numbers attached to the item.

History

When you attach a serial number that does not exist on the document, Everest asks you to confirm that you want to attach the item. Click Yes or No as per your requirement.

The prompt will be displayed only if the Prompt when trying to retrieve and serialize an item that is not present on a document option (path: File > Setup > Preferences > Flow Control > Documents > **Others

  • Others 2** tab) is selected.
