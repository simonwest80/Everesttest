Attach Items to the Debit Order

Inventoried items can be committed to a debit order when:

  1. You identify the items to be returned and move them to a separate area.
    • You send items out for repair or exchange but continue to retain ownership.

You cannot commit items on orders belonging to discontinued vendors.

An entry can be made in the Committed column only for inventoried and serialized items. In the case of a non-inventoried line item this column displays a cross mark.

Entering the committed quantities for items is classified under:

  • Serialized Items
  • Auto-serialized Items
  • Non-serialized Items
