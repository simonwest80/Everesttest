Collections on a Debit Order

Your vendor can make you refunds through cash, check, or credit card for items returned. Everest allows you to make collections on a debit order.

A credit card refund is processed online only if the Use Payment Processor option is selected (path: File > Setup > Accounting > Payment Methods > Options > Edit > In New Window > Credit Card Processing tab).

To accept collections on a debit order, do the following:

  • From the Debit Order profile, select Options > Payment > Collect. You will see the Collection dialog box.
  • Select the appropriate payment method in the Payment Method field.
  • Enter the amount to be collected in the Amount field and click OK.
