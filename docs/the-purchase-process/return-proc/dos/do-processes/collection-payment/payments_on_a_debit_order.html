Payments on a Debit Order

You may initially accept a refund from your vendor for items that you return. Later on, you may decide to take another item in exchange for the returned item. In this case, you would make either a full or partial payment on the debit order to the vendor.

You can pay your vendor through cash, check, or credit card. A credit card payment is processed online only when you select the Use Payment Processor option (path: File > Setup > Accounting > Payment Methods > Options > Edit > In New Window > Credit Card Processing tab).

To make payments on a debit order, do the following:

  • From the Debit Order profile, select Options > Payment > Make Payment. You will see the Payment dialog box.
  • Select the appropriate payment method in the Payment Method field.
  • Enter the amount to be paid in the Amount field and click OK.
