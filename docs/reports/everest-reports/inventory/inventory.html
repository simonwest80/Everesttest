Inventory Reports

Inventory reports display inventory assigned to brands. They are grouped under categories, stock bins, items and items that are tagged to tracking groups or selections.

You can generate the following Inventory reports:

  • Detail by Brand
  • Detail By Category - Entry Date (Serialized)
  • Detail By Category - Stock Bin
  • Detail By Item - Entry Date (Serialized)
  • Inventory Summary
  • Item Tracking (Group)
  • Item Tracking (Selection)
  • Summary by Category - Single Stock Bin
  • Summary by Category - Multiple Stock Bin
  • Summary by Item Code - Stock Bin
  • Inventory Control Sheet Report
  • Price Sheets By Category
  • Stock Discrepancy Report
