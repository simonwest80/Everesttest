Visitors Log

The Visitors Log report gives a detailed overview of people who have visited your organization for different purposes (official or personal), the organization from where they came, their purpose of visit to your company, their names and in/out times and the names of the persons whom they visited. This report can be of great help to the administration department and also for security personnel to track the entry and exit of visitors in case some issues crop up in the smooth day to day running of your business. Visitor logs are of great use to the management for identifying the date when some important discussion might have taken place with a particular visitor and if someone else represented the visitor in subsequent discussions.

To generate the Visitors Log report, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Reports > Time Clock > Visitors Log. You will see the Visitors Log dialog box.

  • Specify the report parameters and click:

  • Preview, to preview the report in the Report Preview dialog box
  • Print, to print the report
  • Export, to save the report in text, Excel, HTML or Crystal Report file formats
