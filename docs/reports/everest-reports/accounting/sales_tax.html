Sales Tax

The Sales Tax report gives information on the total sales tax to report to different authorities within a state or collected during a specified period for both system and manual sales documents appropriate for the search parameters. The report includes details of the taxable and exempted sales. The total sales tax displays against each document, and the total collections for each jurisdiction and the grand total also display. All reports are grouped by tax code, location, and jurisdiction.

Sales invoices appear in the Sales section of the report while credit memos appear in the Returns section. In the case of a manual sales journal, the amounts appear in the Sales section if the Accounts Receivable account is debited or in the Returns section if the Accounts Receivable account is credited.

Detailed Summary of Tax Calculation and Reporting in Everest

To generate the Sales Tax report, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Reports > Accounting > Sales Tax. You will see the Sales Tax dialog box.

  • Specify the report parameters and click:

  • Preview, to preview the report in the Report Preview dialog box
  • Print, to print the report
  • Export, to save the report in text, Excel, HTML or Crystal Report file formats
