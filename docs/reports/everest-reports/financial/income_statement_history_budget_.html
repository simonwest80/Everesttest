Income Statement (History/Budget)

The Income Statement (History/Budget) report, displays the income and expenses for the specified period. The actual amounts in the specified period are compared either with the amounts in the corresponding historical period or with the budget for the specified period.

  • The budget report helps to compare the variance between budgets and actual amounts in the selected period. It helps to analyze how closely the company is adhering to the budget set up.
  • The history period helps to compare the performance of your company in a prior period to the selected period.
  • The report can also be obtained for income and expenses allocated to locations/sub-locations. However, budgets are not defined by locations/sub-locations. The report does not display a breakup of the budget amount for each location/sub-location, but the entire budget amount, for all locations/sub-locations.
  • The income statement is prepared with the posted balances by default and is generated for a period that starts on the first date of the starting accounting period and ends on the last date of the ending accounting period. You can also include provisionally posted or unposted amounts, and hide accounts with zero balance summary or details of each location’s income and expenses, Other Income (Expenses) and Provisional Tax by selecting/not selecting the required parameters.

To generate the income statement (history - budget) report, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Reports > Financial > Income Statement (History - Budget). You will see the Income Statement (History/Budget - Comp) dialog box.

  • Specify the report parameters and click:

  • Preview, to preview the report in the Report Preview dialog box
  • Print, to print the report
  • Export, to save the report in text, Excel, HTML or Crystal Report file formats
