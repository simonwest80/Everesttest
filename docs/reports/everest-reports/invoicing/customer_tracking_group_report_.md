# Customer Tracking (Group)


You can track your customers by the tracking group they are assigned  to, at the time of creation. The **Customer 
 Tracking (Group)** report provides information on how customers are  categorized on the basis of tracking groups.


![]({{site.rpt_baseurl}}/img/lens.gif) [Customers]({{site.ct_chm}}/customer-tracking/customer_tracking.html)


A customer might have heard of your company through a television commercial.  Referring to your customer data, you can gauge the effectiveness of your  television advertising. You can also analyze how much revenue has been  generated via this sales promotion media.


![]({{site.rpt_baseurl}}/img/example.gif) ‘Found  us via’, is  a tracking group and ‘television’ advertisement is a tracking selection.


{:.steps}
To  generate the Customer Tracking Group report, do the following:

- From the main  menu bar, select **Reports** >  **Invoicing** > **Customer 
 Tracking** (**Group**). You will  see the [**Customer 
 Tracking (Group)** dialog box]({{site.rpt_baseurl}}/misc/customer_tracking_group_dialog_box_rep.html).
- Specify the  report parameters and click:


- **Preview**,  to preview the report in the **Report Preview** dialog box
- **Print**,  to print the report
- **Export**,  to save the report in text, Excel, HTML or Crystal Report file formats
