Best Customer

The Best Customer report lists customers from whom you have generated the maximum revenue or profit during a particular time period. The report includes the customer code and name, the revenue generated and the cost incurred, thereby giving you the volume of business transactions with the customer. It also includes the profit, margin and the markup for the customer.

You can also generate this report for “Best Sales Representatives” to monitor the revenue or profit performance of the “Sales Representatives”.

An analysis of the reports could help you draw comparisons of customers which in turn, would leverage your receivables.

To generate the Best Customer report, do the following:

  • From the main menu bar, select Reports > Invoicing > Best Customer. You will see the Best Customer dialog box.

  • Specify the report parameters and click:

  • Preview, to preview the report in the Report Preview dialog box
  • Print, to print the report
  • Export, to save the report in text, Excel, HTML or Crystal Report file formats
