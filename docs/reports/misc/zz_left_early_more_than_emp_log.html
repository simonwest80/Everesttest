Enter the number of hours employees have left early before the shift ended to be included in the report.

An employee’s shift timings are from 9:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m., and the employee has left more than 2 hours early on some days. If you specify 2 in the Left Early By (Hours) field, every day that the employee left early by more than 2 hours for the specified date range appears in the report.

Decimal places entered in the Hours Worked More Than, Hours Worked Lesser Than, Left Early By (Hours), and Left Late By (Hours) fields are rounded to the nearest whole number in the report.
