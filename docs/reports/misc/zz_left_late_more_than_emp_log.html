Enter the number of hours that employees have worked extra, after their shift has ended, to be included in the report.

An employee’s regular shift timings are from 9:00 a.m. till 18:00 p.m., but the employee has clocked out after 20:00 p.m., on some days. If you specify 2 in the Left Late By (Hours) field, then each day that the employee worked extra by more than 2 hours after the shift ended at 18:00 p.m., will be displayed in the report.

Decimal places entered in the Hours Worked More Than, Hours Worked Lesser Than, Left Early By (Hours), and Left Late By (Hours) fields are rounded to the nearest whole number in the report.
