View the Cash Flow Activity of an Account

View the Cash Flow Activity of an account in the Chart of Accounts browser.

Chart of Accounts Browser

To view the Cash Flow Activity, do the following:

  • Open the Chart of Accounts browser. (Select Accounting > Chart of Accounts and click the Hide Tree View icon.)
  • Review the Cash Flow Activity column which indicates the type of activity for each account.
