Document Payment Information

Use the **Document Payment Information option to view the history of payments made on a particular document in the Payment History browser.**

All payments made against this document including amounts applied from, or to other documents are listed.

 If part of a deposit on a purchase order is applied to a purchase invoice, then the payment history for the purchase invoice displays the amount applied from the purchase order and the payment history for the purchase order displays the amount applied towards the purchase invoice.
