Account Number Assigned by Vendor

The Account Number assigned by Vendor may contain any type of character but may not exceed 20 characters. The number helps identify a particular account when you have more than one account with the vendor and also when reconciling the vendor’s statement of your account. This number, if available, appears on all check stubs you print for the vendor for this account that do not include “Date Printed” details.
